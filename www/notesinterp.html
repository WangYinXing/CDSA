Notes on Interpretation

Now that you have found your Destiny Numeral based on the Master Ghost Valley system you have a life reading in a slightly cryptic format. As you can see each life reading comprises of Numeral, Hexagram, Stars, Destiny, Luck Verses (Foundation, Life Path, Marriage, Children and Harvest) and Style.

The Numerals

Let’s begin with the Destiny Numeral. There are a hundred numerals. Each numeral is named according to the Chinese names of the heavenly stems for your year of birth and your hour of birth. For example, Numeral 8 Jia Xin is the eighth out of one hundred life readings. Jia refers to the heavenly stem of the year of birth and Xin refers to the heavenly stem of the hour of birth.

The Hexagrams

Each numeral is linked to a Hexagram from the I Ching (Yi Jing). There are 100 numerals in the Master Ghost Valley system and 64 hexagrams in the I Ching. This means some hexagrams appear under more than one numeral. The original text does not offer any interpretations of the hexagrams and we leave it up to you to see what you might discover from various other sources. You might find it interesting to study the relationship between the numerals, heavenly stems and hexagrams.

The Stars

The star names are translated from Chinese in the original Master Ghost Valley manuscript to English. The stars are not related to actual stars in the sky. How Master Ghost Valley came up with the star names remains a mystery.

Each person was born under the auspices of two stars: a year star and an hour star. Some stars serve as both year stars and hour stars. Most of the stars serve only as hour stars.

You can easily find out your year star and the brief reading according to the year star by pressing the year star button on the Four Pillars Calculator. Your year star is not connected to your Destiny Numeral, but your hour star is linked to your Numeral. We are now going to talk about how you can find your hour star

For each numeral there are three possible stars. You find your personal hour star out of the three possibilities by looking for your hour animal in the tables. Remember your hour pillar was calculated using the Four Pillars Calculator.

Let’s illustrate how to find your hour star using your hour pillar based on the example of Numeral 3.

Numeral 3 Jia Bing

The first star is the Heavenly Obstacle Star and this is the star for somebody born in either Rat hour or Tiger hour. In the table where it says Hour Pillar, if you read across it gives Bing Zi – Fire Rat and Bing Yin – Fire Tiger. Somebody with Destiny Numeral 3 born in Rat hour would take the reading from the metaphor and explanation from the column under Bing Zi – Fire Rat, whereas somebody born in Tiger hour would take the reading from under Bing Yin – Fire Tiger. The same star covers two different hour pillars and gives a different reading for each hour pillar.

Heavenly Obstacle Star

Hour Pillar Bing Zi – Fire Rat Bing Yin – Fire Tiger
Metaphor Fish swimming out of the marsh. Bee flying into blossoming bushes.
Explanation If you are over-ambitious, dreams are not easily fulfilled. You are very sharp and smart but be careful not to go down the wrong track.

The next table is for the Heavenly Illusion Star and covers the situation for those born during the Dragon hour or Horse hour.

Heavenly Illusion Star

Hour Pillar Bing Chen – Fire Dragon Bing Wu – Fire Horse
Metaphor Phoenix on the maple tree. Golden flower on the rock.
Explanation You can marry into a wealthy family, but do not expect too much from your in-laws. Famous for being famous, enjoy your celebrity status.

The final star under Numeral 3 is the Heavenly Arrogance Star, which covers the hours of the Monkey and Dog.

Heavenly Arrogance Star

Hour Pillar Bing Shen– Fire Monkey Bing Xu – Fire Dog
Metaphor Floating in shallow waters. Leading a procession in glamorous robes.
Explanation You work hard, yet results are limited. You gain wealth and return to your hometown to enjoy your retirement.

Now that we have explained how to find the Destiny Star under a numeral, let’s take a look at what is in the rest of the life reading.

Destiny

This is a translation of the prose from Master Ghost Valley about someone’s fate and fortune according to the portents of the numeral.

Luck Verses (Foundation, Life Path, Marriage, Children and Harvest)

In Master Ghost Valley’s original manuscript the verses are four lines of rhyming Chinese poetry. In our translation, we have made each verse into an English poem. Where the meaning is obvious from the verses, we have translated it as such. However, much of the original wording was written in a cryptic manner and in some instances we have maintained the cryptic nature in the translation in order to allow room for imagination and discussion.

Style

This section adds something extra to finish off the reading. We have captured the many cryptic nuances in our translation, which might mean that you may need to ponder on some of the possible meanings.

To illustrate how to interpret the readings, we have a used the celebrity example of Elvis Presley. We did some basic research on Elvis’s life on the internet. There is a lot of information about Elvis on-line and you may find there are more interpretations of the reading for Numeral 3 as applied to Elvis that we have not discovered.

It would also be helpful for you to read the Notes on Symbolism and Historical References in order to help you interpret the life readings.

From the Four Pillars Calculator we obtain Numeral 3 for Elvis. Let’s go through the reading for Numeral 3 and see how that relates to Elvis’s life.

Stars

Elvis’s hour pillar was Bing Yin, which means he was born under the Heavenly Obstacle Star. The metaphor for this star is ‘Bee flying into blossoming bushes’ and the explanation is ‘You are very sharp and smart, but be careful not to go down the wrong track’.

It would be apt to describe Elvis as sharp and smart and we could say that in his later days his dependence on prescription medications was going down the wrong track.

Destiny

In technical matters you are a star. Cultural or academic success can be achieved. When you want to be alone, you cannot. When you want to socialize and be with people you cannot. In difficult times what appears to be a disaster is not a disaster. In times of achievement, good fortune is not really good fortune. A noble and respectable person you are the target of jealousy by lesser mortals. Setting a good example you make a good role model. When you are relaxed you can still find things to make you feel uncomfortable. When you are happy you can still find reasons to be sad. You have siblings and friends, yet it is as if they are not there. Relatives are not close to you. Everything is accomplished by yourself, on your own, that is how it is in your life.

Elvis was definitely a star and has made a huge influence on popular culture. As a famous musician, it would have probably been difficult for Elvis to always be alone or be with others as and when he chose. Apparently, Elvis did not like several of the songs that made him famous, so perhaps in his mind, he did not view all his hits as his good fortune. Throughout his life Elvis made many charitable donations. In 1970, he was named as one of the Ten Outstanding Young Men of the Nation, so we can definitely say he set a good example and made a good role model. Elvis had a twin brother who was stillborn at birth, so in effect, Elvis was an only child. Since Elvis is one of the best-selling solo musical artists, it is fair to say that he made his accomplishments on his own.

Luck Verses

Foundation:
From the terrace you watch the bright moon.
On the high mountain alone you climb.
Unfulfilled dreams are dropped along the way.
You seek peace in the battlefield of your mind.

To watch the bright moon means to succeed. Elvis had great achievement and won many awards including three Grammy awards and the Grammy Lifetime Achievement Award. As his success came from being a solo musical artist, we can say that he climbed the high mountain alone. Elvis starred in numerous movies, but he wanted to be a serious actor, and this was his unfulfilled dream. 1n 1964, Larry Geller became Elvis’s hairdresser. It is reported that Elvis poured his heart out to Larry and started spiritual studies with him, to seek peace in the battlefield of his mind.

Life Path:
Looking back you see the whole country,
Thousands of miles away from home.
In old age you reflect on shame and glory,
Let it be, do not lament on your life story.

Elvis toured USA extensively and was often thousands of miles away from his home, Graceland in Memphis, Tennessee. There was much glory in his hugely successful career, yet he suffered problems with his health and battled with his weight in his later years.

Marriage:
Will a wedding banquet full of luxury,
Lead to satisfaction or misery?
Happy or sad, it is hard to foresee.
To harvest fruit, re-plant the apricot tree.

Elvis and Priscilla married on 1st May 1967 at the Aladdin Hotel in Las Vegas. The couple divorced on 9th October 1973. The Aladdin Hotel in Las Vegas was a luxurious venue and there were happy times for the couple before divorcing.

Children:
Beautiful flowers adorn the back garden, yet no fruit,
The lovely greenery blossoming and blooming.
Chances are Goat and Tiger years bring offspring.
Handsome fruit on the mulberry tree reflects the glory of sunset.

Elvis and Priscilla had one child together, Lisa Marie, who was born on 1 February 1968. According to the Chinese solar calendar, Lisa Marie was born in the year of the Goat. The poem about children is uncannily accurate for Elvis.

Harvest:
In Dragon year opportunities come with dragon on water.
Retirement happens in Tiger year, even without plans.
With cool breeze and bright moon for quiet times,
With pets and plants you enjoy the rural life.

1952, 1964 and 1976 were dragon years. In researching Elvis’s biography, nothing significant was found for 1952 when Elvis was 17 years old. In 1964, Elvis started his spiritual studies after meeting Larry Geller on 30th April. In 1976, Elvis was touring in concert for most of the year. Further research into Elvis’s life might reveal if there is any significance to ‘dragon on water’. Elvis did not retire as such. However, it is interesting to note that after doing some charity concerts in 1961, he did not give a public performance for seven years. In 1962, a Tiger year, he took a retirement from performing live in public without planning it.

Style: A Horse Going through Mountain and River
High winds blow over a thousand miles.
Causing ripples of stormy waters.
With great ambition for many great things,
You are like a goose soaring on powerful wings.
Just as a pine tree stands firmly upright in the snow,
When you seek wealth both success and failure you will know.
Planting beautiful flowers in spring,
Soon you will enjoy being melancholy.
If you want to know what future success you will get,
Imagine you are in fairyland picking a peach at sunset.

Was Elvis’s life like a horse going through mountain and river? He certainly, toured the USA a lot and travelled many thousands of miles. It is said that his life on the road contributed to the breakdown of his marriage to Priscilla. This could be the interpretation of the ripples of stormy waters. Like a soaring goose, Elvis certainly reached great heights in his career and set records for audiences and record sales. Although Elvis’s movies were hugely successful at the box office, they were not well received by film critics – both success and failure you will know. Elvis enjoyed success in his youth, with number one hits by the time he was twenty-one. Spring is the time of youth and planting beautiful flowers indicates success. Despite all his accomplishments, Elvis was not always happy, so he would have felt melancholy at times. To be in fairyland picking a peach means great success.
