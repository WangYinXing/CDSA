What is Chinese Destiny Star Astrology? 

Year Animal Astrology
 At Chinese New Year you hear about the animal sign that is going to rule the year ahead. Babies born in that year are said to be born under that animal sign. Personality and luck are influenced by the energies of the year animal sign. There are a total of twelve different animal signs. Year Animal Astrology is a simple form of Chinese Astrology based on a cycle of twelve years. The same cycle of twelve animals also rule months, days and double hours (the twenty four hours in the day are divided into units of two hours). The twelve animals are pseudonyms for the twelve earthly branches, and time is measured with the twelve earthly branches together with the ten heavenly stems.

Four Pillars of Destiny (Bazi)
 There is far more to Chinese Astrology than your year animal sign. The heavenly stems and earthly branches form a cycle of sixty pillars. A pillar is a unit of time written vertically, with the name of the heavenly stem above the name of the earthly branch. When a baby is born you can write out the date and time of birth as four pillars: a year pillar, month pillar, day pillar and hour pillar. These four pillars become part of a chart for the method of Chinese Astrology known as the Four Pillars of Destiny. The Four Pillars gives you a far more personalized picture of your innate characteristics and luck than your animal sign. An experienced astrologer can analyze and interpret the Four Pillars chart to give a life reading. Learning the art of life readings using the Four Pillars is complex, and it can take years of practice to become highly competent.

Chinese Destiny Stars Astrology
 There is the simple Year Animal Astrology and there is the rather more sophisticated Four Pillars of Destiny. The question remains, what is Chinese Destiny Star Astrology?

Chinese Destiny Star Astrology is another name for the Master Ghost Valley system of life reading. We have translated the ancient Chinese poetry classic known as "Ghost Valley Art of Life Reading" (鬼谷子算命術 Gui Gu Zi Suan Ming Shu) into English and packaged it in this app together with our free Four Pillars Calculator.

How to Use the Four Pillars Calculator
 We plot a Four Pillars chart using solar time. To plot a chart you need to know:

a) a person’s date, time and place of birth;
b) the timezone and longitude of the birth place;
 c) if daylight saving time was in use at time of birth.

The Four Pillars Calculator has a number of buttons:

Select from map – Finds the timezone and longitude of anywhere in the world. You can pan and zoom on the map to find your birth place then tap to mark the location.
Set from current position – sets the timezone and longitude for the location where you are at the moment. Handy if you happen to be in your town of birth when using the app.
Save - saves a record of the birth details you have entered.
Open - loads birth details you have already saved into the calculator.
Delete - deletes  a record of saved birth details
Compute – works out your Four Pillars chart and accompanying luck pillars, plus your Destiny Numeral and Stars.

You can use the Four Pillars Calculator online or offline. You need to be online to get the timezone and longitude from the map, or to set it from your current position. If you use the app offline you can select the timezone and enter the longitude.  Longitude is expressed as a number of degrees east or a number of degrees west. When you input the longitude figure into the calculator, longitudes which are west are entered with a minus sign in front of the number of degrees. For example New York, NY, USA has a longitude of 74W, therefore you would enter -74 into the box for longitude. If the birth place is at a longitude which is east, you just enter the number of degrees.
