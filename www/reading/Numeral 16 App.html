Numeral 16 Yi Ji 

Hexagram: Wind on Earth

Heavenly Pillar Star

Hour Pillar Ji Chou – Earth Ox Ji Mao – Wood Rabbit 
Metaphor Riding a boat in the waves. Lighting up a candle in the wind.
Explanation Beware of hazards in everything. When everything looks fine, be careful there could be something wrong.

Heavenly Rot Star

Hour Pillar Ji Si – Earth Snake Ji Wei – Earth Goat
Metaphor Crossing the sea in a boat. Moon shining over chilly pond.
Explanation When you are in trouble, help arrives. When you are in despair, someone comes to help.

Heavenly Soil Star

Hour Pillar Ji You – Earth Rooster Ji Hai – Earth Pig
Metaphor Dusty mirror shining brilliantly. Sick dragon walking in the rain.
Explanation When your troubles are over, the prosperity thereafter is immense. Misfortune turns into fortune and life runs smoothly.


Destiny:
This life is like excavating the mountain to build a road. It is like digging a well to draw the spring water. You try your best and make a good effort. You know what you need to do and you can manage the tasks in hand.  What is light and what is heavy, you already know, and you can distinguish the virtuous and capable from the foolish. Although you are very clever and possess great wisdom, your luck is mixed.  The centipede can run fast but it is unlike the dragonfly, it is not as capable as the dragonfly.  Life is like a boat that is not anchored.  Just as the new moon does not have enough light, setbacks are due to the timing not being right, no need to blame yourself.

 

Luck Verses

Foundation: 
From the top of the high pavilion you look afar to see the nine skies.
Winds blow over the great river making lots of angry waves.
Over thousands of miles, the mythical big bird Peng flies.
In the dragon and phoenix mansion, you are the hero who dines.

Life Path:
Many ups and downs, times of glory and times of shame.
Set sights to be the prime minister, such high ambitions,
With ability to pass exams, gain qualifications.
Also, can overcome own limitations.

Marriage:
Pair of mandarin ducks playing in the pond,
When you are in love, be wary of the storm.
Clouds blown away by wind reveal the bright moon.
Lift the bamboo screen, to see pomegranate,
Blossoming beautifully, before your eyes.

Children:
Two fruits on the branch, only one ripens.
Good luck for your children, from your acts of charity.
One of your sons will gain a high position.
A successful son brings the family much glory.

Harvest:
In Ox and Horse years, everything is so near, yet so far.
The destination is so far away, from wherever you are.
Monkey and crane dancing together, having fun,
Whilst you play the flute, the music floats to heaven.

 

Style: Begonia Outside the Fence
High on top of the pavilion look at the high clouds.
Winds are blowing over the water to make waves.
If you ask about the fame and fortune you are seeking, there is a lot of gossip and a lot of controversy in your life.
The night rain hits the flowers and the petals will be blown away.
The wild goose flies into the bitter cold wind.
After the darkest day of the winter solstice, what is unsteady becomes stable.
You do things casually and it soon becomes natural.
Your character is straightforward and you can face up to heaven.
Wild goose leaves birthplace and flies away.
When the string breaks on the lute, you can replace it with another.
Dealings with relatives are not that warm, yet you get on well with friends.
Fame and fortune will come again and again.
