Numeral 76    Xin Ji

Hexagram: Heaven on Earth

Heavenly Traditional Star

Hour Pillar Ji Chou – Earth Ox Ji Mao – Wood Rabbit
Metaphor Leaves falling in the autumn wind. Fishing in cold pond.
Explanation Career reaches peak by middle age. Even though you work hard, you need to manage your expectations when it comes to doing what is unfamiliar.

Heavenly Chariot Star

Hour Pillar Ji Si – Earth Snake Ji Wei – Earth Goat
Metaphor Watching the moon which is overshadowed by clouds. Fish in well coming out from the swamp.
Explanation Facing a troublesome time of temporary difficulties. Hope is in sight, right ahead. After being trapped for a long time, there is hope for becoming prosperous.

Heavenly Penalty Star

Hour Pillar Ji You – Earth Rooster Ji Hai – Earth Pig
Metaphor Yellow flowers preparing for the frost. Watching the moon on the terrace.
Explanation Virtue and discipline, strong and clean. Even when encountering misfortune, there is no harm. You get promoted and achieve good results.

 

Destiny:
This is the life of an admiral, general or minister. In the old days it would be someone who was an assistant to the emperor. Nowadays you can be somebody who helps to run the country. You are talented in both literary arts and military or fighting techniques.  Your nature is unselfish, with a character which is hard and soft at the same time. Capabilities are very good for rescuing and liberating people. Sometimes you meet fortune, yet sometimes you meet misfortune. Wealth is adequate throughout your life. You can expect to live a long life. Competing and winning are what you love to do. You can distinguish what is right from what is wrong.  If you choose a spouse who has the luck to escape from danger and withstand pain, it will bring you extra longevity too. 

Luck Verses

Foundation: 
Gold and jade are not real treasures to be counted on.
Being yourself with a peaceful heart is the best medicine.
In Dog and Dragon years, there will be prosperity,
Because meeting the right person at right time will be lucky.

Life Path:
Fortune runs with a pair of Ox tails, as it happens.
At Rat’s head and Tiger’s tail, contentment,
Success, not as great as the previous generation,
Anyhow, still receive an award in the imperial palace,
It’s an achievement still worthy of congratulation.

Marriage:
Fan made of goose feathers looks like a dragon’s tail.
Alone in grand hall, stands a lady with jade beauty.
Into great wealth and social status, you can marry.
Keep a peaceful mind, keep it flat and even, like a level trail.
Matchmaker under the moon will do what’s right, without fail.

Children:
Famous tree blossoms, many fruits it bears,
Green and yellow fruits are coming in pairs.
In late years, a lot of grandchildren,
Many babies are born in your grand mansion.

Harvest:
Encounter the powerful people in days of difficulty.
Like going into a dream, leave this world, eventually.
It’s not the water rooster that can be haunting.
Yet it still stirs up the peaceful life, stirring, stirring.


Style: One After Another Millstones Grinding Jade
Stars above are emitting light like arrows shooting from heaven.
The moon is shining on the heavenly book, which reveals the secrets of life, as it descends to earth from the sky.
In the Rooster hour, rooster crows three times, reminding you to go on an important trip, to approach the emperor in the north.
The bright full moon is coming out in the east.
If you want to polish your sword you need to find the right stone.
If you want to refine the jade, there is no need to fear the powerful grinding instruments.
Focus on what you want to target.
Then you shoot the arrow to aim at the phoenix.
