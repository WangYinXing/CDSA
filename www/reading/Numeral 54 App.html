Numeral 54    Ji Ding

Hexagram: Earth on Earth

Stars

Heavenly Blasting Star 

Hour Pillar Ding Chou – Fire Ox Ding Mao – Fire Rabbit
Metaphor Autumn flowers in the rain. Yellow flowers in the last quarter of the year.
Explanation Come into good years in middle age, yet be prepared for some unexpected storms. If you are well determined then you will live your old age happily.

Heavenly Barbarian Star 

Hour Pillar Ding Si – Fire Snake Ding Wei – Fire Goat
Metaphor Sampan going in the sea. Wild goose spreading its wings in autumn.
Explanation You are solitary and vulnerable, beware of changes of fortune. If you want to build a career, it will happen after forty.

Heavenly Blurry Star

Hour Pillar Ding You – Fire Rooster Ding Hai – Fire Pig
Metaphor Cicadas and birds are coming to the forest. Boating under the cold moon.
Explanation You can depend on others to look after you. Happy throughout your whole life.

Poetic Life Reading

Destiny:

You are very thoughtful and wise. With cleverness you are capable of making good plans, yet on the other hand you can be cunning and suspicious. Management skills and the ability to hold power feature strongly in your life. Fate says there are no sons for you, but you are destined to have a good spouse. The three penalties and seven killings affect your life. Therefore, be careful of injuries caused by accidents.  You need to go out to earn money in order to save and accumulate. Wealth and fame can be kept to be developed at home, all because you are charitable day and night. Everywhere you are carrying out acts of charity. When you are doing well, know when to stop, for then you will be able to avoid losses.  If you ask how your relatives and siblings are doing, they have their own lives.

Great ambitions to do big things, soar up in the sky.

Accumulate good deeds, and eventually

Marry someone from another country.

In Dog and Goat years after sad news, happy tidings told.

End of Rat year, start of Ox, events quite shocking.

Dream a dream once more, to enjoy the beauty of it all.

Fame and fortune should have a foundation.
Success or failure is all because of your destiny.

Commentary

Destiny

You have executive skills and you can take power. Being charitable generates good karma for acquiring fame and fortune.

Luck Verses

Foundation

Although you are motivated to fulfil your dreams, it takes time to accomplish your goals. You can improve your luck by doing charitable acts. In time you will meet a business partner and build a successful enterprise.

Life Path

To “meet dangerous waves at night on your boat” means that you encounter challenges with your business, yet luck will be with you in a Goat year. Once your business is stable, it will turn out to be successful.

Marriage

“Swallow flying, day and night nightingale dancing” suggests that there is a happy relationship. “Strong gusts and heavy rain at window howling” means that external circumstances bring difficulties for your relationship. You need to be aware that there are unfortunate times (“dim moon”), as well as good times.

Children (‘Children’ can also mean subordinates or followers)

Not all your sons will be around to keep you company in your later years. There is a wonderful celebration (“Dragon and phoenix dancing alone”) in your household in a Dog year or a Goat year. For followers, you will have some loyal followers, but not all of them will stay with you.

Harvest

“Rooster is telling the time, when moon is just setting,” refers to new beginnings, because the rooster is announcing the dawn. “What is supposed to have withered, blossoms beautifully again,” suggests that there is a revival of something, which had appeared doubtful.

Style

“Snow begins to melt” alludes to a short-lived success. “Who is grafting on new branches?” suggests that new life can be brought to an established enterprise, like grafting on new branches to an old tree. You can find a happy couple relationship (“mandarin ducks pair up”). You go your own way on your travels (“wild goose flies, it misses the flock”). The third month of spring is at the end of the season, therefore, you enjoy good times (“flowers blossom”) when you are young (springtime). There is no need to worry about your luck changing suddenly (“One night of dusty winds to blow it all over”).
