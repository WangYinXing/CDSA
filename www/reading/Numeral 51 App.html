Numeral 51    Ji Jia

Hexagram:  Earth on Thunder

Heavenly Ceremonial Star

Hour Pillar Jia Zi – Wood Rat Jia Yin – Wood Tiger
Metaphor Riding a horse on the heavenly street. Germinating buds meeting drought.
Explanation You have the will to keep actively thinking of new ideas. Destined to be in humble circumstances, be prepared to encounter challenges.

Heavenly Wealth Star

Hour Pillar Jia Chen – Wood Dragon Jia Wu – Wood Horse
Metaphor Withered pine in the snow. Cicada on willow tree.
Explanation Even though you experience adversity, your ambitions are firm. There are honorable people to support you therefore, you can do something great.

Heavenly Bug Star

Hour Pillar Jia Shen – Wood Monkey Jia Xu – Wood Dog
Metaphor Moon shining over cold pond. Fish in winter rely on grass in the pond.
Explanation When the timing is not with you, there are people coming to help. Protected by the fence of other people, you are cared for by foster parents. Remain positive about the future.

 

Destiny:
In this life, you are often going the wrong direction because instead of walking in the dark shining a light on the path ahead, it is like you are walking backwards and shining a light on where you have just stepped. Reflecting on where you have just been is not the same as looking at where you are going. You are carefree and although you are good at starting things, finishing them is another matter.  Your character is one with low tolerance levels and you are uncompromising. Therefore, it is not easy for you to enjoy a harmonious marriage and it is suitable to have children late in life.  You need rely on your own pair of hands and not to count on relatives being able to give you support. Opportunities to meet honorable people come before you, but it is not easy to seriously prosper. Be wary of playing host to those friends who give sarcastic remarks after enjoying your generous hospitality.  It can feel like you do not receive the full rewards for all your good deeds, yet you want to do them. Whoever you saved might not show their gratitude. You have to be careful with everything, sadly, this is your fate.

 

Luck Verses

Foundation: 
Ride the dragon on flat ground to reach the blue sky.
Travel a great distance, leave home, say goodbye.
Go to create good karma by cultivating good deeds.
Road to success is not far away, see where it leads.

Life Path:
Give your ideas the chance to grow completely.
Profitability depends on luck deserved naturally.
Your many good deeds match heaven’s wishes fully.
Therefore, you face no harm or calamity.

Marriage:
Mend the string on the musical instrument1,gently.
To fill the empty room, you seek to remarry.
Find another drum to play a melody.
Children and descendants, there are many.
Put down the pipa2, singing all alone, lonely.
Songs remind you to return to home country.

Children:
In late spring, watch the shadow of a branch of flowers.
There are a lot of sons, yet long for daughters,
So you are enjoying being leisurely.
Born with the beauty of a pine tree,
Plum blossom outside the window looks chilly.

Harvest:
In the garden in spring, peach and plum are blossoming.
Expect not flowers destroyed in one night by rain pouring, wind blowing.
You can recall what happened whilst dreaming.
In your dreams you have all the beauty of life brimming.


Style: Two Lotus Flowers Growing in the Pond
Fame and fortune will not be complete for you throughout your life.
The lotus roots have shoots of green leaves sprouting.
Mandarin ducks bathing in the water separate early.
In autumn, wild geese fly away for the same destination.
When other people have their sons when they are old, you have flowers to keep you company when you are old.
When travelling and meeting the water flowing in peach valley what is seen is the ox sleeping under the willow tree.


Footnote 1
A couple making music together is a metaphor for a marriage. A broken string on a musical instrument means that one of the couple has died and to mend the string means to re-marry.  In ancient China the mortality rate was high and it was fairly common to lose a spouse.

Footnote 2
Pipa is a string musical instrument. To put down a musical instrument and sing without it implies one half of the couple has departed.
