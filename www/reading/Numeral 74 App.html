Numeral 74    Xin Ding

Hexagram: Heaven on Earth

Stars

Heavenly Hair Star

Hour Pillar Ding Chou – Fire Ox Ding Mao – Fire Rabbit
Metaphor Appreciating the bright moon at night. Bare branches waiting for spring.
Explanation Very good late years. When you are cold and life is difficult, keep a peaceful mind and wait with patience.

Heavenly Broad-mindedness Star

Hour Pillar Ding Si – Fire Snake Ding Wei – Fire Goat
Metaphor Stand alone to appreciate the lotus. Fallen petals meeting the rain.
Explanation You are alone without much assistance, fortunately, you have good children. Beware of risky situations in your late years.

Heavenly Bestowing Star

Hour Pillar Ding You – Fire Rooster Ding Hai – Fire Pig
Metaphor Dragon in clouds is present. Cold moon in autumn.
Explanation Meeting someone who appreciates you and your future is bright. After middle age you will be happy and free.

Poetic Life Reading

Destiny:

This life is like the wind and thunder, there is sound but no form. You want to skillful but you become inelegant. You want to walk a straight path, yet you have to deviate a bit. You get money like you get rain during April showers and you spend money like the high tide at dawn. Whilst you can generate a good income, it also flows out quickly.  Although you have a lot of good plans, life cannot be satisfactory all the time. You work for others but do not have a bosom friend.  Whoever you expect to be your intimate friend is not really that intimate. Whoever is not supposed to be your close friend turns out to be very close. To avoid ill-omens, if you are a man you should marry a woman who is strong and hearty.  It is a good omen if your children are difficult in the beginning and easy in the end.

 

Luck Verses

Foundation:
Everybody says orchids and wonderful grass are auspicious, are lucky.
The honorable man has a firm fate and he is honored by many.
Accumulating good deeds and earning a great name are fine qualities.
People will appreciate you from youth to maturity.

Life Path: 
Rat and Ox years for you are most lucky.
The path will be flat, leading you to glory.
People who appreciate you are in the south-west, mostly.
They will help bring you to the capital city.

Marriage:
It is very difficult to get married early.
Marriage depends on finding harmony.
When the characters of husband and wife do not match, it will be difficult to last.
Enjoy cool breeze under bright moon with someone who knows you best.
In your late years sleep alone, hear the bell tolling as you rest.

Children:
One tree has shed its flowers, on another good fruits bearing.
East wind is taking good care of children, mothers are caring.
For wonderful jade, a long time you’re waiting.
Good mulberry trees, satisfaction they’re bringing.

Harvest:
Ox and Goat year misfortune comes one after another,
Through Tiger and Rabbit years miserable times linger.
Even good doctors cannot keep you healthy.
Although moon is bright over pavilion, cannot appreciate the beauty.

  

Style: Wild Geese Flies over High Mountain
Goose flies over mountain with tired wings.
It is good to follow water fowls to reshape feathers.
At the beginning of Dog year, rest a bit again and again.
At end of Rat year everything will be to your heart’s content.
All because of meeting a good friend in a strange land.
You will reach the sky from ground level because there is a ladder to walk on.
Cuckoo is weeping in the rain.
It won’t come home unless there are turbulent waves in the well

Commentary

Destiny

“This life is like the wind and thunder, there is sound but no form,” implies that you like talking about your ideas. You also need to take action to make them happen. Quite often, life is not straightforward, yet that is what makes it interesting.

Luck Verses

Foundation

You create good karma and you are lucky enough to have a good reputation throughout your life.

Life Path

“The path will be flat, leading you to glory,” suggests that you do not need to struggle in order to have achievement. People from the south-west help you to succeed (“help bring you to the capital city”).

Marriage

“Enjoy cool breeze under bright moon with someone who knows you best,” suggests that a happy marriage can be found when you and your spouse understand each other well. “In your late years sleep alone, hear the bell tolling as you rest,” implies that you might outlive your spouse, or that you separate.

Children (‘Children’ can also mean subordinates or followers)

“For wonderful jade, a long time you’re waiting,” suggests that you need to be patient, for you might need to wait before children are born. You will have good children (“good mulberry trees”). For followers, you can expect good followers to come late.

Harvest

Take extra care during Ox years and Goat years. Ox, Tiger and Rabbit are consecutive years. To avoid problems in Tiger years and Rabbit years, you need to look after your health in Ox years. You might find yourself in pleasant surroundings (“moon is bright over pavilion”), but you need to be fit and well in order to enjoy life.

Style

You travel far away (“A goose flies over mountain with tired wings,”) and re-invent yourself (“reshape feathers”). You will get to the top step by step (“reach the sky from ground level because there is a ladder to walk on”). After you succeed a long way from home (“in a strange land”), you are unlikely to return to your hometown (“it won’t come home”).
