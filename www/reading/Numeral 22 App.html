Numeral 22   Bing Yi

Hexagram: Fire on Wind

Stars

Fame and Fortune Star

Hour Pillar Yi Chou – Wood Ox Yi Mao – Wood Rabbit
Metaphor Spring swallows coming back to nest. Good harvest and you can easily pay your taxes.
Explanation Hard-working and quite frugal, a lot of children to make you happy. An affluent life with gourmet foods and fine clothing. Nothing to worry about.

Coziness Improvement Star

Hour Pillar Yi Si – Wood Snake Yi Wei – Wood Goat
Metaphor Broken fan on a hot day. “Fairyland flower” in the rain.
Explanation Whatever your capabilities, you still have good times. Whatever trials and tribulations you face, you remain unharmed.

Phoenix Chamber Star

Hour Pillar Yi You – Wood Rooster Yi Hai – Wood Pig
Metaphors Hidden stars waiting for the clouds to disperse. Cicada on willow tree in summer.
Explanation You have success in later years, you have to wait patiently. Despite a shallow foundation, fortunately, there is something you can cling onto.

Poetic Life Reading

Destiny:

Everyone admires your life which appears to have the brilliance of a diamond, in the olden days it would have been described as like lustrous jade and shiny gold. Like the three legged urn, which is full of potential, you are someone of great ability. Like the pillar and beams that support a building, you are someone capable of holding your own. You are a child prodigy, whose gifts are less noticeable when you get older. Good things come meandering into your life; they don’t come in a straightforward fashion. Whenever there is something wrong, something that needs changes, you can waste a lot of time. Despite making a lot of effort the gains do not measure up, you work hard, but do not build anything that is solid and practical.  Alas, the good things do not come in full, like beautiful clouds they are easily dispersed, like fragile, colorful glassware they are easily broken. When you are young, you have great ambition and can show you are clearly talented. Unfortunately, luck is not with you when you are in your early years. When you start out you are as proud as the dragon, yet the dragon changes to a leopard, you have to accept changes.

 

Luck Verses

Foundation: 
The capital city is beautiful in spring,
In your youth, you are held in high esteem.
To go to the phoenix pond is right, it seems.
Even if you don’t get to wear a belt of gold,
Many good things are coming, for you to behold.

Life Path:
You visit beautiful places as you travel,
Yet, it’s not as good as shooting an arrow.
In the end you will be honored with the flying horse,
Wherever you go, you can have achievement, of course.

Marriage:
Like a crane on three islands, you are hard to get,
With tiger and dragon being on guard,
Finding a compatible spouse is quite hard.
Unlike the mandarin ducks that settle down,
You flirt with the seagulls all over town.

Children:
Two flowers means two daughters, a fruit one son.
Early on you plant bamboo on dry land.
Among the rocks, a beautiful orchid sprouts,
A wonderful fragrance, it sends out.
You encourage your son, but a daughter has more clout

Harvest:
In Dog years, if you get drunk be most careful.
Rooster crows facing water, means consequences awful.
All the time, reflect on your dreams that came early.
Don’t take the first opportunity, examine every possibility.

 

Style: Two Birds Sleeping in the Deep Forest
The bird wakes up and leaves the forest, the timing is not right.
Remove the dust on the ladder before you can walk up the ladder to heaven.
Wild geese flying in formation over autumn waters leave the northern territories.
They make an abode in the deep forest on a very secure branch.
Don’t forget the creator who made this world.
You don’t have to worry, everything will come right.
If you want to know which direction to go to make a good living, the wind brings the fragrance of the flowers to give you the guidance.

Commentary

Destiny

When you are young, your talents are already apparent. When you encounter changing circumstances, it would benefit you to accept changes.

Luck Verses

Foundation

“The capital city is beautiful in spring” alludes to you having achievement when you are young. You are able to reach high places (“phoenix pond”), even if you do not amass great wealth (“wear a belt of gold”).

Life Path

“Shooting an arrow” means to aim high, to have big ambitions. To be “honored with a flying horse” implies that you will gain an award of some sort for your achievements.

Marriage

“Like a crane on three islands, you are hard to get, with tiger and dragon being on guard” suggests that you act uninterested, and tend to be apprehensive when someone shows a romantic interest in you. To “flirt with the seagulls all over town” suggests that you can enjoy the single life until you find your match.

Children (‘Children’ can also mean subordinates or followers)

You can have two daughters and one son. Even though you give your son plenty of encouragement (“plant bamboo on dry land”), it is your daughter who has accomplishment (“among the rocks, a beautiful orchid sprouts”). For followers, your female followers will have greater achievement than your male followers.

Harvest

“Rooster crows facing water, means consequences awful” suggests that there could be unpleasant outcomes if you get drunk during Dog years.

Style

“Remove the dust on the ladder before you can walk up the ladder to heaven,” implies that you have to deal with practical matters before you can climb to success. You can make changes and find yourself in a safe place (“Wild geese flying in formation over autumn waters leave the northern territories. They make an abode in the deep forest on a very secure branch”). “The wind brings the fragrance of the flowers to give you guidance” suggests that you can follow your nose.
