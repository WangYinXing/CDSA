Numeral 49 Wu Ren

Hexagram: Mountain on Water

Stars

Heavenly Abundance Star 

Hour Pillar Ren Zi – Water Rat Ren Yin – Water Tiger
Metaphor Fine during the day, rain at night. Hibiscus blossoming on the bank.
Explanation Everything is smooth, there are no worries. You get help everywhere.

Heavenly Fortune Star

Hour Pillar Ren Chen – Water Dragon Ren Wu – Water Horse
Metaphor Sunflower in a pot, facing the sun. Boating in shallow waters.
Explanation You meet noble people and the fortune is immeasurable. Facing adversity, but there is hope in the end.

Heavenly Blasting Star

Hour Pillar Ren Shen – Water Monkey Ren Xu – Water Dog
Metaphor Firefly emitting light. Walking at night under the moon.
Explanation You love to be charitable, never mind whether you will get a good reward. Luck in old age is very good. Great fortune and great rewards.

Poetic Life Reading

Destiny:

In this life you were born with a character that is very upright but a nature that is soft and tender.  You have the heart to love people and never intend to hurt anyone. Noble people will put you in an important position in their eyes, so there is no need to be concerned about ignoble people, who will look down on you. As you live with people with different surname, it could be that you were adopted or placed in foster care. This is a positive situation as your destiny is such that it would have been disadvantageous for you to stay in the family.  People can change. When you are doing things, you can blow hot and cold. Sometimes you are very enthusiastic, sometimes you are indifferent. You need to manage your expectations when you are doing good things, for you cannot always expect a good return. Throughout this life, you won’t be hit by poverty and there are no legal problems.  However, luck is not with you in your early days. Just as the melon is sweet, and the stalk is bitter, sweetness and bitterness come in different parts of life. Nevertheless, you can live a long life, which is very glorious in later years.


Luck Verses

Foundation: 
Gold and jade are not the real treasures.
Cannot distinguish ling zhi1and orchids, from fragrant grass.
Beneath the bright moon, cool breeze over lakes and rivers, 
To find fame and long life, you borrow the path.

Life Path:
Start business with gold and jade, the original plan.
Like rams knock down the fence, a defiant enterprise,
Becomes an empire with a long-lasting life span.
During sunset pick up profits to live a leisurely life.

Marriage:
You meet bright moon over autumn river.
Halfway you see the flower wither,
And just pick one branch.
At night water fowls sleep in the water.
Fragrant apple, water chestnut follow one another.
Marry again, second chance.

Children: ;
Strange flowers, strange fruits imply stepchildren.
Due to many hidden virtues, you enjoy filial duty.
High achievers bring glory to the family.
Wearing red and purple, they’re nobility. 

Harvest:
On the path that’s homeward bound,
Walk without any companions.
Under the moon, there in a lone boat, on the cold river,
Recollect your old home.
See white clouds over the mountain, like a beautiful picture.


Style: Orchid, Ling Zhi (Medicinal Mushroom) and Fragrant Grass
Gold and jade are not necessarily treasure.
Your attention is focused on orchid, medicinal mushrooms and fragrant grass.
Your home is by the water and the clouds.
How many flowers can you see on the top of the trees?
Pi-Pa2 has broken string, mend the string to re-marry.
In the evening, you enjoy the cool breeze.
You seek another romance from the plum blossom and snowy moon.
Fortunately, there is not much fog on the waves, and it is good to follow the emperor to visit places outside the capital.

 

Footnote 1
Ling Zhi is a type of medicinal mushroom believed to have anti-cancerous properties.

Footnote 2
Pi-Pa is a string musical instrument. Playing a Pi-Pa is symbolic of a happy marriage.

Commentary

Destiny

It is beneficial for you to live and mix with people other than your own family, so be relaxed when you are away from the family home. Accept that there are ups and downs in life.

Luck Verses

Foundation

Money is not everything (“gold and jade are not the real treasures”). Fragrant grass is quite common whereas Ling zhi (medicinal mushroom) and orchids are not. You need to distinguish between what is special from what is ordinary. When you have good luck (“bright moon, cool breeze”) you can find a way (“borrow the path”) to become well known.

Life Path

You invest in a new business (“start business with gold and jade”), but in the beginning it does not go according to plan (“Like rams knock down the fence, a defiant enterprise”). In the end, it turns out to be a lasting business and generates good profits for you in your later years (“during sunset”).

Marriage

There is good fortune when it comes to relationships (“bright moon”) but it does not last (“flower wither”). However, you get another chance to marry again.

Children (‘Children’ can also mean subordinates or followers)

Your stepchildren are successful and influential (“wearing red and purple”). Because of good karma from your charitable acts your stepchildren are devoted to you. For followers, you can attract loyal and outstanding followers.

Harvest

You can look back and recall many highlights from your journey through life.

Style

Rather than focus on wealth, it would be a good idea to focus on your wellbeing. “Your home is by the water and the clouds” could be taken to mean you live near water, somewhere high up, or it could mean that you make your home anywhere you choose. You are likely to marry again later in life (“plum blossom and snowy moon” imply your winter years, the last stage of your life). Fortunately, not much blocks your path through life and it is good to follow the leader (“emperor”) to step out into the wider world.
