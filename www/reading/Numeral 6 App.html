Numeral 6    Jia Ji

Hexagram: Thunder on Earth

Stars

Heavenly Stamp Star

Hour Pillar Ji Chou – Earth Ox Ji Mao – Wood Rabbit
Metaphor Frosty moon in a clear sky. Thirsty horse drinking from the spring.
Explanation There is a path leading to success, you can climb the corporate ladder. All troubles are in the past, make the most of the good days while they last.

Heavenly Sparkle Star

Hour Pillar Ji Si – Earth Snake Ji Wei – Earth Goat
Metaphor Wading through water to catch fish. Dusty sword loses its luster.
Explanation In Ren and Gui years you gain rewards from your plans. Even without mentors you can be a bold and clever hero.

Heavenly Symbol Star

Hour Pillar Ji You – Earth Rooster Ji Hai – Earth Pig
Metaphor Looking for the moon behind the clouds. Wilting grass meeting spring.
Explanation It is suitable for you to go abroad to find people who appreciate your abilities. Your old age will be enjoyable as you develop new interests.

Poetic Life Reading

Destiny:

This life is fortunate, blessed by a fortunate star that brings many happy events. You want a peaceful life, yet you have many worries.  In your most embarrassing and uncomfortable moments, you are rescued by a lucky star. You think your ideas are amazing, but they turn out to be nothing spectacular and are rather ordinary. When you think you are doing the right thing, the outcome is wrong. Your relatives treat you as a stranger, outsiders understand you a lot better.  There is a mismatch with your spouse. The pair of you are like chalk and cheese, you do not share much in common. Being an unhappy couple affects the children. There are a lot of arguments with your siblings with nothing accomplished, like clattering an iron broom against a copper bowl. Yet in the end the harvest is good.

 

Luck Verses

Foundation:
Great goals to catch the celebrated turtle,
Difficult to go fish in stormy high seas,
Nevertheless you overcome many troubles.
Lush plum blossoms and sturdy maple trees,
Remind you of home sweet home, comfort in dreams.

Life Path:
Seeking big goals, ambitions are high.
A golden horse, you shall ride,
To meet influential people in the jade hall,
Your good name in famous places, leaves them enthralled.

Marriage:
A single wild goose flies towards a great big tree,
To welcome him, a crane sings notes beautifully.
On the branches of a maple tree they stay, resting firmly.
Seagulls and herons flock around, happy times in good company.

Children:
At sunset the cicada sings on the branch of the willow.
Red evening clouds fly across blue skies,
Herons bathing in the pond below.
On the apple tree, blossoms are red and white,
Evening breeze caresses the dream into the night.

Harvest:
You stop the horse and look back on a spring scene so charming,
On a high mountain you shoot three arrows, carefully aiming,
Thought you had shot a bird, yet all birds are laughing.
When the clouds blow away, you finish daydreaming.

 

Style: Phoenix Sleeping in the Maple Tree
Like a phoenix fishing for intelligent turtles,
You sail along bravely in high seas and high waves.
Wild goose flies over clear sky, seagulls making their journey of migration.
Rain falls so hard the branches of the tree are damaged,
The fruits are in danger of falling off.
If your hometown is in the north, you head south, to settle down elsewhere.
In springtime you will meet helpful people.
Year of the Dog brings you success.
In Ox and Horse years you will have good motivation.

 

Commentary

Destiny

Even though you can enjoy good luck, not everything in your life is perfect. You might have differences with your spouse and disagreements with your siblings. It all turns out well in the end.

Luck Verses

Foundation

You can achieve your goals even though you face some difficulties. You will miss home, which suggests that you moved away.

Life Path

You are highly ambitious and can gain a good reputation. You can climb the greasy pole to meet the top people.

Marriage

You and your spouse are from different backgrounds and may have some misunderstandings to begin with. You can enjoy a good social life with lots of friends.

Children (‘Children’ can also mean subordinates or followers)

Children can bring happiness. They will fly the nest. Followers will come and go.

Harvest

When you look back you can reminisce about good times. You might not get everything you dreamed about. Not all your high ambitions will be met (you aim, but you do not hit your target).

Style

You are willing to face challenges in order to seek a worthwhile aim. You can soar to great heights, whilst others are being quite ordinary. The circumstances you face can sometimes be risky. You are likely to move away from your hometown. “In springtime you will meet helpful people” can refer to your youth, or the time of year.
