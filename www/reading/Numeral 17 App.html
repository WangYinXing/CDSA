Numeral 17 Yi Geng

Hexagram: Wind on Lake

Heavenly Fortune Star

Hour Pillar Geng Zi – Metal Rat Geng Yin – Metal Tiger 
Metaphor Warming the wine on the red clay furnace pot. The tree is bare in winter, new leaves sprout in spring.
Explanation Bing and Ding years bring benefactors to your aid. Bad luck is left behind and you encounter good luck in Jia and Yi years.

Heavenly Dynamic Star

Hour Pillar Geng Chen – Metal Dragon Geng Wu - Metal Horse
Metaphor Filtering gold from sand in the river. Roll up the bamboo screen to see the moon.
Explanation Ren and Gui years can be profitable. There are opportunities, but the time is not right, you still have to wait.

Heavenly Tongue Star

Hour Pillar Geng Shen – Metal Monkey Geng Xu – Metal Dog
Metaphor Hungry bird flying through the forest. Flowers blossom in spring.
Explanation Poverty in early years, yet you will live a leisurely and enjoyable life in later years. You can get rich young.

 

Destiny:
This life is like gold and jade, it is ruled by a brilliant star.  This is a kind star that brings gourmet food and beautiful clothing.  You can shoulder responsibility and have the ability to manage.  You pretend to be cautious and applaud loudly. When you offer incense to Buddha, you are a bit lazy about talking to Buddha, suggesting a lack of sincerity in worship. You want to help people and don’t want to hurt others.  Getting seriously rich and building an empire is what motivates you.  There is always a road paved for you to escape from misfortune, yet when you think all will happen easily, there are barriers.  Whatever you gain, you have to work hard for it. Superiors love you, yet you lack a good rapport with subordinates.

 

Luck Verses 

Foundation:
It is not difficult for you to get to see the emperor.
When you come, two mountain ranges obstruct the way.
Who knows why? Yet picture looks good and you can make it easily.
About own matters you’re leisurely, other people’s problems keep you busy.

Life Path:
When your hair’s grey your wealth will be plenty.
Achievement will also come belatedly.
You acquire fields, orchards, lots of property.
Looking back, a life lived beautifully. 
Walked ten thousand miles, worked diligently.

Marriage:
Mandarin ducks snooze cozily together,
Amongst blossoms, it’s a beautiful picture.
At night time lots of other birds gather around.
Yet you are still afraid of the rain and wind.

Children:
At night time the fruits ripen,
Late in life you’ll have a son.
East winds brings you many students,
Indicative of your power and influence.

Harvest:
When you are old, in Ox and Horse years, take care.
Maple trees by the riverside.
Life is like a dream, you should be aware.
Setting moon hides behind a tree.

 

Style: Meeting Someone at the Golden Gate
Single-minded about conquering the two mountains, yet you don’t have the time, you are too busy.
There is a boat waiting for you by the riverside.
Money is hiding, like the moon hides behind the clouds, wait for wealth.
Work very hard but everything turns into a dream.
It is not too difficult to meet with someone on the way.
When the time is right and the yang qi begins to turn, then you will be summoned to the golden gate to meet the emperor.
You are flexible and well-planned.
When you are young there are a lot of advancement and retreats, a lot of ups and downs.
When your hair is grey, you will have the time of your life.
