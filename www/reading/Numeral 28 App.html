Numeral 28    Bing Xin

Hexagram: Fire on Heaven

Stars

Heavenly Retreating Star

Hour Pillar Xin Chou – Metal Ox Xin Mao – Metal Rabbit
Metaphor Bright moon on plum blossom. Decayed bud meeting spring
Explanation You enjoy a leisurely life throughout. The worst has passed. Difficulties are over, and there are a lot of opportunities.

Heavenly Gloomy Star

Hour Pillar Xin Si – Metal Snake Xin Wei – Metal Goat
Metaphor Lighting up the candle in the wind. Lonely goose flying in the autumn sky.
Explanation Good days are not that frequent, beware of how you are affected by changes. After middle age, be prepared to be self-sufficient.

Heavenly Terrace Star

Hour Pillar Xin You – Metal Rooster Xin Hai – Metal Pig
Metaphor Sailing at sea on a paper boat. Fish swimming into the net.
Explanation Avoid over-estimating your ability. Avoid walking into traps and wasting energy in doing so.

Poetic Life Reading

Destiny:

This life is full of good planning and technical skills. When you spend money, you can get good returns. Whatever you need to do, you do it in the proper way. Unfortunately, your destiny is meeting the Retreating Spirit, you do not always move forwards. You are very calculating, and you calculate very accurately and have necessary capital to do whatever you want. Even though you may work hard, up to midnight, you can muddle up what you have done. If you want success, you have to make a lot of reforms. It is just because your destiny chart is firm. Even though there is danger, what turns up will only be half of what was expected. The malevolent forces will somehow be mitigated. In your old age you are strong, you are just as energetic as when you were young. When you reach water to help you, you will have great achievement. Water years will be the most successful times for you.

 

Luck Verses

Foundation:

Like the pine sinking roots amongst the rocks, your base is secure.
Young and ambitious, your goal to establish a large empire,
As the years wear away, you draw your sword and play,
Lament on all the problems that brought you dismay.

Life Path:
The capital city, a thousand miles away,
Eventually, you reach the destination.
Rise through the ranks, gaining a position.
Return home before the end of the story,
Enjoy bamboo, pine and mulberry.

Marriage:
On meeting your beloved during your prime,
You cannot expect it to last for all time.
Together when the moon, bright and beautiful,
Pond full of sea birds, enjoy being a couple.

Children:
Fruits as sons, two or three,
Leave one in the forest.
Good sons bring glory to your family,
Because in a past life, many good deeds.

Harvest:
In years of Dog or Ox, with water,
Dive to the sea bed, collect coral.
Ox or Goat year, doing well, cross the bridge to the fairies,
Enjoy being by west lake with beautiful willow trees.

 

Style: Crane Standing on the Pine Tree
Pine tree stands proudly in spring and autumn.
White crane tries to land on top of the tree, even if some branches are broken in heavy frost and snow.
The pine is quite a different structure from the flowering peach and plum trees.~
Your relatives leave you like a floating cloud.
Whatever assets you have will be halved, yet half will be enough.
If you take good care of the mulberry tree in your back garden, you will enjoy the shade of the tree in the end.

Commentary

Destiny

You are organised and like to do things properly. Your destiny is such you do not always move forwards, you may need to take a step back before you can advance (“meeting the Retreating spirit, you do not always move forwards”). Water years can mean those years beginning with a digit 2 or a digit 3, e.g. 1992, 1993, 2002, 2003, 2012, 2013, 2022, 2023, etc. Water years can also mean Pig years (1995, 2007, 2019, etc) or Rat years (1996, 2008, 2020, etc).

Luck Verses

Foundation

You have high ambitions and a solid foundation. Later on in life, you can look back on what worked out and what did not.

Life Path

“The capital city” can mean the headquarters or any large organisation. You have career success and enjoy your retirement.

Marriage

Married life can be satisfying. It is important to make the most of the good times you share together.

Children (‘Children’ can also mean subordinates or followers)

You can have two or three sons. Your sons will be successful because of your good karma from a past life. For followers, your followers will have achievement.

Harvest

In Dog years or Ox years you can gain wealth (“dive to the sea bed to collect coral”). Coral was seen as something precious. Ox and Goat years can also prove to be fortunate and you could find yourself in beautiful surroundings.

Style

You are full of determination (“Pine tree stands proudly in spring and autumn”), and can remain persistent in the face of adversity (“even if some branches are broken in heavy frost and snow”). You can endure challenges that others might not face. You cannot rely on your relatives. If you look after what you have, you will have enough for what you need.
