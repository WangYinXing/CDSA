Numeral 83    Ren Bing

Hexagram:  Water on Fire

Heavenly Chef Star

Hour Pillar Bing Zi – Fire Rat Bing Yin – Fire Tiger
Metaphor Starting the journey on a snowy day. Autumn leaves flying in the wind.
Explanation Born into a modest background but with a strong determination, eventually there is success. When you are facing hardship there is a noble person giving you a hand.

Heavenly Pillar Star

Hour Pillar Bing Chen – Fire Dragon Bing Wu – Fire Horse
Metaphor Playing a flute inside a cave. Peony in spring.
Explanation You can enjoy a leisurely life. With early prosperity you can walk up to the blue sky.

Heavenly Contentment Star

Hour Pillar Bing Shen– Fire Monkey Bing Xu – Fire Dog
Metaphor Firefly giving out light on a cold night. The flower coming out of a rotten branch.
Explanation Whatever you do you cannot boast about it in front of other people. After failure there is a chance.

 

Destiny
You are born straightforward and quick in action. Although you are bold in taking up responsibility, you are never bold without careful planning. When others give you flattery, you should not give in to flowery words. You believe in always standing upright. Unfortunately, what you get in return are complaints and resentments.  In working hard for everything it can bring lawsuits and gossip.  Out of the kindness of your heart, you carry another person across the river in your boat. What you get in return are hassles and worries.

 

Luck Verses

Foundation:
Carriage has turned over, so you have to put it upright again.
When you’re lost, the path is difficult to walk on, don’t lament.
Good friends will come to bring you to the capital city.
Where the world is at your feet, you worked successfully.

Life Path:
Noble people join you and lead you in right direction.
Repeated wealth, repeated glory, you make it happen.
Spend time on mountains with water for your enjoyment.
Swallows and nightingales, romance has time limitation.

Marriage:
Like changing the key to play another melody,
Change your spouse another you’ll marry.
Cannot play the right note with an impure string,
It’s not like the old song, you’re used to playing.
Just watch out for the bright moon in the cool breeze at night.
Cries from crane and baboon sound like complaining.

Children:
After rain and wind not many flowers blooming.
In autumn, there will be three fruits hanging.
Some are unripe and some are ripening.
Mulberry trees under the moon, hear cuckoo crowing.

Harvest:
When Rabbit runs and Rooster crows, obstacles to overcome.
In late spring, moon sets to hide behind mountains.
After difficulty, you’re like an old goose feeling melancholy.
Better to learn Buddhist philosophy and do deeds for charity.

 

Style: Monkey Playing in Pine Forest
After the rain, the mountains are beautiful.
When the clouds fly away, the full moon is bright again.
The world is full of beautiful things.
All you need is to have your heart ready for the beauty of nature.
When the goose has flown to a distant mountain,
Flowers have gone leaving behind two fresh fruits.
Tiger’s waist is hurt by a knife.
The ox horn is replaced by the violin bow.
