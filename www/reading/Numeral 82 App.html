Numeral 82   Ren Yi

Hexagram: Water on Wind

Heavenly Dim Star

Hour Pillar Yi Chou – Wood Ox Yi Mao – Wood Rabbit
Metaphor Wearing the clouds to face the moon. Withered tree meeting spring.
Explanation Be content with reasonable wishes then nothing bothers you and there are no worries. Even though the inherited business is going down, there is a sign of life again.

Heavenly Sinking Star

Hour Pillar Yi Si – Wood Snake Yi Wei – Wood Goat
Metaphor Pine and cedar in winter. Fish escaping the net.
Explanation With a determined mind, poverty will not hurt you. Fail in the beginning, with eventual success. Everyone admires you.

Heavenly Bestowing Star 

Hour Pillar Yi You – Wood Rooster Yi Hai – Wood Pig
Metaphors Petals from the willow tree flying in the wind. Galloping under the rocky hill.
Explanation Stick close to the noble people it is easy to get rich. Everywhere you have protection, there is no worry.

 

Destiny:
This life is like an outstanding star shining brilliantly in the chaotic universe. Yet when watching from the ground it is like a heavy and muddy star in a comet.  There is a beginning but no ending. Forget the past and lose the future. When it comes to making friends, it is easy for you to come together and easy for you to part with them.  Among relatives, three of them are happy to be in your company, yet two of them are worried about staying together with you. It is suitable to be half a monk and half a layman.  It is only in this way can you go after fame and fortune. This suggests you can be a worldly religious person who is able to apply spiritual values to be successful.  Spouse and children are in danger of being lost. You cannot live on the inherited business.  It is not suitable to live alone by yourself, it is better for you mix with others and to live under the same roof with various other people.
