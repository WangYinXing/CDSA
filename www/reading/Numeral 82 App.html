Numeral 82   Ren Yi

Hexagram: Water on Wind

Stars

Heavenly Dim Star

Hour Pillar Yi Chou – Wood Ox Yi Mao – Wood Rabbit
Metaphor Wearing the clouds to face the moon. Withered tree meeting spring.
Explanation Be content with reasonable wishes then nothing bothers you and there are no worries. Even though the inherited business is going down, there is a sign of life again.

Heavenly Sinking Star

Hour Pillar Yi Si – Wood Snake Yi Wei – Wood Goat
Metaphor Pine and cedar in winter. Fish escaping the net.
Explanation With a determined mind, poverty will not hurt you. Fail in the beginning, with eventual success. Everyone admires you.

Heavenly Bestowing Star 

Hour Pillar Yi You – Wood Rooster Yi Hai – Wood Pig
Metaphors Petals from the willow tree flying in the wind. Galloping under the rocky hill.
Explanation Stick close to the noble people it is easy to get rich. Everywhere you have protection, there is no worry.

Poetic Life Reading

Destiny:

This life is like an outstanding star shining brilliantly in the chaotic universe. Yet when watching from the ground it is like a heavy and muddy star in a comet.  There is a beginning but no ending. Forget the past and lose the future. When it comes to making friends, it is easy for you to come together and easy for you to part with them.  Among relatives, three of them are happy to be in your company, yet two of them are worried about staying together with you. It is suitable to be half a monk and half a layman.  It is only in this way can you go after fame and fortune. This suggests you can be a worldly religious person who is able to apply spiritual values to be successful.  Spouse and children are in danger of being lost. You cannot live on the inherited business.  It is not suitable to live alone by yourself, it is better for you mix with others and to live under the same roof with various other people.

Commentary

Destiny

“It is suitable to be half a monk and half a layman,” suggests that you can find a balance between spiritual values and materialistic desires. Learn to live in the present and not be concerned about what others might think.

Luck Verses

Foundation

Dragon and phoenix dancing, a dazzling display,” suggests that you need to be prepared to show your ability, in order to make a name for yourself. “Gold has to be refined many times before it is shiny,” means that you keep developing your skills. “To get close to the emperor, need to have met noble people already,” implies that being well connected is important for getting to know those at the top.

Life Path

You are likely to bring about change. Do not be afraid to speak about your ideas. Who you meet and where you meet them is important to your success.

Marriage

There are opportunities for finding a relationship (“Cast your net to catch fish, it meets the tide with good timing”). Be realistic when it comes to choosing a spouse, because you do not want to miss a good chance.

Children (‘Children’ can also mean subordinates or followers)

Two daughters and one son are predicted (“two flowers and one fruit”). “One fruit, that’s strangely crimson” could be referring to an outstanding son, who might come along later in life. For followers, you attract capable ones.

Harvest

Tiger and Rabbit years are likely to bring challenges (“on the mountain climbing, in the rabbit garden running”), and you might have reasons to feel sad (“Cuckoo tears flowing down”). Nevertheless, there are many good times (“bright moon and cooling breeze”) and you enjoy a sense of freedom.

Style

You have hidden talents (“hard rock is hiding jade inside”) and your skills need to be refined (“Gold is made in the cold sand”). There is no need to try too hard (“When you really want to plant flowers, they will wither”), for you will get what you need (“When you do not intend to plant willow, the willow tree will give you shelter”).
