Numeral 69 Geng Ren

Hexagram: Lake on Water

Heavenly Galloping Horse Star

Hour Pillar Ren Zi – Water Rat Ren Yin – Water Tiger
Metaphor Riding a horse with an umbrella. Climbing the mountain to watch the moon.
Explanation Be aware of the pressure from above. To stand out from the crowd is easier said than done. High ambitions, it is hopeful.

Heavenly Prosperity Star

Hour Pillar Ren Chen – Water Dragon Ren Wu – Water Horse
Metaphor Moss meeting frost. Riding a treasure boat with the wind.
Explanation When the foundation is shallow there are a lot of uncertainties. When ability is great, career is fabulous.

Heavenly Messenger Horse Star

Hour Pillar Ren Shen – Water Monkey Ren Xu – Water Dog
Metaphor Willow on the riverside in autumn. Honeycombs filled with golden honey.
Explanation In late years, look back on earlier good times. Work hard throughout life, profit will be immeasurable.

 

Destiny:
This life is like the auspicious dragon’s head with the inauspicious snake’s tail. It is a destiny that offends the Retreating Spirit. Wealth meets the Exhausting Spirit and you cannot retain money. Your character is outspoken and frank. When you fail in the beginning, you will find success in the end. Husband and wife get success early together. Siblings betray each other.  You try hard to develop business, yet wealth is not retained and there is a lot of wasted effort. If you want to be successful wait until you reach sixty years of age.

Luck Verses

Foundation: 
Looks like a fisherman’s house in waters misty,
Your home is in the five lakes and four seas.
Withdraw fishing rod and net, when wind drops, waves calmer,
Enjoy life in a picturesque place of white and red flowers.

Life Path:
In worldly matters, much sincerity, pour in plenty of energy.
After many comings and goings, many times you get the glory.
Looking back to see beyond clouds, there the wide skies.
Stop fishing pull out the net and enjoy the rest of your life.

Marriage:
When the tree is under attack of gusts and heavy rain,
Pair of mandarin ducks need to decide, fly or not to fly?
Eventually, mandarin ducks are all over the pond with plant life.
When bright moon shines over fishing bank, the dim stars in short supply.

Children:
Flowers blossom on the tree,
In autumn, fruits left, there’re three.
Outside your door a branch, a phoenix son when you’re old,
Who brings glory to your family, praise to your household.

Harvest:
Who believes there is so much danger in those years?
One unpredictable event brings concerns and fears.
A lot of regrets on the mountain and with flowing streams,
The restaurant, where good times were had, no longer seen.

 

Style: Rain Battering the Lotus Flower
The old orchard in the mountain is not your home.
You are aware that where you should not be living, there you make a living.
Mandarin ducks are beaten to disperse in the lotus pond.
Catkins from the reeds are flying in the wind.
Wild goose is flying.
Some branches bear fruit.
Many things to do and yet they are not done.
To be successful you need help from someone who is dressed in red.
It is like the decayed tree in spring budding again.
