Numeral 43    Wu Bing

Hexagram:  Mountain on Fire

Cowboy Star

Hour Pillar Bing Zi – Fire Rat Bing Yin – Fire Tiger
Metaphor When wind is blowing on sail. Playing a musical instrument inside the urn.
Explanation Someone is about to give you a hand. Avoid wasting time when you are doing something without being noticed.

Heavenly Wolf Star

Hour Pillar Bing Chen – Fire Dragon Bing Wu – Fire Horse
Metaphor Playing a musical string instrument under the moon. Walking in the dark beautifully dressed.
Explanation Happy throughout life, leisurely and carefree. When you are successful you do not like to look extravagant.

Heavenly Emptiness Star

Hour Pillar Bing Shen– Fire Monkey Bing Xu – Fire Dog
Metaphor Lone sampan sailing in the sea. Attempting to fry off the fat on the fatty meat on top of a stone.
Explanation Beware of any risks you face when you are on your own, unassisted. Carefully manage your expectations.

 

Destiny
Like the orchids blossoming on rocks and the pine and cedar growing well on the mountain, your talents are not seen. You live in a place which is far away from the crowd. There are no strong roots. Even though your grandfather was wealthy, unfortunately, you were not born during that time period. By the time you were born the wealth had faded. Relatives cannot give you much of a hand and your siblings behave like strangers.  Your destiny implies that you could suffer childhood injuries. To avoid being disabled, you could be a little boy who is dressed up as a girl, or your parents may choose to hand you over for adoption into the care of another family. You have to trust that a sweet melon eventually carries a bit of bitterness. There is nothing perfect in the world.

 

Luck Verses

Foundation:
Rain falls on peach blossom outside the wall.
When you bring it in, not the same at all,
It will be an entirely different life.
When you sail along the west moon river,
How many friends understand you better?
Ancestral business, do not rely on,
And support from friends, do not count upon.
Behave like a tiger or a dragon,
Work hard and start a family again.

Life Path:
Recognition will be received one day,
Become rich and famous in a big way.
Dog years you will start to enjoy fortune.
Where will you gain proper recognition?
It will be someplace that is far away.

Marriage:
Keep the blind closed, swallow cannot come in and dance.
In early years, no interest in marriage or romance.
Outside, nightingale remains on the willow tree.
Autumn cinnamon has a good scent from the rocks,
Don’t say the day is late and blossom is not pretty,
It’s not like the peach in spring, still time to marry.

Children:
When you are young you have two marriages.
If there’s one fruit, already too many.
Time flies and it is autumn already,
You can sing to your young son cheerfully.

Harvest:
When you ask when is the returning path?
Then you know it is Rabbit or Monkey.
What’s beyond? At the bridgehead, you can see.
On the west river, boats sail fast at night.
Go take the boat instead of being bound in bitterness city,
Go find a doctor when you’re sick in old age, quickly, quickly.

 

Style: Wild Goose Losing the Flock
On the horizon there is water and sky joining together.
Over the sea the wild goose has missed the flock.
Back-stabbing is everywhere.
Fame and fortune is changing all the time.
At present the affluence is not real affluence.
In the later days, the poverty is not real poverty, for you have good friends.
There is someone by the river to share happy times and find entertainment in music and dancing in the jade palace.
