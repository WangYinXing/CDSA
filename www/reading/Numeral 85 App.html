Numeral 85    Ren Wu

Hexagram: Water on Mountain

Stars

Heavenly Treasure Star

Hour Pillar Wu Zi – Earth Rat Wu Yin – Earth Tiger
Metaphor Wild goose stays at jade mine. Dig earth to plant palm trees.
Explanation You go alone to a distant land. Eventually, you’ll bring home the jade. Because your roots are deep and the stalks are firm, your career is wonderful.

Heavenly Scepter Star

Hour Pillar Wu Chen – Earth Dragon Wu Wu – Earth Horse
Metaphor Sleeping peacefully in an empty house. Nightingale in the forest.
Explanation You have a wide and generous mind, there is now worry. Get good support from spouse.

Heavenly Wealth Star

Hour Pillar Wu Shen – Earth Monkey Wu Xu – Earth Dog
Metaphor Moon shining on cold pond. A fine horse trapped in the mud.
Explanation When you are in difficulty, there is a noble person coming to your rescue. At present there are a lot of difficulties. Eventually, you will be very successful.

Poetic Life Reading

Destiny:

You are born with a character that is stiff and bold. Highly ambitious, whatever you do, you go into a lot of details in the planning. Whenever you talk you can control the crowds. You stand upright like the pine tree in the cold. You are beautiful like the lonely moon shining above. Relatives will distance themselves from you and it is hard to get together with your brothers and sisters. Whatever comes together has to part. Whatever is successful carries some failed parts. Noble people will be very helpful if you respect them. You will not be a stupid flatterer and you do not like to be flattered. You lament the good days going too fast and the good things coming too late. Once you have met your bosom friend your career will climb to the top.


Luck Verses

Foundation:
With some ups and downs, your luck can flounder.
Many happenings in life are like going through barriers.
Goat and Dog years bring nice days you can look back on,
Lovely to ride on the deer and ox between two mountains.

Life Path:
Hear the thunder, hear the thunder, rainstorms coming.
Flying dragon and dancing phoenix, fortune they’re bringing.
Sitting on the terrace, leisurely sitting.
Kun fish becomes big bird Peng, to jade hall it’s flying.

Marriage:
String broken on musical instrument, replace with another.
Loss of the spouse, there’s tears wetting clothes all over.
Swallow flying away leaving people feeling saddened.
On high mountain hear the water with no companion

Children:
After the flowers are gone, there is one fruit.
Fruit that comes late is most becoming.
Mulberry trees blooming, grandchildren are coming.
See the cowboy in the breeze, the flute he’s playing.

Harvest:
Goat or Dog years prepare for the road to heaven.
Happiness and sadness mingle on reflection.
Look back on events that brought you glory.
Days are numbered, even though spring wind comes strongly.

 

Style: Jade and Gold Filling the Hall
Wild goose by the riverside is screaming in the autumn wind.
One bird comes to occupy the bushes.
Without karma, even when you come close to each other, you will not meet.
With karma, even if you are a thousand miles apart, you will meet each other.
Old cinnamon trees after passing three autumns will still be beautiful.
Thousand year old peach tree will produce one red fruit.
If the nobleman is riding the precious horse, the hall filled with jade and gold will be decorated with silk

Commentary

Destiny

When thinking ahead, choose not to hold yourself back by focusing upon too many details. You can make good use of your public speaking skills.

Luck Verses

Foundation

“Lovely to ride on the deer and ox between two mountains,” suggests that you have good times since the deer is symbolic of good luck and the ox is reliable.

Life Path

“Flying dragon and dancing phoenix, fortune they’re bringing” means you can expect good luck after facing difficulties (“rainstorms”). “Kun fish becomes big bird peng, to jade hall it’s flying,” implies that you can re-invent yourself and go far to prestigious places. Kun fish is a mythical fish that can transform itself into big bird peng, a large mythical bird with the ability to fly long distances.

Marriage

It is predicted that you go through the loss of a relationship (“string broken on musical instrument”), which is sad. However, you can find someone else and settle down again.

Children (‘Children’ can also mean subordinates or followers)

It is predicted that a son who is born when you are older is quite an amazing character. You will be happy (“cowboy in the breeze, the flute he is playing”) because there will be grandchildren. For followers, you can expect to have an outstanding follower who will continue your legacy.

Harvest

When you look back on your life there are ups and downs, yet you enjoy good luck (“spring wind comes strongly”) towards the end of your days.

Style

There is a time when you are on your own (“wild goose by the riverside is screaming in the autumn wind” and “one bird comes to occupy the bushes”), yet you are destined to meet your match. “Old cinnamon trees after passing three autumns will still be beautiful,” suggests that you enjoy good luck over a length of time. You have an outstanding son (“A thousand year old peach tree will produce one red fruit,”). You can encounter a noble person to help you to succeed.
