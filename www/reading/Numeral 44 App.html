Numeral 44    Wu Ding

Hexagram: Mountain on Earth

Heavenly Assistant Star

Hour Pillar Ding Chou – Fire Ox Ding Mao – Fire Rabbit
Metaphor Nightingale in the valley is shifting to a different tree. Planting a pine tree in the urn.
Explanation Difficult in the beginning, easy in the end. Gain promotion within a short period of time. Avoid wasting energy and time on projects that appear limited from the start.

Heavenly Revolving Star

Hour Pillar Ding Si – Fire Snake Ding Wei – Fire Goat
Metaphor Wafting with a broken fan on a hot day. Peony in Urn.
Explanation After experiencing a setback, the situation will improve. Cherish your ability, no matter how the achievements turn out.

Heavenly Treasure Star

Hour Pillar Ding You – Fire Rooster Ding Hai – Fire Pig
Metaphor Digging a well on the mountain. Pine and Cedar in spring.
Explanation Manage your expectations when seeking fame and fortune. Because of your firmness and persistence there are new opportunities.

 

Destiny:
You are born with a character that is very positive, confident and broad-minded. However, success and failure are unpredictable, and advance and retreat are uncertain. You are busy with building a reputation and gaining a good name. All the time you are trying to make a good profit. You should be more leisurely and pay less attention to seeking fame and fortune, which is on your mind continually. No wonder you are not at peace.
