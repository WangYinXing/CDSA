Numeral 60    Ji Gui

Hexagram: Earth on Mountain

Heavenly Handle Star

Hour Pillar Gui Chou – Water Ox Gui Mao – Water Rabbit
Metaphor Planting corn in green field. Clean as jade, clear as ice.
Explanation Luck is with you, harvest is wholesome. Because of your good character, everybody respects you.

Heavenly Knowledge Star

Hour Pillar Gui Si – Water Snake Gui Wei – Water Goat
Metaphor Fruits on a golden tray. Wearing beautiful clothes travelling by boat.
Explanation Know how to treat people in a smooth way. Nothing to worry about. Successfully establish your career, rewarded to go home.

Heavenly Nature Star

Hour Pillar Gui You – Water Rooster Gui Hai – Water Pig
Metaphor Golden rooster welcoming the morning. Watching the moon over the clouds.
Explanation The best days are at hand. Difficulty in the present, hope in the future.

 

Destiny:
This is a life that is full of great knowledge and great ideas, combined with superb planning skills. You are very controversial. Failure and success are undetermined. You know how to be non-committal and how to handle situations. Fame and fortune are not easily attainable. Because you can chop down big trees, you can handle major matters, but because you can burn a frying pan, you are not so adept with minor matters. If you get something with great skill, and lose the same in a clumsy way, then you know it is destiny that overrides your effort. In Ox and Goat years you can enjoy fortune.

Luck Verses

Foundation:
Cannot meet the one in red, a benefactor.
Although the lotus rhizome grows in mud,
What emerges is a beautiful flower.
At the water concourse, noticed by someone helpful,
With such assistance, you can be successful.

Life Path:
Dig apart the stones to bring water from the stream to reach the sea.
In autumn, flowers blossom on the cinnamon tree.
When it’s time for your good luck you’ll have celebrity.
Because of good deeds, you’ll establish a good family.

Marriage:
From a grain of sand in the clam a pearl forms,
Into a dragon, with pearl in mouth, it transforms.
Who knows, dragon will be trapped in dry pond, unfortunately.
For the right time to enjoy romance and marriage, wait patiently.

Children:
Three branches with one fruit to bear.
Rat and Horse year born are superior.
The qi lin, a gift, heaven confers,
A wonderful son, with a great career,
Makes family famous and revered.

Harvest:
When should it be that you establish your life?
When you meet the Horse or the Ox, the time is right.
When you see youngsters dancing and singing in the water,
It’s time to enjoy the good music on horseback, thereafter.

 

Style: Flowers Withering in Late Autumn
When you have made a lot of money, in late autumn there will be losses.
When you expect success, there is danger.
Those flowers that can stand the cold are blossoming in late autumn.
Mandarin ducks who do not like the cold will fly away at midnight.
Patience is the right way to steer a middle course.
It is not right to make rapid decisions without good planning.
You have to imagine that your home is far away, many mountains away.
You have early success and everything has a pre-determined timetable.
