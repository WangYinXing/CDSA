Numeral 18    Yi Xin

Hexagram: Wind on Heaven

Stars

Heavenly Comfort Star

Hour Pillar Xin Chou – Metal Ox Xin Mao – Metal Rabbit
Metaphor Wolves and tigers eating. Yellow flowers in later years.
Explanation Lots of gourmet food to eat and fine clothing to wear. After raising your family and establishing a career you can enjoy a leisurely retirement. 

Heavenly Noble Star

Hour Pillar Xin Si – Metal Snake Xin Wei – Metal Goat
Metaphor Renovating an old shabby house. Holding a broken fan in late autumn.
Explanation With help from others you can bring your business to a second spring. Any ill-fortune you encounter in your later years proves to be harmless.

Heavenly Rafter Star

Hour Pillar Xin You  – Metal Rooster Xin Hai – Metal Pig
Metaphor Lighting a lamp on the rocks. Plum trees blossoming in the snow.
Explanation Whatever you do, it is full of style, also think about the substance. When you encounter misfortune, something miraculous happens.

Poetic Life Reading

Destiny:

In this life you have your ways and your plans. As a broad-minded character, you know what to look for and you know to look far. Encountering a lot of difficulties, you have to go through snow and frost, yet you come out of it fine. When you come across a noble person, do not expect much support. Relatives cannot help much. You start a lot more projects than you finish. It is better for you to marry a cousin. When your son marries, he is wrapped up in his new life, instead of gaining a daughter, you lose a son. It is better for you to be creative and not so conservative in your approach to doing things. The relationship with your siblings is not that great. You lose your parents quite early. Because you speak up all the time, it will offend people who will attack you due to jealousy. 

 

Luck Verses

Foundation: 
You want to pass exams, to have recognition.
But in Snake or Dog years, you’ll be unlucky.
When twice you overcome difficulties,
Then you will taste the sweetness of success.

Life Path:
The crops will grow by themselves in the field.
You will have success and be outstanding.
If you try hard to conquer the double mountain,
Wealth and good standing will be waiting.

Marriage:
When the phoenix calls, the goose flies away.
When a pair flies together, cold fog rises from the waves.
By the house in the west, flowers fall in the wind and rain.
A good, late marriage will be obtained.

Children:
Like a bear hunting away from home,
Work hard to raise your children, but you are not close.
Fortunately, you have a son so successful, he wears red robes.
Moon shines over the mulberry tree full of the scent of flowers.

Harvest:
How many times in your lifetime can you be really happy?
What is the answer, you do not know.
Reminisce about the happy times,
Looking out of south facing window.
When the Rooster crows it is time to go.

 

Style: Pearl at the Bottom of the Sea
When you take life too seriously, you hurt yourself.
Solitary goose flying in the wind is very lonesome.
Pair of mandarin ducks playing in the water, enjoy being in nature.
In the old house you are not so comfortable.
There are good things to eat when you have money.
What kind of things can you be proud of in your life? A pair of good fruits on the tree.
There will be lots of tricky traps on your path of life.
Whatever you dream about cannot give you real satisfaction.
After you have brought up your children you can benefit from children’s success.

Commentary

Destiny

To go through “snow and frost” means you have the ability to deal with adversity. You can have more achievement if you are self-reliant, innovative and persist with your goals.

Luck Verses

Foundation

Accept that there are times (especially in Snake or Dog years) when luck is not with you, and encourage yourself to try again.

Life Path

Once you have put in the effort you can reap the rewards (“crops will grow by themselves in the field”). If you are willing to face challenges (“climb to the top of the mountain”) you can have accomplishment.

Marriage

“When the phoenix calls, the goose flies away” implies that you tend to escape from those who chase you romantically. A phoenix and a goose are not the same type of bird, which suggests that it is not easy for you to find a perfect match and discover true love (“When a pair flies together, cold fog rises from the waves”). When you are ready to settle down, you might feel that many suitable partners are already taken (“By the house in the west, flowers fall in the wind and rain”). However, you can marry late and enjoy a good relationship.

Children (‘Children’ can also mean subordinates or followers)

“Like a bear hunting away from home,” suggests you work hard and as a result, you are apart from your children. This indicates you might not be close to your children, yet they can be independent and successful. To be able to “wear red robes” implies acquiring a high social status. “Moon shines over mulberry tree full of the scent of flowers”, means your children can be wealthy and powerful. Your followers will have achievement in their own right.

Harvest

The south-facing window is the sunny window. “Looking out of the south facing window,” means it is a good idea to look on the bright side of life. “When the Rooster crows it is time go,” suggests that you accept that life is full of new beginnings, and to let go of the old.

Style

“Solitary goose flying in the wind is very lonesome” suggests that you have fight some battles on your own. “Pair of mandarin ducks playing in the water, enjoy being in nature” implies that you can enjoy married life. “A pair of good fruits on the tree,” means you can have outstanding children (or followers) and be proud of their accomplishments.
