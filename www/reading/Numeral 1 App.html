Numeral 1    Jia Jia

Hexagram: Thunder on Thunder

Heavenly Noble Star

Year or Hour Pillar Jia Zi – Wood Rat Jia Yin – Wood Tiger 
Metaphor Fast wind, fast rain. Facing the moon in the wind.
Explanation Good luck is with you. There are always benefactors to help you. Difficulties in the beginning, getting better towards the end. You will have a fortunate old age.  

Golden Crane Star

Year or Hour Pillar Jia Chen – Wood Dragon Jia Wu – Wood Horse
Metaphor Collecting burning wood in the forest. The ox sleeping in the wilderness.
Explanation Born with a silver spoon in your mouth. Eventually, life turns out mediocre. You have great talents and great power, but unfortunately you have to fight alone. 

Golden Phoenix Star

Year or Hour Pillar Jia Shen – Wood Monkey Jia Xu – Wood Dog
Metaphor A man walking in the rain. Watching the moon coming out in the terrace.
Explanation With firm determination, you will get out of poverty. You have an uncompromising character that can enjoy peace of mind. There is not much worry throughout your life.

 

Destiny:
You are clever and ambitious, yet lonely. In many ways you are extra-ordinary. Whilst you may gain much knowledge, your achievement is limited. Studying by rote learning works well for you because you have an amazing power for memorizing information. Stepping into middle age, you will become more and more solitary. Nevertheless, in your forties you will meet a lot of helpful people. However, you can only gain social entertainment from your helpful friends rather than reap any financial rewards in business. Your life luck is such that big troubles will become small and small troubles will disappear. Just as well, since you are too hesitant in decision-making. Friends are easily lost and solitude becomes your enjoyment. With indecision as a weakness you miss a lot of opportunities. Although you are proud, you do not like to flatter others. You are open-minded and good causes are where you prefer to spend money. Nobody would describe you as miserly. With high aims in life, no one can stop you from doing what you truly want. Your children have their own life. Eventually, you will be an empty nester. What a pity, this is a semi-monk's life.

 

Luck Verses

Foundation: 
Your wits are so quick, as quick as lightning.
With a loud, clear voice the world finds you frightening.
Unable to settle down you go here and there, wandering.
In a huge, great world, but where are you heading?

Life Path:
Ready to shoot with your bow and arrow.
Killing deer, then presenting it to the wealthy.
Eventually gold and jade fill your abode.
Dragon and Tiger years turn out to be most lucky. 

Marriage:
At home together play musical instruments.
Enjoy life even in tough situations.
Amongst the trees of cinnamon,
In the back garden a wild bud blossoms. 

Children:
Spring blossom comes late.
Winds so strong, so great.
Still one red apricot, that’s alright,
It makes the back garden look so bright. 

Harvest:
On meeting Xin and Ren, stay put, stay for long.
It’s only suitable for enjoying music and song.
When the Rooster sings by the river, it’s time to dream.
Life is like flowers carried by the gurgling stream. 


Style: Distant Thunder Style
In the distant sky, lightning flashes and thunder sounds.
Wild winds blow from all directions, knowing no bounds.
As the clouds disperse, the loving birds ford the stream.
Flowers hit by wind and rain, some petals fall down.
From north to south, travelling in different lands,
Moving home all the time, like shifting sands.
What is the end of the story, may I ask?
Leading a child by a bamboo, is the task.Food and clothes are changing,
Finding love is like sifting,
Gold from sand, slowly by hand.
A person dressed in purple comes to your rescue.
