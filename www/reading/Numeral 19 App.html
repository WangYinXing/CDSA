Numeral 19    Yi Ren

Hexagram: Wind on Water

Stars

Heavenly Melon Star

Hour Pillar Ren Zi – Water Rat Ren Yin – Water Tiger
Metaphor Horse pulling the millstone. Large ship crossing the sea.
Explanation Avoid going too fast, it is better to go slowly to succeed. Your broadmindedness ensures a bright future.

Heavenly Copy Star

Hour Pillar Ren Chen – Water Dragon Ren Wu – Water Horse
Metaphor Germinating grass. Trying to fish by the water.
Explanation Cherish many small achievements. Ren and Gui years are the times to go after what you want.

Heavenly Pivot Star

Hour Pillar Ren Shen  – Water Monkey Ren Xu  – Water Dog
Metaphor A group of nightingales dashing for butterflies. Playing the lute under the willow.
Explanation You can enjoy romance throughout life. You can enjoy an easy life.

Poetic Life Reading

Destiny:

You are born under a judicial star that has power. It is a disciplinarian star which can make you stern. If you have this destiny, you can start an enterprise or inherit a business from your ancestors. Your character is such that you can be stern and you can be flexible. Misfortune and fortune arrive at your door at the same time. You have high ambitions that are firm.  Your nature is kind-hearted but your words can hurt. When you are laughing, it can turn into frowning. Whatever is sufficient becomes insufficient. All because you do not bow to authority, therefore, many a time you have to straighten out things. If you are patient and know your destiny then you will have enough all the time.


Luck Verses

Foundation: 
It is cloudy and dark, but no rain falls.
Thunder and lightning become very powerful.
At the entrance of the mountain, someone gives you an arrow 
Which helps you with accomplishments most of all.

Life Path:
When you look back, you already knew what was the plan.
In Ox and Goat year, you can expect things to happen.
When the moon comes out of eastern mountain,
And the red sun sets, then someone will give you a hand,
To succeed, in reaching paradise pond, in fairyland.

Marriage:
Mandarin ducks are flying into the lake.
At midnight the moon becomes bright,
Someone introduces you to your soul-mate,
It is pre-destined how the marriage works out.

Children:
Fruit ripens on the tree,
It falls and is washed away.
Due to your charitable deeds,
You’ll get a good son, eventually.

Harvest:
Throughout the year you work hard, work late.
Yet, at full moon, no funds for wine to celebrate.
There is no easy way to succeed,
Because many a missed opportunity.
Left behind, you listen to crows at midnight.

 

Style: Wild Goose Flies over the Sand Dunes
Dark clouds, but no rain, and the thunder is empty.
Good days come like a flash of lightning.
Wild goose flies over the wide river, leaving its hometown.
You can only see the pine tree standing upright in winter.
You have to leave home and leave your family to make a living.
A lot of flowers, but only one fruit.
During Dragon year you will ride a horse to heaven. 

 

Commentary

Destiny

You can come across as strict or demanding, even though your nature is much softer. If you have a more compromising approach, you can make life easier for yourself.

Luck Verses

Foundation

“It is cloudy and dark, but no rain falls”, means the calm before the storm, the quiet time before activities or difficulties begin. There is a lot happening when “Thunder and lightning become very powerful”. “At the entrance of the mountain, someone gives you an arrow”, means someone will give you something useful when you are facing a challenge.

Life Path

“When the moon comes out of eastern mountain, and the red sun sets, then someone will give you a hand” suggests that you can get the help just when you need it the most. Because you get support you will eventually succeed (“reach paradise pond, in fairyland).

Marriage

“At midnight the moon becomes bright” suggests that the timing for someone to introduce you to your soul mate is not too early or too late.

Children (‘Children’ can also mean subordinates or followers)

“Fruit ripens on the tree, It falls and is washed away,” suggests that you need to wait patiently for children (fruit) to arrive. For followers, you will eventually have good followers.

Harvest

In ancient China, people used to drink wine outdoors under the light of the full moon, as a monthly celebration. “At full moon, no funds for wine to celebrate” means you do not have a lot of spare cash. To succeed, you need to be aware of opportunities and to grab them with both hands.

Style

“Dark clouds, but no rain and the thunder is empty”, suggests that there is nothing much happening then all of a sudden you have good luck, “Good days come like a flash of lightning”. “A lot of flowers, but only one fruit”, suggests that you have more daughters (flowers) than sons (fruits), or more female followers than male followers. “During Dragon year you will ride a horse to heaven” means you can climb the ladder of success during a Dragon year.
