Numeral 31    Ding Jia 

Hexagram:  Earth on Thunder

 

Heavenly Power Star

Hour Pillar Jia Zi – Wood Rat Jia Yin – Wood Tiger
Metaphor Shadow of bamboo on a snowy day. Dragon entering snake’s lair.
Explanation Difficult at present, will be prosperous later. Going forward bravely, without looking back. Beware of back-stabbing.

 

Heavenly Balance Scale Star

Hour Pillar Jia Chen – Wood Dragon Jia Wu – Wood Horse
Metaphor Rain in long drought. Adorn and light a lamp.
Explanation Difficulty in the beginning, you can still amuse yourself. If you live humbly for a long time, everything should be fine. Having gone through all the dangerous and difficult situations, you will see success.

 

Heavenly Scalepan Star

Hour Pillar Jia Shen – Wood Monkey Jia Xu – Wood Dog
Metaphor Sailing in a boat against the current. Searching for gold in a torrential river.
Explanation Throughout your life you will encounter a lot of challenges. Be prepared to encounter dangerous barriers, and to manage your expectations of the returns.

 

Destiny:
This life is risky, but not exactly risky. What appears to be beautiful is not exactly beautiful. In the beginning you will be humiliated, but in the end you will be glorified. To begin with you will need to overcome many obstructions and you will get through them all.  Move from your hometown and leave your family behind.  Your approach to doing things lacks good planning, like picking fruit and flowers randomly as you come across them. You grow up as if fording a torrential stream.  You experience the bitterness of frost, with an unstable existence that is full of sudden rain and strong gusts of wind.  What appears to be proper becomes inappropriate, and may be considered evil. Peaceful situations become bothersome.  Even when you are quiet, your heart and mind are not at rest.  When your mind is at rest, your feet are busy moving.  The Post Horse star and Heavenly Void star shining together means that life should be leisurely but turns out to be hectic.

 

Luck Verses
Foundation: 
The willow bursts into blossom on the coast in the east.
Under night moon, with a clear mind, you watch waves at sea.
It seems that destiny is having a laugh, unfortunately,
There is always wind and frost when you ought to be lucky.
Wait till willow is bare, after all the leaves are gone, it will be re-born.

Life Path:
On the mountain with deep streams flowing,
The beautiful melodies you are playing,
Sound like the dragon’s splendid singing.
When called you shoot an arrow on horseback.
A virtuous person many find arousing,
Your triumphs have people applauding.

Marriage:
Like peach and willow blossoms scattered when windy,
Romantic relationships are blown apart.
Watch others happy with their sweetheart.
Wait for dark clouds to disperse and wind to cease,
Then once again, the bright sun you will see,
Fragrant orchid is in front of window, finally.

Children:
Living remotely with an orchid for company.
A hut in the wilderness with a bamboo fence,
The refreshing wind you can sense.
Fortunately, blossom is out on one plum tree.
In the west garden many peaches and plums,
Means you will have successful sons.

Harvest:
When going to the mountains, take care, the tiger can be scary.
Go bravely to make ancestors proud of the family name you carry.
Under the bright moon by the five lakes you can laugh with pride,
For your soul will be blessed by all the stars in the sky.

 

Style: Withered Branch Germinates in Spring
During spring all the plants have new branches.
What appears to be rotten wood also germinates.
The dignified tiger is roaring in front of the rocks.
Riding the horse on the path to prosperity is very appropriate.
Bulrushes burst in autumn, as overhead the wild geese fly south.
The nightingale is singing on the willow tree.
In the garden the peach and apricot are blossoming.
Such a charming family background, yet you leave it all behind.
You row away in a sampan looking for a brighter future.
