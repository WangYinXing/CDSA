Numeral 67    Geng Geng

Hexagram:  Lake on Lake

Stars

Heavenly Modest Star

Year or Hour Pillar Geng Zi – Metal Rat Geng Yin – Metal Tiger
Metaphor Duckweed under the rain. Waves chasing your boat.
Explanation Your whereabouts are unsettled, be cautious of risks. Be careful of various obstructions.

Heavenly Metropolis Star

Year or Hour Pillar Geng Chen – Metal Dragon Geng Wu - Metal Horse
Metaphor Snow, moon and plum blossom. Drinking in your own company in the rain.
Explanation You appreciate a quiet life and can become a solitary philosopher. In times of worry, you can still enjoy yourself.

Heavenly Head Star

Year or Hour Pillar Geng Shen – Metal Monkey Geng Xu – Metal Dog
Metaphor Going up the pavilion under the midnight moon. Playing the musical instrument under the moon.
Explanation Very good luck in later years. Happy throughout your life.

Poetic Life Reading

Destiny:

This life is like the orchid in the quiet valley or it is like the pine and cedar flourishing in the mountains. You cannot work together with your siblings. What is left by parents cannot be retained.  It is suitable to start own business or escape into the monastery.  Closeness with relatives is like the falling leaves in autumn wind. Friendships are like ice in rivers in spring. There are a lot of ups and downs in life. Fame and fortune comes and goes. To have political success is out of the question. Eventually you will be just somebody relaxing in the woods.

 

Luck Verses

Foundation:
Row, row, row your boat over the five lakes.
Go with the wind into rival countries.
Change jobs with different companies.
Face a lot of difficult situations, under pressure.
Because of your virtues can get near to the source of power.

Life Path:
Recollecting the days near Horse’s head and by side of Dragon,
When unexpected happiness came from heaven,
Your ability appreciated by a powerful person.
In your career, took you up to a high position,
Like a helping hand to climb up the mountain.

Marriage:
To play music with you, an elegant spouse is found.
Sadness doesn’t come from the beautiful sound.
For many years sail together in the high tide.
Who is appreciating beautiful musical notes with you?

Children:
Don’t complain about being lonely when old.
In late years, twin sons are coming, it is foretold.
Recollecting old family is sad, you sigh.
Drink and get drunk to enjoy life.

Harvest:
At times of Yin or Shen, in year of Bing Zi.
It’s easy to miss an opportunity.
Green mountains, blue waters still found,
Means opportunities are always around.
Go on board the boat for fishing,
And in the east wind you’re bathing.

 

Style: Spring Flowers and Autumn Leaves
Sail alone on five lakes in a sampan.
Go east, west, south, north commuting between countries.
You have brief happiness in spring when the flowers bloom.
By autumn you come back to reality but the trees are already bare.
Where you fish should be stable in the calm lake.
Wild goose is flying alone in the high sky.
You are a person struggling in the red dust of worldly life.
Why not brush away red dust and examine the Buddha’s picture?
Jump out of the chaotic worldly life and study Buddha’s thoughts. 

Commentary

Destiny

It would suit you to be independent of your family and to develop your own career (“like the orchid in the quiet valley or it is like the pine and cedar flourishing in the mountains”). It might also be a good idea for you to follow a religious or spiritual path (escape into the monastery).

Luck Verses

Foundation

“Row, row, row your boat over the five lakes, go with the wind to rival countries” means you go all over the place and work for different companies.

Life Path

“Recollecting the days near Horse’s head and by side of Dragon, when unexpected happiness came from heaven,” means good times in Horse years and Dragon years.

Marriage

A harmonious and lasting relationship with an attractive partner is predicted.

Children (‘Children’ can also mean subordinates or followers)

Twin sons are predicted and you will be an older parent. Let go of any unhappiness you might feel about your own family background. For followers, you can expect some loyal male followers.

Harvest

“At times of Yin or Shen,” means a Tiger (Yin) year or a Monkey (Shen) year. A Bing Zi year is a Fire Rat year. A Fire Rat year occurs once every 60 years, e.g. 1936,1996, 2056. “Go on board the boat for fishing, and in the east wind you’re bathing” implies that if you take up an opportunity you can prosper.

Style

You travel all over the place (“Sail alone on five lakes in a sampan”). “You have brief happiness in spring when the flowers bloom” implies you can enjoy success when you are young. You are unlikely to have the same achievement in your later years (“By autumn, you come back to reality but the trees are already bare,”). Therefore, it would be sensible for you to aim to make a steady living (“Where you fish should be stable in the calm lake”). When you face difficult times (“struggling with the red dust of worldly life”), consider seeking sanctuary in spiritual studies.
