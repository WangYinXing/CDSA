Numeral 89 Ren Ren

Hexagram: Water on Water

Heavenly Dim Star

Year or Hour Pillar Ren Zi – Water Rat Ren Yin – Water Tiger
Metaphor Polishing an iron rod to make a needle. Spring is in the pine forest.
Explanation Run business purposefully, eventually you’ll be successful. Mature quite early and be firmly determined.

Heavenly Weeping Star

Year or Hour Pillar Ren Chen – Water Dragon Ren Wu – Water Horse
Metaphor Watching the sky from inside the well. Sunflower in the pot facing the sun.
Explanation It is a good idea to manage your expectations when you do not have all the information. Helpful people will be there for you and there is no worry about making a living.

Heavenly Chronology Star

Year or Hour Pillar Ren Shen – Water Monkey Ren Xu – Water Dog
Metaphor Germinating plants decaying. Phoenix going out to meet companion.
Explanation A humble existence throughout life. Career depends on the help from your spouse.

 

Destiny:
This life is a burden to your parents. You should desert your brothers and sisters, as siblings will not be of much help. It is difficult for you to get a spouse and children. Therefore, you should seek a quiet and peaceful life and you should not plan for anything. In your one career, you will be rewarded twice. You work for one thing, but worry about twice as much.  Knowledgeable people admire you, but the ignoble people will be jealous. Because you show your wisdom when you are young, it brings difficulty and adversity in middle age.There are powerful people who employ you, so there is no need to fly all over to look for a career.
