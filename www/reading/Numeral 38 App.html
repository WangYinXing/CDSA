Numeral 38    Ding Xin

Hexagram: Earth on Heaven

Stars

Heavenly Imaginary Star

Hour Pillar Xin Chou – Metal Ox Xin Mao – Metal Rabbit
Metaphor Evening rain falls on the porch over the front door. Boating in shallow water.
Explanation Any misfortune at the moment does not really hurt. You need to be able to assess your own ability in order to get to the destination.

Heavenly Well Star

Hour Pillar Xin Si – Metal Snake Xin Wei – Metal Goat
Metaphor Excavating a well to look for spring water. Watching the sky from the bottom of the well.
Explanation If you work hard on your projects, eventually you will be rewarded. It is a good idea to manage your expectations when you do not have all the information.

Heavenly Ceremonial Star

Hour Pillar Xin You – Metal Rooster Xin Hai – Metal Pig
Metaphor Throwing a ball inside the tent. Rain in the drought
Explanation You are determined in dealing with obstructions. During your most difficult moments there is someone who comes to rescue you.

Poetic Life Reading

Destiny:

You are born with the ability to plan and organize. The foundation of your life is built on diligence. You are not afraid of hard work. Unfortunately, you are born with the Retreating Spirit, which means that when you are supposed to advance, there is no advancement. When you are supposed to be worried, you appear to be quite happy. In your early years you have to work hard.  You go through many pairs of shoes because of how far you have walked.  When you are supposed to be happy it ends up with you grumbling. When everything should be right, it becomes awful. Siblings become sworn enemies, like neighboring countries locked in a conflict, constantly at war. You even fall out with your parents. You do a lot of thinking in the morning, but you are laughing in the evening. Remember to keep your larder in your house well stocked with the seven essential ingredients (firewood, rice, oil, salt, soy sauce, vinegar and tea) for preparing a meal. If you want to be comfortable you need to change your career.

 

Luck Verses

Foundation: 
Worries and danger, no luck early on,
Just like when the clouds block the moon.
Go you must, a distant place, play your song,
To serve someone who likes your tune.

Life Path:
In Horse or Ox year meet someone, born in year of Jia or Bing.
Snake year’s difficult, so request from this person his blessing.
He is the one who can bring you a good position.
Then you’re like a phoenix flying over dangerous gate, no hesitation.

Marriage:
Wind blows fast with mild rain in cold spring.
Nightingale outside your door, a duet it won’t sing.
Young love, for you, no such thing.
Opportunities for marriage come in mid-life, your autumn.
In later years mandarin ducks play in clear pond, a twosome.

Children:
When you are right under the green pine tree,
You offer decaying flowers, with fallen petals, your sympathy.
Deep along the path lined with cinnamon and mulberry,
On the east wall there a fruit enjoying spring breeze.
One son comes late, he is extraordinary.

Harvest:
In Geng Shen year meeting Bing or Ji, opportunity.
In Snake year you hear a call from the south fairy.
Past events are like dreams that are empty.
As time flows, the cuckoo calls, it’s time to leave.

Style: The Moon Shining Over the Floating Cloud
When you encounter difficulties in life there is always a direction to go.
Everybody knows the floating cloud makes the bright moon dim.
When there are blossoms at midnight, it is better not to have wind.
Wild goose flying over the big river cannot even see its shadow.
Do not plant flowers in your old garden.
Instead go to a faraway land to plant the evergreen pine.
You should know that when you come to a well with spring water, one peach tree will be so glorious it makes the bare tree seem in bloom.
Like one tree making two trees appear red, what you achieve will be widely known.   

Commentary

Destiny

“You are born with the Retreating Spirit” means that your luck is such that you encounter setbacks when you want to move forwards. To keep your larder well stocked with the seven essential ingredients is a metaphor, reminding you about the importance of being well prepared.

Luck Verses

Foundation

“No luck early on, just like when clouds block the moon,” suggests you face difficulties when you are young. You can leave your hometown to move far away and find work. An employer (“someone who likes your tune”) values your skills.

Life Path

Someone who was in a Jia year was born in a year ending with digit 4, such as 1954, 1964, 1974, 1984, 1994, etc. Bing years are those ending with digit 6, such as 1956, 1966, 1976, 1986, 1996, etc. You can meet a helpful person who was born in a Jia year or a Bing year. Ask for support from this helpful person during a Snake year when you are facing difficulties and you can soar over barriers (“like a phoenix flying over dangerous gate”).

Marriage

You are unlikely to find true love and get married when you are young. “Wind blows fast with mild rain in cold spring” alludes to romance luck being difficult when you are young (springtime). You are likely to find a happy marriage in mid-life (autumn).

Children (‘Children’ can also mean subordinates or followers)

You can encounter difficulties when it comes to having children and you need to remain patient. Eventually, you will be fortunate (“path lined with cinnamon and mulberry”) and you will have an extraordinary son (“on the east wall there is a fruit enjoying spring breeze”). For followers, you will have capable followers, in the end.

Harvest

A Geng Shen year occurs every 60 years. Examples of Geng Shen years are 1920, 1980, 2040, etc. Bing years are those ending with digit 6, such as 1996, 2006, 2016, 2026 etc. Ji years end with digit 9, such as 1999, 2009, 2019, 2029 etc. “In Geng Shen year meeting Bing or Ji, opportunity” suggests that someone born in a Bing year or a Ji year could bring you an opportunity in a Geng Shen year. In a Snake year you are likely to leave the past behind you and move on in some way.

Style

You can have better luck when you move away from your hometown. You will gain a reputation for being successful.
