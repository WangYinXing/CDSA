Numeral 94    Gui Ding

Hexagram: Mountain on Earth

Large Tying Rope Star

Hour Pillar Ding Chou – Fire Ox Ding Mao – Fire Rabbit
Metaphor Dim moon is emitting virtual light. Polish the dusty iron mirror.
Explanation Even though you are bright and gracious, you need to manage your career expectations. Because you are determined in your ambitions you can bring pride to your family again.

Heavenly Gust Star

Hour Pillar Ding Si – Fire Snake Ding Wei – Fire Goat
Metaphor Dog and nightingale chasing deer. Duckweed floating on marsh in spring.
Explanation You fight for fame and fortune, but success is not easy. You drift aimlessly in early years, no direction in life.

Heavenly Travelling Star

Hour Pillar Ding You – Fire Rooster Ding Hai – Fire Pig
Metaphor Yellow chrysanthemums standing firmly in the frost. Riding the wind to come home.
Explanation Well disciplined. Even though there is misfortune, it is harmless. In later years, everything is to your satisfaction. Life is easy.

 

Destiny:
In this life you feel so tied up with worries, it is as if you cannot move.  Entangled in a web of love and hate, you waste a lot of energy over disagreements. You want to arrive at the destination, but the destination is too far away and you have wasted your energy and time. All your relatives are like burning charcoal placed on ice, they cannot help you. Children are like ephemeral flowers before your eyes. Beware of a traitor who is a blood relative. There will be fighting among siblings in the family. During middle age there are lots of ups and downs. A lot of barriers and misfortune bother you all the time. Work hard until your hair turns grey, after all hardship you can enjoy a stable and peaceful life.

 

Luck Verses

Foundation:
First half of your life you’re lost and aimless.
Efforts sink in a grass boat, achieved no purpose.
In time, gold is refined, jade is polished.
You visit emperor and empress in palace,
Later years, fame and fortune accomplished.

Life Path: 
Like the good craftsman busy carving,
In the end you’ve created something.
Rat and Ox years will be amazing.
A good name, Tiger and Dragon bring.

Marriage:
From behind the clouds, comes bright moon,
It is like the beautiful lady is on the moon.
Bless children by doing charitable deeds.
Best times are found in the evening clouds.

Children:
Branch about to blossom, flowers are ready,
Yet wind and rain makes them feel wary.
Remember about doing deeds for charity,
For it will help your children to succeed.

Harvest:
End of Snake and beginning of Horse is not the right time.
Don’t cross the bridge, don’t leave home before your prime.
On the empty boat in the wilderness, there’s nobody sailing.
Plum and willow are laughing at the flurries, laughing in spring.


Style: Wedding Mirror Being Polished
When the wedding mirror is covered with dust, it has lost its luster.
Re-polish it to make it bright again.
Hate coming out of love is already a fact.
How can the broken mirror become complete again?
Remember you met the noble person who was laughing.
And you followed the woodcutter into the forest in a bamboo raincoat.
Old pine tree does not change its habit of standing in rain and frost.
Even on the high mountain the new shoots are coming out.
