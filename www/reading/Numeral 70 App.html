Numeral 70    Geng Gui

Hexagram: Lake on Mountain

Stars

Heavenly General Star

Hour Pillar Gui Chou – Water Ox Gui Mao – Water Rabbit
Metaphor Big boat in the sea. Moon shining over cold pond.
Explanation You hold power in your hand, there will be a lot of achievement You live in humbly. Fortunately, there is someone to take care of you.

Heavenly Flying Star

Hour Pillar Gui Si – Water Snake Gui Wei – Water Goat
Metaphor Fishing in cold waters. Banquet among the flowers.
Explanation Even though you work hard, you need to manage your expectations when it comes to doing what is unfamiliar. Glory and wealth, fortune and longevity.

Heavenly Bow Star

Hour Pillar Gui You – Water Rooster Gui Hai – Water Pig
Metaphor Catch fish in net against the current. Digging the gold and getting the gold.
Explanation If you want to be successful, avoid being trapped in an adverse situation. What you get is what you want. Wealth luck is very bright.

Poetic Life Reading

Destiny:

In this life the Solitary Star is heavy and the Retreating Spirit is also heavy. It is most suitable to leave home and establish your own life, for you cannot rely on your parents to enjoy prosperity.  You need to make a living overcoming layers and layers of waves. Like an acrobat somersaulting over the pole at a hundred feet above ground, life can be challenging. Because your Four Pillars chart is firm, the foundation cannot be shaken.

Luck Verses

Foundation:
Phoenix pavilion and dragon tower in the capital city.
Come to visit these places, travelling a long way.
Step on the jade and gold, then re-located faraway.
When you look from the riverside, your heart is still in the capital.
You long to be back at headquarters, not in some outpost. 

Life Path:
The autumn scenery, even though still bright, is quite solitary.
You used to hold power to assist for the good of the country.
Alas, your services the emperor does not want to retain.
Near to the centre of power, you’re anxious to remain.
Yet, you have to take up a light carriage off to a new post.

Marriage:
Always in springtime the peach is blossoming.
By the riverside, mandarin ducks are meeting.
Under the bright autumn moon the shadows are clear.
Flocks of nightingales and swallows come to rest on apple trees here.

Children:
Fairy peach branch has half green, half yellow fruits hanging.
Flowers in two gardens are most fragrant in the evening.
There are two branches that are most flourishing,
Above the earth and under the sky, by the pond they’re great looking.

Harvest:
Rabbit is looking for dragon’s den, a lot of merits accumulating.
In south garden, orchid and cinnamon are flourishing.
It is a great pleasure to be with the flowers, drinking.
At the sound of the flute, towards the sandbank, heading.

 

Style: Nightingale Resting in the Wild Forest.
There is no need to measure the amount of gold in the house.
Let others comment on your strengths and weaknesses.
The lonely wild goose sleeps by the riverside.
There is an untied boat floating across the river.
The nightingale sings on the flowering branch in late spring.
People living in straw huts feel that days are getting longer and longer.
When you are asked about success and failure in the past,
There are a lot of beautiful songs singing farewell to the setting sun.

Commentary

Destiny

“In this life the Solitary Star is heavy and the Retreating Spirit is also heavy,” means that being independent is a good choice, and it would prevent others from holding you back. You can overcome various challenges.

Luck Verses

Foundation

You arrive at a prominent organisation (“phoenix pavilion and dragon tower in the capital city”) and gain a prestigious role (“step on jade and gold”). However, after a re-organisation you find yourself relocated out of the headquarters.

Life Path

“The autumn scenery, even though still bright, is quite solitary,” suggests that you are still in a promising position in your later years. Nevertheless, you would prefer to be close to the CEO/leader (“emperor”) and be in an authoritative position helping the company (“country”). Instead you take a journey elsewhere (“take up a light carriage”) for another post.

Marriage

A sincere, happy and long-lasting relationship is predicted. Nightingales are symbolic of romance and swallows represent domestic bliss.

Children (‘Children’ can also mean subordinates or followers)

Many good-looking children are predicted. For followers, you have many attractive followers.

Harvest

You enjoy a good retirement because of your hard work.

Style

“There is no need to measure the amount of gold in the house,” means you are comfortable financially. “The lonely wild goose sleeps by the riverside, “ implies that you like to go your own way without following the crowd. “There is an untied boat floating across the river,” suggests that there might be times when you may feel adrift. “The nightingale sings on the flowering branch in late spring,” infers that romance blossoms towards the end of your youth. “People living in straw huts feel that the days are getting longer and longer,” is not actually referring to other people, it means that when you do not have much to do, you feel somewhat bored. You are popular and a lot of people come to give you praise at the end of your life (“beautiful songs singing farewell to the setting sun”).
