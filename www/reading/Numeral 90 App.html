Numeral 90    Ren Gui

Hexagram: Water on Mountain

Stars

Heavenly Calamity Star

Hour Pillar Gui Chou – Water Ox Gui Mao – Water Rabbit
Metaphor Digging for treasure and finding gold. Dominating the dragon and suppressing the tiger.
Explanation Whatever you wish for, you get. Source of wealth is abundant. You are powerful enough to have people follow you.

Heavenly Seal Star

Hour Pillar Gui Si – Water Snake Gui Wei – Water Goat
Metaphor Clean as jade, clear as ice. Leisurely clouds flying over the mountain.
Explanation Even though you inherit a lot from your ancestors, it is difficult to last. Enjoy peace and leisure throughout your life.

Heavenly Regret Star

Hour Pillar Gui You – Water Rooster Gui Hai – Water Pig
Metaphor Duckweed floating in the rain. Hibiscus in flowerpot.
Explanation Keeping yourself busy, but beware of adversity. Beautiful, but not really practical.

Poetic Life Reading

Destiny:

In this life the five elements are well balanced and there is peace amongst the Four Pillars. Wealth is naturally at hand and the foundation is firm. You are famous amongst neighbors.  Your virtuous name is known to even the villages nearby. It is only because the star you were born under offends the Heavenly Calamity, therefore it is easy to get a hidden disease and wealth is easily lost. Throughout your life, you put up with gossip. Even though there are a lot of Retreating Spirits, eventually you will be rich and noble. 

 

Luck Verses

Foundation:
Attend to the orchard and forest, resulting in trees quite huge.
Take care of the trees, frost and dew they have to go through.
When one day great carpenter places big axe on huge tree,
To build a phoenix palace, it will bring great glory.

Life Path:
In a strange land, noble people come to ask for you.  
In a different land, there’s a reward of gold for you.
Double strange encounters, your wishes fulfilled for you.
By the waterside you will get good tidings, get good news.

Marriage:
Flowers bloom in driving rain and shocking shower.
Pair of mandarin ducks is startled by the thunder.
White apple blossoms, gorgeous flowers by the water,
Bright moon and cool breeze, long nights you enjoy under.

Children:
Cherry tree several flowers blooming.
When you are old, fruits are coming.
Bees and butterflies fly among the green and the yellow.
Fortune and wealth without resting, around you follow.

Harvest:
Riding Ox and Horse good luck bestowed.
Water Rooster will bring much sorrow.
Difficult to find a bosom friend,
Who appreciates high mountains, where streams flow.
After the rain, fisherman will be on the go.


Style: Flower and Willow Bring Glory Together.
There will be ten thousand miles of mountain from east to west.
Whatever was in the past is not right.
When the wind blows, wild goose leaves the flock.
When the rain comes the mandarin ducks fly alone.
The grass in spring blossoms, but difficult to have fruit.
In late autumn when all the leaves are gone, there is a fruit on the branch.
It is a beautiful scene with the setting sun.
Only worries are that next year the good times do not come.

Commentary

Destiny

“The five elements are well balanced and there is peace amongst the Four Pillars,” suggests that you have a fortunate astrology chart. “Retreating Spirits” means setbacks. Despite setbacks, you will have fame and fortune, in due course.

Luck Verses

Foundation

You build a worthy business and nurture it so that it becomes successful and prestigious (“phoenix palace”).

Life Path

You become sought after in foreign places and you prosper far from home.

Marriage

You encounter some storms in your relationship yet it is still a romantic picture (“white apple blossoms, gorgeous flowers”). “Bright moon and cool breeze” implies that there are many good times.

Children (‘Children’ can also mean subordinates or followers)

Children arrive late. “Bees and butterflies fly among the green and the yellow,” suggests that your children are successful; hardworking (“bees”) and sociable (“butterflies”).

Harvest

Ox years and Horse years bring good luck, whereas a Water Rooster year is not so fortunate. People come and go in your life.

Style

Be prepared to go your own way and to travel far. Do not expect too much when you are young (“The grass in spring blossoms, but difficult to have fruit”). In late years you get good results (“In late autumn when all the leaves are gone, there is a fruit on the branch”). You enjoy good times in your final years (“beautiful scene with the setting sun”).
