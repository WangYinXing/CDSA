Numeral 79 Xin Ren

Hexagram: Heaven on Water

Stars

Heavenly Seaweed Star

Hour Pillar Ren Zi – Water Rat Ren Yin – Water Tiger
Metaphor Playing the musical instrument inside the tent. Planting flowers in the valley.
Explanation Very clever and full of wise plans, therefore can have great achievement. Asking for fame and fortune. Success comes late.

Heavenly Awakening Star

Hour Pillar Ren Chen – Water Dragon Ren Wu – Water Horse
Metaphor Hiding gold under the roof. Polishing a worn out mirror.
Explanation Diligent throughout your life, yet you need to manage your expectations. The house becomes shabby when your circumstances are reduced, eventually you stand up again.

Heavenly Axe Star

Hour Pillar Ren Shen – Water Monkey Ren Xu – Water Dog
Metaphor Push cart along crooked lane. Broken boat carrying treasure.
Explanation Face trials and tribulations. Challenges come one after another. Even though you have wealth luck, be cautious.

Poetic Life Reading

Destiny:

The star of Three Penalties and the star of Seven Malicious Spirits shine on this life. To avoid misfortune it is very important to cultivate kindness in your heart, and to show this kindness to all living creatures. You are particularly suited to being a vegetarian and it would have the added benefit of making your appearance more attractive. Easy to get angry and easy to get happy is the essential nature of your character. It is suitable for you to leave your hometown and establish a new life.


Luck Verses

Foundation:
For the flowers on the mountain, it is finally raining,
Even though there’s spring light they aren’t talking.
When flowers open up, it is like they’re moving mouths chatting.
One day when the warm air comes back, when warm air returning.
Nightingales and flowers, the orchard and forest will be attracting.

Life Path:
Ride the Ox and Horse at times when it’s lucky
Middle age is better than years that came early.
Success and fortune due to own efforts purely,
Do the right things and everything will be easy.

Marriage:
When it’s a cloudless sky, from heaven there’s a beautiful light.
Coming out again from the clouds is the moon so bright.
Mandarin ducks fly away then return to re-unite.
In the pond, there are water fowls standing upright.

Children:
In late spring, one fruit left after all the blossom.
One branch bears ripe and unripe fruits in autumn.
Eventually, there will be a peach that ripens.
This one will be with the golden mother to toast her birthday celebrations.

Harvest:
Horse and Ox bring a lot of difficulties.
With the Dog will be crossing mountains in safety.
Like flowing water and the fallen petals in quiet late spring,
Seagulls and white pelicans pass time quietly.


Style: Moon and Venus Compete in Brightness
The moon and Venus are fighting for the bright name.
Pick up the saber, with hair flying down, looking very ferocious.
The lonely wild goose flying high in the sky does not join the flock.
In quiet forest the wolves are competing with each other.
You have ambitions to go to a distant land, but the timing is not right.
When you don’t have plans, the fortune will come.
Might as well forget about the past and live your old days in quietness and solitude.

Commentary

Destiny

“The star of Three Penalties and the star of Seven Malicious Spirits shine on this life,” implies that it is easy for you to encounter trouble. Therefore, it would be wise to create good karma, to lessen the effects of any problems, before they occur. It is a good idea to leave your hometown and establish a new life somewhere else.

Luck Verses

Foundation

Flowers “aren’t talking” refers to flowers, which have not opened, not bloomed, and it means that the time is not quite right. The time is right when the warm air returns for that is when the future appears bright (“Nightingales and flowers, the orchard and forest will be attracting”).

Life Path

Luck comes during Ox years and Horse years, or it can mean during luck pillars with Ox or Horse. Life gets better as you get older.

Marriage

It is predicted that a relationship will start off well (“cloudless sky, from heaven there’s a beautiful light”). You might hit difficulties as a couple, yet it will turn out well in the end (“moon so bright”). You have friends standing by (“water fowls standing upright”).

Children (‘Children’ can also mean subordinates or followers)

It is predicted that you will eventually have an outstanding child (“peach”). This wonderful child is very loving and will make a point of celebrating parents’ birthdays.

Harvest

There can be difficulties during Horse years or Ox years. Dog years tend to turn out well. Whatever is happening, you can choose to remain calm and relaxed.

Style

“The moon and Venus are fighting for a bright name,” implies that you find yourself in a competitive situation. You are spirited and want to fight, to compete (“Pick up the saber; with hair flying down, looking very ferocious”). “The lonely wild goose flying high in the sky does not join the flock,” suggests that you like to stand apart from the crowd. “In quiet forest the wolves are competing with each other,” means you face rivalry. You will succeed in the end.
