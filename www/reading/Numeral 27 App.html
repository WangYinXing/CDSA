Numeral 27    Bing Geng

Hexagram: Fire on Lake

Heavenly Beginning Star

Hour Pillar Geng Zi – Metal Rat Geng Yin – Metal Tiger
Metaphor Chrysanthemums on both sides of the path when you are looking for spring. Rotten lotus meeting the dew.
Explanation When you are determined there are plenty of opportunities. When you are in difficulty, someone comes to help you.

Heavenly Officer Star

Hour Pillar Geng Chen – Metal Dragon Geng Wu - Metal Horse
Metaphor Willow by the riverside in autumn. Waiting for the wind to hoist your sail.
Explanation Old age is adequate. Wait for the right timing to make your move and there are always profits.

Heavenly Handle Star

Hour Pillar Geng Shen – Metal Monkey Geng Xu – Metal Dog
Metaphor Petals blown away in the wind. Thick ice meeting the sun.
Explanation You have a shallow foundation, be careful of changes to your luck. You go through all kinds of difficulties and eventually you have successful days.

 

Destiny:
This life is unpredictable. Sometimes you are angry, sometimes you are happy. People cannot expect you to be even-tempered. When it comes to doing what is supposed to be an easy task, you manage to make a mess of it. When the fruit is already there, instead of just picking it, you still work hard for it. If there are no worries at present you don’t feel comfortable.  Despite a good foundation, you encounter a lot of trials and tribulations.  Somehow you invite people to attack you. Your spouse is vulnerable, and you will lose your companion more than once. A younger son will have achievement. Whatever plans you have are well made, but you need to do it all by yourself. In the beginning there is always difficulty, in the end it becomes easy.  Just as well that you don’t mind being busy.

 

Luck Verses

Foundation:
Developments and progress come late.
Whatever you inherit you have to relinquish.
Many a time you are cut off from your base.
In a strange land you will encounter the snake,
An unusual opportunity, an empire to establish.

Life Path:
A childhood spent in poverty,
Rat and Dragon years bring victory.
Lending you a hand are some powerful people,
Starting own business, make your house beautiful.

Marriage:
From the green swamp emerge two lotus flowers,
No need to decide in romantic matters.
Both companions are most helpful,
So enjoy a life quite beautiful.

Children:
When it comes to daughters, three become two.
Wait for winter winds for a son, who
Will bring much glory to the family.

Harvest:
In years of Dragon and Dog things turn out badly,
With Gui and Ding not much harmony.
Danger after danger come menacingly,
Even if no lawsuits, still loss of money.

 

Style: The Lone Wild Goose Flying in the Sky
It is not easy to make any progress.
You work hard in your early days.
Be careful of your business going sour.
When the wild goose flies over autumn water, the journey is not smooth. When it flies, it lands and stops a while then flies off again.
When you launch a business, you stop and start many times. 
In spring, there are flowers in the forest and spotted red petals on the ground. 
You can establish your career only when someone appreciates your ability. 
You will have to run a mile further until you reach the big mountain covered by white cloud.
Eventually, there is success waiting for you. 
Even when you have no tasks at hand you are still busy in your mind, for you still have to think about what to do next.
