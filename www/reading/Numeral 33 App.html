Numeral 33    Ding Bing

Hexagram:  Earth on Fire

Stars

Heavenly Painting Star

Hour Pillar Bing Zi – Fire Rat Bing Yin – Fire Tiger
Metaphor Sailing in the waves. Announcing the hour when the moon is at dawn.
Explanation After going through dangerous situations you will enter a beautiful land. In your later years, be wary of trying too hard. Maybe you can relax.

 

Heavenly Noble Star

Hour Pillar Bing Chen – Fire Dragon Bing Wu – Fire Horse
Metaphor Searching for plum in the snow. Sunflower in the snow.
Explanation Enjoy yourself in a hostile environment. Benefactors come to your aid. You know what to do at the right moment. Even though you may be of a humble background, your good social skills will get you far.

 

Heavenly Planning Star

Hour Pillar Bing Shen– Fire Monkey Bing Xu – Fire Dog
Metaphor Mouse losing tooth. Riding a horse over the cliff.
Explanation Planning may be deep and detailed, yet you still need to manage expectations. You bravely take risks to go forwards, but you need to be cautious about where you might end up.

Poetic Life Reading

Destiny:

This life is one which is full of good planning. You have good management skills and organizing ability.  Though you are careful in speech, your character is rigid and impatient. You are very thoughtful with everything. Many times you encounter bad luck of the Retreating Spirit, but you are not in real danger, even if the situation is risky.  Two leaves and three flowers then you can get the fruit means you need to do more work before you see the result. Copper bowl and iron brush means you must marry a hard-working spouse, someone with good endurance.  You will be wronged by ignoble people, and get yourself into trouble.  Do not make friends with disagreeable people. You cannot take over a family business.  It is important to find a golden friend who can help you turn an ordinary situation into something wondrous.

Commentary

Destiny

You are well organized and capable. The “Retreating Spirit” means setbacks. Even when you encounter setbacks they are usually not too serious. A truly wonderful friend can be a source of inspiration and support.

Luck Verses

Foundation

“No ox when you want to plough the field” means you do not have all the tools to do the job. You can be successful in Snake years or Tiger years, when progress is rapid (“Set sail with sail hoisted on the mast”).

Life Path

“Before moving, heavenly phoenix will open its wings” means there will be a signal when the right opportunity comes, so look out for it. You will encounter someone who will give you some wise advice (“Receive heavenly book from a nobleman you encounter”). “Meet someone who values your skills at dragon pavilion” implies that you can meet people in high places who value your abilities. “Jade and gold” alludes to gaining wealth.

Marriage

“Flowers will then bloom near your house, so sweetly” suggests that a sweet romance will blossom when you meet someone from far away.

Children (‘Children’ can also mean subordinates or followers)

You can have your own son and adopt another child. For followers, you can have successful followers.

Harvest

A Jia year is a year ending with the digit 4, such as 1994, 2004, 2014, 2024, etc. You will have good fortune in a Jia year or an Ox year. If you do charitable acts you will eventually be very successful.

Style

“If you want to smelt metal into gold you will need a furnace. In order to get something useful from different raw materials you need to change the furnace” means different resources require different techniques. If you want to see results you need to apply the appropriate techniques. “Capital city” can refer to the actual city or it can mean to go the headquarters of a large organization.
