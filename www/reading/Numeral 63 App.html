Numeral 63    Geng Bing

Hexagram:  Lake on Fire

Stars

Heavenly Dawn Star

Hour Pillar Bing Zi – Fire Rat Bing Yin – Fire Tiger
Metaphor Broken fan in autumn. Plum blossom in tenth moon.
Explanation After middle age be cautious. You have good luck in your early years, everything is fine.

Heavenly Chariot Star

Hour Pillar Bing Chen – Fire Dragon Bing Wu – Fire Horse
Metaphor Huge fish swimming in swamp. Huge tiger coming out of forest.
Explanation Even though you hold a modest position, you have great ability. The future is bright and the sky is the limit.

Heavenly Nobleman Star

Hour Pillar Bing Shen– Fire Monkey Bing Xu – Fire Dog
Metaphor Crossing the harbor in a sampan. When the flower is withering be careful of the frost.
Explanation You manage on your own throughout your life. Take good care of yourself in your late years.

Poetic Life Reading

Destiny:

This star indicates you are a person who is virtuous. Therefore, you can get close to noble people. Since your character is straightforward and your heart is kind, there is no way you would compromise with dishonorable people to agree to do something unethical.  Giving flattery to others is something you do not believe in.  You are very firm in the five virtues of benevolence, righteousness, propriety, wisdom and trust.  Your Four Pillars chart is peaceful and harmonious.  Picture a crane on a pine tree and the moon shining over a cool and calm pond, if you do good deeds you will live very long.

Commentary

Destiny

“Your Four Pillars chart is peaceful and harmonious” implies that you were born with a calm nature. The crane is a symbol of longevity and good fortune. If you create good karma with charitable acts you can enjoy a long and happy life.

Luck Verses

Foundation

“On lone pine tree, the crane is there calling” suggests that you have a lot of determination. You are ambitious yet you are not interested in gaining wealth, instead you seek knowledge.

Life Path

In ancient China, only the very wealthy could afford to paint their houses with “red door and green windows”. A lavish lifestyle does not suit your carefree nature therefore you “change the décor of your house to suit your needs”.

Marriage

As a couple, you enjoy a quiet domestic set-up. When you feel lonely, invite friends round for company.

Children (‘Children’ can also mean subordinates or followers)

“For good musical instruments spend a lot of money” refers to spending on your children’s education and not just on equipment. For followers, you train them well and they will be successful.

Harvest

Be cautious during Dragon and Dog years. If you feel troubled or have a lot on your mind it is important to relax (“Take it easy and imagine it is the shepherd that is singing”).

Style

“Wild goose is flying over the clouds to a faraway land,” suggests that you make a long journey. “Petals flying over stream making the water red,” means that the red petals fall into the water, and implies that some things do not last. At start of the Dragon year (Dragon’s head) or end of Tiger year (Tiger’s tail) you will hear good news.
