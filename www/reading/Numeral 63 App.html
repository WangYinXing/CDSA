Numeral 63    Geng Bing

Hexagram:  Lake on Fire

Heavenly Dawn Star

Hour Pillar Bing Zi – Fire Rat Bing Yin – Fire Tiger
Metaphor Broken fan in autumn. Plum blossom in tenth moon.
Explanation After middle age be cautious. You have good luck in your early years, everything is fine.

Heavenly Chariot Star

Hour Pillar Bing Chen – Fire Dragon Bing Wu – Fire Horse
Metaphor Huge fish swimming in swamp. Huge tiger coming out of forest.
Explanation Even though you hold a modest position, you have great ability. The future is bright and the sky is the limit.

Heavenly Nobleman Star

Hour Pillar Bing Shen– Fire Monkey Bing Xu – Fire Dog
Metaphor Crossing the harbor in a sampan. When the flower is withering be careful of the frost.
Explanation You manage on your own throughout your life. Take good care of yourself in your late years.

 

Destiny
This star indicates you are a person who is virtuous. Therefore, you can get close to noble people. Since your character is straightforward and your heart is kind, there is no way you would compromise with dishonorable people to agree to do something unethical.  Giving flattery to others is something you do not believe in.  You are very firm in the five virtues of benevolence, righteousness, propriety, wisdom and trust.  Your Four Pillars chart is peaceful and harmonious.  Picture a crane on a pine tree and the moon shining over a cool and calm pond, if you do good deeds you will live very long.

 

Luck Verses
Foundation:
On the lone pine tree, the crane is there calling.
You’ve great ambitions to reach the purple clouds flying.
White jade and yellow gold are not worthy treasures,
Writing with great skill and wisdom are finer pleasures.

Life Path:
Jade cloth, gold and pearls all before your eyes to see.
Red door and green windows not as it should be,
Occupant of opulent house is carefree like a fairy.
Change the décor of your house to suit your needs.
In gratitude to heaven, accumulate good deeds.
When wealthy, give to charity rather than enjoy luxury.

Marriage:
White clouds, right by your window sill, floating.
Husband and wife are quietly whispering,
Like a pair of swallows in distant mountain.
Enjoy a quiet married life, as time goes by feel lonely.
In the back garden lots of butterflies and bees,
Means many visitors come to keep you company.

Children:
For good musical instruments spend a lot of money.
Throughout the years willow twigs and peach roots keep you company.
Give your children a good education.
For that will build a strong foundation.
Outside they build careers that are amazing,
In late years pine trees and cinnamon will be flourishing.
Bring back a lot of grandchildren is the meaning.


Harvest:
Difficulties in Dragon and Dog years lots of wind and rain.
There is the bridge for the fairy, what does it really mean?
West mountain has bitter wind and unkind rain.
Hear the crane calling and the baboons screaming.
Take it easy and imagine it is the shepherd that is singing.
