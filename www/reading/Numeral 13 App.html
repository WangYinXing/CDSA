Numeral 13    Yi Bing

Hexagram:  Wind on Fire

 

Heavenly Blessing Star

Hour Pillar Bing Zi – Fire Rat Bing Yin – Fire Tiger
Metaphor Looking at the moon from the pavilion. Beehive filled with honey.
Explanation You can expect a perfect ending. Everything is smooth, you profit everywhere.

Heavenly Propriety Star

Hour Pillar Bing Chen – Fire Dragon Bing Wu – Fire Horse
Metaphor Horse neighing under the willow tree. Chrysanthemums in autumn.
Explanation When the time comes you can have the opportunity to do whatever you want. You are still strong in your later years.  

Heavenly Zen Star

Hour Pillar Bing Shen– Fire Monkey Bing Xu – Fire Dog
Metaphor Fireflies emitting light. Spring orchids and autumn chrysanthemums.
Explanation Even though you are weak you can have accomplishment. Good years throughout your life.

 

Destiny
A phoenix coming out of a group of chickens, and a dragon coming out of the snake’s belly, suggest you come from a modest background to have accomplishment. Even though you have a lot of good plans, you can never be really satisfied.  You are quite hesitant over many things.  When you are at your most comfortable, you create hardship for yourself. In a peaceful situation, you worry about gossip.  Even though you try your best, it is not easy for you to have good results. Flowers in full bloom hit by the night rain. Bright moon covered by passing clouds. Beware of unexpected bad luck during fortunate times. You are extravagant and like to spend money to keep up with others, even if you can barely afford it. If your house is made of straw, you will buy curtains made of jewels and pearls. You like to acquire things that look good, even though they might not be a good match to what you already have.


Luck Verses

Foundation:
Sun and moon both shine at the same time.
A virtuous character, you can
Meet the Emperor, after imperial exam,
In years of Ox or Dog, luck most timely.
Flying the flag of success eventually.

Life Path:
Goat year is the time to start on projects new.
Fame and fortune will come to you.
On reaching success, make a grand announcement.
Remember, of course, to give thanks to heaven.

Marriage:
The mandarin ducks are crossing their necks.
Bystanders admire your closeness as a couple.
Give thanks to ancestors for your good luck,
Share your glory with lots of friends.

Children:
The peach and plum trees are plentiful.
Don’t ask who will be most successful.
Your children will wear belts of gold with robes of purple,
That means they’ll gain power that is political.

Harvest:
Dragons make a lot of changes in lakes and rivers.
Tiger jumps over barriers to come back to the mountain.
When you are alone you can toast the moon and sun.
In the end you can become a hermit if you want.

 

Style: Sun and Moon Both Bright
Your career goes through a winding path.
You waste a lot of money.
When you take risks in business, glory will follow.
In the early days you hide behind the clouds.
In later days, flowers will be blossoming on your path.
One child will keep you company in old age.
