Numeral 86    Ren Ji

Hexagram: Water on Earth

Stars

Heavenly Revolutionary Star

Hour Pillar Ji Chou – Earth Ox Ji Mao – Wood Rabbit
Metaphor Becoming the full moon again. Clouds re-opens the stage for you to watch the moon.
Explanation Things can go against you. Destined to re-marry after being widowed. Evil in the beginning, fortunate in the end. Your wishes are fulfilled.

Heavenly Stabilizing Star

Hour Pillar Ji Si – Earth Snake Ji Wei – Earth Goat
Metaphor Blossom all over the tree. Riding a crane with a loaded belt.
Explanation Prosperous throughout entire life. Every venture is profitable. Your destiny is to be rich.

Heavenly Happiness Star

Hour Pillar Ji You – Earth Rooster Ji Hai – Earth Pig
Metaphor Swimming fish finds the way. Duckweed on a wide expanse of water.
Explanation Lively person and whatever you do there are no obstructions. It is hard to track you down. You can make money everywhere.

Poetic Life Reading

Destiny:

In this life you like to adorn your appearance and show off. You love to be in good company. With lofty ambitions, you are very clever. Unselfish in how you do things, your approach is straight.  You do not hide any secrets and can speak outright from your heart. Unethical people pretend to be sincere and loving to your face, but they will stab you behind your back. Yet you do not fear jealousy from such ignoble people. There is rescue in all misfortune because there are plenty of fortunate gods in this life.  There is imperfection in whatever you do.  Even when there is a good ending, there can still be some ugliness in process.

 

Luck Verses

Foundation: 
Love to help others gain, love to help them succeed.
Everything is done whole-heartedly, yet in the end it turns gossipy.
Blessed, due to doing many unexposed good deeds for charity.
Upstanding friends, like green pine and bamboo, keep you company.

Life Path:
Tiger and Horse years are not easy when you are elderly.
Someone lends you a helping hand to arrive at the path of celebrity.
Amuse yourself by the autumn river in times of difficulty.
To succeed, go on a chariot for ten thousand miles through mulberry trees.

Marriage:
It is lamentable that mandarin ducks are not playing in the pond.
In autumn, standing on the sand dunes, stand there lonely.
So you have birds and magpies to keep you company.
Water plants will be green along the river, green, green, greenery.

Children:
Flowering buds on plum tree blossom early,
The flowers, there are four or it could be three.
One branch remains in the snow with red flowers.
On the west wall, concubine has good flowers, good fruit.
Recollect how in the east, main mother, is to be paid tribute.

Harvest:
With good helping hands, you can turn snake into dragon.
What is supposed to be unsuccessful will turn out a triumph.
All because of a missed opportunity,
All you can do is listen to the bells when you are elderly.
If you took the chance, there would be a late harvest.

Style: Wild Geese Flying Over a City in the South
You have to fly over one layer of cloud after another.
You have to swim over one layer of water after another.
Fairyland will be there waiting for you.
Wild geese are leaving the marshland in autumn.
Mandarin ducks are chasing the morning breeze.
People have left when the moon is giving out its last glory at dawn.
When the cloud comes to the end of the path, there is no further path to go.
If you ask me about the future, there are flowers all over the tree elsewhere.

Commentary

Destiny

“There is rescue in all misfortune because there are plenty of fortunate gods,” means you have a lot of good luck, even when circumstances go against you. Although you might need to deal with some unpleasant situations, it works out well in the end.

Luck Verses

Foundation

You create good karma by not expecting any acknowledgement for charitable acts (“Blessed, due to doing many unexposed good deeds for charity”). You can rely on some good friends.

Life Path

You are able to remain calm (“amuse yourself by the autumn river”) during difficult times. To “go on a chariot for ten thousand miles through mulberry trees,” means you travel a long way and work hard in order to succeed.

Marriage

In middle age (“autumn”) you might be feeling lonely because your marriage has ended (“mandarin ducks are not playing in the pond”). However, you have friends (“birds and magpies”) and you can enjoy life (“water plants will be green along the river”).

Children (‘Children’ can also mean subordinates or followers)

Many good children are predicted. “Recollect how in the east, main mother is to be paid tribute,” suggests that someone who was not the birth mother of your children takes care of them. For followers, you can expect many loyal followers.

Harvest

“With good helping hands, you can turn snake into dragon,” means with the right support you turn a troublesome situation (“snake”) into something positive (“dragon”). If you miss an opportunity you might regret it when you are old.

Style

You find success (“fairyland”) after much effort. Be prepared to move on (“Wild geese are leaving the marshland in autumn”). You enjoy romantic times early on in your life (“mandarin ducks are chasing the morning breeze”). Success might not be where you expect it to be.
