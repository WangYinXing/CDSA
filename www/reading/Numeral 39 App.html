Numeral 39    Ding Ren

Hexagram: Earth on Water

Stars

Heavenly Record Star

Hour Pillar Ren Zi – Water Rat Ren Yin – Water Tiger
Metaphor Cicadas singing on willow tree. Good horse with golden saddle.
Explanation Someone protects you and you can have great achievement. You have a firm foundation and good opportunities.

Heavenly Tortoise Star

Hour Pillar Ren Chen – Water Dragon Ren Wu – Water Horse
Metaphor Watching the moon from the pavilion. Smelting the gold successfully.
Explanation You can have a very satisfactory result. Proud and arrogant, you are not bothered by others.

Heavenly Centipede Star

Hour Pillar Ren Shen – Water Monkey Ren Xu – Water Dog
Metaphor Trying to harvest lotus on the rocks. Nightingale coming out of the valley.
Explanation Diligent throughout life. There is a lack descendents. Jumping out of the risky situation, and climbing the social ladder all the time.

Poetic Life Reading

Destiny:

In life you are full of good planning, you are very careful, and can look into the depth of everything.  Your character is very blunt and straight. Since you are quite serious in your work, you often put yourself under pressure.  Added to that, you get little help from relatives. To begin with, a spouse and children turn out to be empty dreams. Just as a branch can have buds that blossom again, another marriage brings three daughters and you can generate an ordinary income to raise your family. Since working in your hometown is disadvantageous, it is suitable for you to make a change and not to follow the family profession. The hidden sickness in your body is difficult to cure. Even though the foundation of your life is stable, luck is not with you. Be content, wait for right time to come, then your hidden ability will be useful. Eventually, you will have good luck one day.  If good things come, what does it matter if they come late?

 

Luck Verses

Foundation: 
In the barn enough old grain handed down.
Best to make a new living, leave hometown.
Success depends on the help from outside
You’ll get real reward, heaven will provide. 

Life Path:
In Rat or Ox year, it’s time to advance.
Years of Horse and Ox fly to the nine skies.
When determined, dangers bring no worries.
If you’re kind-hearted, won’t have many enemies.

Marriage:
Mandarin ducks disturbed by shocking events from nowhere.
When happy, what can break a relationship? Beware!
In the end you are accompanied by many children
To the pine and bamboo forest, to enjoy retirement.

Children:
Children are vulnerable to wind and rain.
After the rainfall, no fruit on pine tree.
To bring fortune, do deeds for charity,
In old age your kids keep you company.

Harvest:
In Dog or Goat year prepare to go,
To cross fairy bridge no need a crane to ride.
Pine and bamboo thrive by the waterside.
Monkey and crane calling over nine skies.

 

Style: Rat in Old Barn
Rice in the old barn is more than enough for you to eat.
If half is eaten by rats, there is still plenty for you.
You are destined to be lonely therefore you should leave your hometown.
Being very clever, you do not need to study.
There are a lot of things on your mind bothering you.
You are not satisfied with your achievements.
In front of your garden two trees are blossoming, then you roll up the curtain to enjoy the beautiful scenery.

Commentary

Destiny

Even though you are good at making plans, you need to be patient and wait for when good luck turns up.

Luck Verses

Foundation

“In the barn enough old grain handed down” means what you inherit will be adequate for your needs. However, you can be more successful if you leave your hometown. “Heaven will provide” means you will have good luck.

Life Path

You can make good progress in a Rat year or an Ox year. Horse years and Ox years you can succeed (“fly to the nine skies”).

Marriage

Your marriage can come under pressure when you suddenly hit difficult times. “Pine and bamboo forest” are symbolic of endurance, therefore your relationship can last.

Children (‘Children’ can also mean subordinates or followers)

You can encounter difficulties when it comes to having children and you need to remain patient. Good karma from your charitable acts will mean that your children will be around you when you are old.

Harvest

There is a happy ending to your life. In a Dog year or a Goat you are likely to move to another place.

Style

“Rice in the old barn is more than enough for you to eat. If half is eaten by rats, there is still plenty for you” means if you lose half of your ample inheritance there is still enough. “In front of your garden two trees are blossoming, then you roll up the curtain to enjoy the beautiful scenery” implies that you can relax and appreciate what you have.
