Numeral 29    Bing Ren

Hexagram: Fire on Water

Heavenly Riches Star

Hour Pillar Ren Zi – Water Rat Ren Yin – Water Tiger
Metaphor Ox sleeping on lawn. You plant the trees and it becomes a forest.
Explanation Life will be steady and there is no lack of comfort. You can expect success in your career.

Heavenly Mansion Star

Hour Pillar Ren Chen – Water Dragon Ren Wu – Water Horse
Metaphor Nightingale in spring coming out from the valley. Broken wall in night rain.
Explanation Danger in beginning, safe in the end. You have a good retirement life. Despite difficulties, in the end it is safe.

Heavenly Calamity Star

Hour Pillar Ren Shen – Water Monkey Ren Xu – Water Dog
Metaphor Butterflies meeting flowers. Flying dragon in heaven.
Explanation When you are successful be careful of temptations to lead you the wrong way. Standing aloft, watching what is below, beware of danger.

 

Destiny:
The nature of this life is you are enthusiastic in whatever you do. You already have a good career, but you want to make a change and the change is not for the better. Fame and fortune are so near, yet so far. You want to do things and you do not want to do them. Your good friends eventually become your enemies. There is failure hidden in success. You wish to hold opportunity in your hand, but you have missed out. No matter how you plan, luck is not with you. It is like sailing a boat against a gust of wind or it is like trying to get the stubborn and disobedient donkey to grind the millstone. You do not need to worry about cold weather or times when there is not enough food.  You need to wait for the red faced nobleman star. Your laughter is half-hearted. If you want be successful away from your hometown, you have to be careful of the downfall of inherited business. In your late years you will be happy and can live an easy life because wind and frost came early in your life.

 

Luck Verses

Foundation: 
Inherited business brings many worries,
It is not worth a lot, whatever it may be.
If you have an opportunity to go overseas,
It is good to set sail to catch fishes.

Life Path:
To celebrate academic success, a banquet invitation.
Holding the horn of the ox you climb the ladder to heaven.
Heaven sends you two lucky blessings,
Accumulate good deeds for fame with fortune.

Marriage:
The moon comes out from behind the clouds at night.
Sun sets over the mountain, everything is quiet.
Crane is calling, to wake you up from your dreams.
With the wind and the rain, you cannot sleep.

Children:
Many flowers in the wind,
One fruit, so there is a son.
Do more good deeds, your son you’ll teach.
Making you happy, when old age you reach.

Harvest:
A prosperous retirement for you to enjoy.
In years of Tiger, Monkey, Rat or Horse, take a rest.
Retire to the suburbs where you find peace,
With monkeys and cranes for company.

 

Style: Pine Standing in the Mountain in the Cold
Willow blossoms are dancing freely.
Pine in the cold is accompanied by the bamboo.
You can make your living after many changes.
The relationship with your folks will not be smooth.
Play the chords on a musical instrument, playing a different melody in the wind.
Look at the beautiful peach blossom, while it is blossoming the leaves are green again.
