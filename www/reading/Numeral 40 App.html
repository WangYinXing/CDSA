Numeral 40    Ding Gui

Hexagram: Earth on Mountain

Heavenly Chef Star

Hour Pillar Gui Chou – Water Ox Gui Mao – Water Rabbit
Metaphor Golden plate full of fruit. Big river running into the sea.
Explanation Fame and fortune just comes. Your ambition spreads far and wide. There is a lot of room for development.

Heavenly Gallop Star

Hour Pillar Gui Si – Water Snake Gui Wei – Water Goat
Metaphor Watching the moon from the pavilion. Throw your fishing line into a school of fish.
Explanation A bright and brilliant future, your success has no limits. When luck comes, profit will be immeasurable.

Heavenly Academic Award Star

Hour Pillar Gui You – Water Rooster Gui Hai – Water Pig
Metaphor Watching the moon with clouds as your clothes. Fishing over cold pond.
Explanation The good days are right in front of you. Even though you work hard, you need to manage your expectations when it comes to doing what is unfamiliar.

 

Destiny:
This life is like the floating cloud coming over the mountain. The wild crane is calling in the wind. Your mind is everywhere and your ambition is elsewhere. You will not be successful by depending on your ancestors, but you can establish yourself by leaving your hometown. Wealth is like spring water in the well, it is auspicious. Yet your life is like a candle in the wind. Two or three times you change in social status. Starting from scratch, you re-establish your family four or five times.  As a layman you are not lonely.  In disastrous situations there is the Rescuing Spirit following you and you can seek sanctuary in a monastery. Becoming a monk is not the final outcome. Leaving the monastery to revert to a life of a layman, there is the Retreating Spirit to bother you.  Destiny has your spouse and children leaving you. Siblings have to part from you. When luck is not with you, you have to use your energy.  The best way is to walk on leisurely ground, but destiny has you falling into the fire pit.


Luck Verses

Foundation: 
Like the sea takes a shape, in mid-life things are clearer.
Bright moon in clear sky gets brighter.
In Goat and Dog years you can be accomplished,
Get ready for a distant land, go to get established.

Life Path:
On peach tree, pine nuts are growing.
Ox and Goat years bring good earnings.
Good opportunity within high society, with the finest.
Wear golden belt with purple robe in maple forest.

Marriage:
Water is flooding the pond on spring day,
In clear waters, the mandarin ducks play.
Bump into admirers, mess up your life.
In the end, can still enjoy married life.

Children:
Outside your wall, three fruits are hanging.
Flowers rot, one fruit comes out of the mountain.
Children have own lives that are successful.
Thank heavens above, you should be grateful.

Harvest:
In the end you’re such a happy visitor on earth.
Meeting Rat and Horse, it’s time to return north.
Musician has gone, all quiet on swallow terrace.
Looking in the mirror there’s nothing to witness.

 

Style: When the Clouds Disperse You See the Moon
Water is cold, there is no fish but you are sitting on fishing pier.
Black clouds disperse, so you can see the blue sky.
Red dust is carried away by the wind then you can see the green bamboo under the moon.
You take out a sword from your shelf then you show the world you have ability to serve your country.
Unfortunately, in the past you have been using your ability in the wrong way and consider those who appreciated your potential to be dull.
