Numeral 72   Xin Yi

Hexagram: Heaven on Wind

Stars

Heavenly Hot and Brilliant Star

Hour Pillar Yi Chou – Wood Ox Yi Mao – Wood Rabbit
Metaphor Orderly clouds reflecting daylight. Listening to music in the opera house.
Explanation There is a path leading up to the blue sky. You can name the day when you will get promoted. Moments of sadness and moments of happiness. It is unpredictable when you gain and when you lose.

Heavenly Rejection Star

Hour Pillar Yi Si – Wood Snake Yi Wei – Wood Goat
Metaphor Planting flowers on the rocks. Fishing against the current.
Explanation Even though you work hard to do business, you need to manage your expectations. What you plan might not work out when you encounter an adverse environment.

Heavenly Revolutionary Star

Hour Pillar Yi You – Wood Rooster Yi Hai – Wood Pig
Metaphors Draw a mark on your boat to identify the spot where there is a lot of fish, so you know where to come back to fish again. When you want to cut wood, you are given the axe.
Explanation Avoid wasting your energy, with ideas that don’t work. You get whatever you want and whatever you do will be doubly rewarded.

Poetic Life Reading

Destiny:

In this life it is better for you to be independent than to depend on your ancestors. Relatives turn a cold face towards you. There is also a lack of brotherly love.  Colleagues do not offer much help. You rely on your own two hands to make a living. You do things all by yourself and single-handedly build things up. Fortunately, you are capable of doing many things and are able to think and ponder about situations. It appears that your plans can satisfy your ambitions, but resources sometimes flourish and sometimes wither.


Luck Verses

Foundation:
Spring and autumn have gone, but matters are not settled.
Look to the future, the cycle of nature to be followed.
After big changes, there comes opportunity,
When no need to wait for people to be helpful,
When no need to work over-diligently.

Life Path:
When you slip several times, depend on others to give you a hand.
For good results start a new business, old ones you must disband.
When looking back, flourishing mulberry trees you’ll see.
Good tidings from heaven reach phoenix pavilion, find success eventually.

Marriage:
Pair of mandarin ducks, swim in clear pond.
With two spouses, you’ll make more than one bond.
Fallen petals after spring, many affairs they bring.
When it’s over, don’t stay, lovers simply fly away.

Children:
Flowers blossom, but no fruit, until one comes very late.
This one, most carefully, you cultivate.
If you do good deeds for charity,
Good children around you when you’re elderly.

Harvest:
When you meet the Dog, it’s a topsy-turvy year.
From Tiger to Snake, what is most shocking?
In these four years such a lot’s happening.
It depends on a good doctor to find a cure,
For then you can escape from any danger.


Style: Twin Fish Playing in Water
You are very ambitious but you have to wait.
Ducks and geese fly in different directions over the misty water.
In the old garden after the rain at night there is new grass.
The new tree standing in the spring wind brings new branches.
From the old days, you have come to know how good your horse is when the journey is really long.
From now on everything is stable and life is emerging.
There is someone who can understand and appreciate your ability, and you can forget about the dark old days.

Commentary

Destiny

It suits you to be self-reliant. You have the wherewithal to reach your goals with whatever you have at your disposal.

Luck Verses

Foundation

“Spring and autumn have gone, but matters are not settled,” suggests that you need to persevere to fulfil your ambitions. You will be able to take hold of an opportunity that you can manage by yourself.

Life Path

When you encounter setbacks (“slip several times”), you can rely on others to help you. You will be successful in the end.

Marriage

You are likely to have more than one marriage, and many love affairs.

Children (‘Children’ can also mean subordinates or followers)

“Flowers blossom” suggests daughters, and later on a son (fruit). If you create good karma by being charitable, it helps your children. For followers, you are likely to have more female followers.

Harvest

Dog years might turn out to be chaotic. “From Tiger to Snake,” means the four consecutive years of Tiger, Rabbit, Dragon and Snake. If you feel unwell during these years, it’s important to seek medical attention promptly.

Style

“Ducks and geese fly in different directions over the misty water,” implies you need to get clear on where you are going. There is hope after overcoming difficulties (“in the old garden after the rain at night there is new grass”). “The new tree standing in the spring wind brings new branches,” suggests that a new venture can develop well. “From the old days, you have come to know how good your horse is when the journey is really long,” means you remain determined and keep going.
