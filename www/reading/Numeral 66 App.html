Numeral 66    Geng Ji

Hexagram: Lake on Earth

Heavenly Monitor Star

Hour Pillar Ji Chou – Earth Ox Ji Mao – Wood Rabbit
Metaphor Crow on bare tree. Clouds returning to empty sky.
Explanation Ancestral business is not something you can rely on, it is on its last legs. Make your own way in the world. Diligent for your entire life, need to manage your expectations at the end of the day.

Heavenly Scalepan Star 

Hour Pillar Ji Si – Earth Snake Ji Wei – Earth Goat
Metaphor Flying locust in the paddy fields. Appreciating flowers in spring.
Explanation Beware of underhanded people playing tricks behind your back. Good luck in early years, however, be cautious for it cannot be guaranteed to continue.

Heavenly Measurement Scale Star 

Hour Pillar Ji You – Earth Rooster Ji Hai – Earth Pig
Metaphor Small stream flowing into the sea. Fishing over cold pond.
Explanation You have some capability and can have some achievement. Even though you work hard, you need to manage your expectations when it comes to doing what is unfamiliar.

 

Destiny:
In this life you are born clever, frank and open.  There is early training in dealing with life that comes from overcoming challenges. When it comes to finding out about making necessary changes, it might be too late.  Relatives turn a cold face to you, as if there is snow even in late spring, when you expect it to be warm. Although you are lucky enough to have children and grandchildren your relationship with them is not that intimate, they are like the image of flowers in mirror.  You may feel there is a lack of real sentiment. Nothing substantial can be accomplished because your mind is often undecided on whether to go forwards or back. You can be wasting time on contemplating too much; that is your destiny.


Luck Verses

Foundation: 
Don’t blame people when their love for you is not durable.
Several layers of fame and fortune make you admirable.
Years of Goat, Dog, Horse, laugh and point at mulberry tree,
At these good times your family name carries much glory. 

Life Path:
Make good use of what you have to elevate your family.
In Rooster and Dog years name spreads far and wide.
Praise from music classroom flows freely outside.
Feel happy under bright moon with cool breeze beside.

Marriage:
Pairs of mandarin ducks in big pond playing.
Water fowl all flock together in the evening.
Such a merry social life, very entertaining,
At home under bright moon, cool breeze blowing.

Children:
After the rain, wind is quiet, fruits find it frightening.
Strange flower brings heavenly peach in evening,
Means in your later years, baby son will be arriving.
Banquet in gold and jade hall, children dancing and singing.
Later, poetry and propriety, lots of descendants are learning.

Harvest:
Retire up the mountain at times of Tiger or Monkey.
Appreciate the beauty of the scenery in the valley.
Crowing bird arouses you from your dreams,
Baboon and crane want to go, yet find it’s not easy.
Realize awake from dream, time to go back to reality.

 

Style: Solitary Wild Goose Carrying the Letter
Reflection of water in well distorts the stars in the sky.
For half of your life your wealth and worldly possessions are not satisfactory.
Mandarin ducks are too lazy to dance under the midnight moon.
Willow tree sees sadness at midnight in autumn.
Solitary wild goose brings a lot of melancholy.
Good days of achievement come late.      
The noble people, bringing you success, will come from Kun and Li palaces,
Leading the way to go, to the white jade pavilion together.
