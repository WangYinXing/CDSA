Numeral 57    Ji Geng

Hexagram:  Earth on Lake

 

Stars

Heavenly Arrogance Star 

Hour Pillar Geng Zi – Metal Rat Geng Yin – Metal Tiger
Metaphor Beautiful bright spring. Shooting a swallow in quiet forest.
Explanation You have great achievement when you are young. Be cautious for it can all change in the blink of an eye. Avoid taking the blame when luck is not with you.

Heavenly Opening Star

Hour Pillar Geng Chen – Metal Dragon Geng Wu - Metal Horse
Metaphor Trapped dragon walking in the rain. Rocky spring under the rain.
Explanation After being trapped for a long time you achieve something great. The timing is right. It will be advantageous for all moves.

Heavenly Jade Stamp Star

Hour Pillar Geng Shen – Metal Monkey Geng Xu – Metal Dog
Metaphor Nightingale in spring, swallow in summer. Ancient moss on the rock by the stream.
Explanation Enjoy romance throughout your life. Luck is good when you are old. Your name is remembered forever.

Poetic Life Reading

Destiny:

This is a person who is like a tall tree that stands upright. Your spirit is like the lonely moon in the sky. You are sincere about everything and your character is honorable. There is a lack of harmony among your siblings and these are not good relationships. Being enthusiastic in everything you do, creates gossip around you. When others take advantage of you, it makes you angry. When you are true to others it doesn’t work out. Minding other people’s business will only bring you worries. Whatever has a good result in the end must encounter a lot of difficulties along the way. Everything that you start is filled with enthusiasm, but you lack stamina to see things through to the end. When you are supposed to be leisurely, your mind is very busy. When the situation appears to be calm you are worried deep inside your heart. Don’t blame destiny that luck is not always with you.

Commentary

Destiny

“Your spirit is like the lonely moon in the sky” suggests that you would rather stand alone than go along with something which you do not feel is right. Some things will work out, but not all the time. There is no point in complaining when luck is not with you.

Luck Verses

Foundation

“Like green pine tree and cedar you stand gracefully,” suggests that you have the determination to succeed. “More than enough land for growing crops, plant some mulberry and other trees,” means you are prosperous. Mulberry trees were only planted on spare land, after enough crops were planted for food.

Life Path

“Dawn of a good era, every Rooster year,” implies that you enjoy fortunate times every Rooster year. You also enjoy good luck in your later years (“from the right direction wind does blow”). To reach “the top of the forest” means you reach the top of your profession.

Marriage

When a relationship breaks up (“mandarin ducks fly apart”), there are other romantic opportunities. You might as well make the most of the interest from admirers and enjoy yourself (“under bright moon and cool breeze”).

Children (‘Children’ can also mean subordinates or followers)

Doing charitable deeds generates good karma, which helps your children (“fruits”) overcome difficulties (“wind is strong and rain is heavy”). To be like “dragon and phoenix flying to heaven” means your children will succeed. For followers, you can attract successful followers.

Harvest

You can settle in pleasant surroundings. “Fisherman rows boat through fairyland of peach trees” implies that you spend your last days in a wonderful setting.

Style

“This life is born without luck,” suggests that you did not start your life in fortunate circumstances (“wind is hitting the lotus flowers”). “In the Li palace fire is so strong wood is burned to charcoal,” means beware of burning yourself out in your prime. “In Dui palace metal grows and takes root” implies that luck will be with you in your later years. “You worry about the red petals flying in the wind” alludes to the red lotus and the possibility of a relationship breaking apart. “Unfortunately, there is no fruit you can harvest” indicates that it would be difficult for you to save a troubled romance.
