Numeral 10    Jia Gui

Hexagram: Thunder on Mountain

Stars

Heavenly Fortune Star

Hour Pillar Gui Chou – Water Ox Gui Mao – Water Rabbit
Metaphor Willow blossoms accompanied by baboon. Orchid in spring, chrysanthemum in autumn.
Explanation You are not decisive. Fortunately there is something or someone you can rely on. You will encounter wonderful situations throughout life.

Heavenly Prosperity Star

Hour Pillar Gui Si – Water Snake Gui Wei – Water Goat
Metaphor Decayed wood waiting for spring. Yellow flowers in autumn.
Explanation Need to wait for the right time to come. You have wonderful luck when you are old.

Heavenly Noble Star

Hour Pillar Gui You  – Water Rooster Gui Hai  – Water Pig
Metaphor Fish escaping from your net in the pond. Bamboo sticks turn into dragons.
Explanation Disasters have gone by, misfortune has turned into fortune. With lots of good planning, you are able to transform and re-invent yourself repeatedly.

Poetic Life Reading

Destiny:

This life enjoys all the good things from the five elements. Your good fortune comes from everything being right. Your powerful words like thunder, which can arouse somebody from their dreams.  With vital energy that is strong and flexible, you have many ideas and make plans with a lot of depth. Although you learn a lot of different subjects, you are not an expert in any of them. At the start you are full of enthusiasm and great at beginning new things, but you do not finish everything.  Whatever you touch, you have to use your mind.  Even though you do not mean to, your impatient words can hurt and offend people. You have a kind heart and are well meaning, yet it brings nothing but enemies and complaints.  Your spouse has a resilient constitution and can withstand illness. Retirement will start in Tiger year or Rat year.

Commentary

Destiny

The five elements is a way to refer to everything on earth. This is because in Chinese philosophy, everything on earth is classified according to the five elements of wood, fire, earth, metal and water. “All the good things from the five elements” means you can enjoy all the good things on earth. Being careful in your speech can help you avoid problems.

Luck Verses

Foundation

Usually, you make good plans, yet they are often quite difficult. You pursue a career, which requires a specialised skill and involves travelling.

Life Path

During your travels you can meet an inspiring woman close to somewhere near water. Ox years and Horse years will be particularly lucky.

Marriage

A long and happy marriage is predicted.

Children (‘Children’ can also mean subordinates or followers)

“One outside the wall” alludes to the possibility of a child being born outside of the main family. It could mean a step-child from another relationship, or a child from an affair. For followers, you could attract those who had previously been in another camp.

Harvest

You can be a successful leader (“to hold the flag”). You end your days away from your hometown.

Style

You have eventful life, so time flies by. When you face challenges (“under strong winds”), you can count on your superiors, but watch out for your subordinates.
