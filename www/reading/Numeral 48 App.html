Numeral 48    Wu Xin

Hexagram: Mountain on Heaven

 

Heavenly Servant Star

Hour Pillar Xin Chou – Metal Ox Xin Mao – Metal Rabbit
Metaphor Watching the moon in the silent night. Birds in winter make a lot of noise on a sunny day.
Explanation In your late years, watch out for any signs of melancholy. Difficulty is in the past and life is getting better everyday.

Heavenly Gloomy Star

Hour Pillar Xin Si – Metal Snake Xin Wei – Metal Goat
Metaphor Riding a horse at the edge of a cliff. Playing the musical instrument in the urn.
Explanation Be cautious about taking any risks. Avoid wasting time when you are doing something without being noticed.

Heavenly Dim Star

Hour Pillar Xin You – Metal Rooster Xin Hai – Metal Pig
Metaphor Boating in stormy seas. Mirror covered in dust.
Explanation Beware of any risks you face. Calamity star has not retreated. Be prepared for uncertainties.

 

Destiny:
In this life there is often a lot on your mind, not everything will be successful. Therefore, you need to accept that some things will work out a lot better than others.  Relatives cannot be relied upon, and siblings lack fondness towards you. The early roots and foundation are limited. Laziness prevents early academic success. When the Heavenly Gloomy star and the Heavenly Dim star come, there is hidden illness that cannot be revealed. You can shine as a pioneer, moving back and forth, in order to get rid of the old system and to bring in a revolutionary new order. Do not expect much help from friends and be prepared for relatives to bring you worries. This is all because of the difficulties brought by frosty winds early in your life. Trust that there is someone who can decide everything in your life.


Luck Verses

Foundation: 
Fire logs on your shoulder, on a risky path into deep forest,
You see gold when you’re halfway there.
It doesn’t run smooth when you try your hardest.
To pursue the deer, go easy, they’re just there.

Life Path:
Not been loving throughout life is a cause for regret.
A dead tree to glorify in spring, how can you expect?
Always found on autumn river, are those who understand.
See the important waterway in year of Dog or of Dragon.

Marriage:
The mirror in your hand is obviously broken.
Although it can be repaired, won’t happen.
A past life means marriage is pre-destined.
No need to be bitter about the outcome.

Children:
In mid-spring one flower is trying to blossom.
Unfortunately, you have to travel by boat in a choppy sea.
You have been working hard, so heaven offers sympathy,
And bestows upon you a son, to serve you when you’re elderly.

Harvest:
You cannot go back to the five lakes.
In your late years, one fruit to serve you nicely.
It is your fate when year of Dragon or Snake,
Collapse by the river, end of the story.


Style: Purple Swallow Building a Nest
High ambition in a character that is very stern.
Whether it is bitter or sweet you still manage to run the business.
Purple swallow builds a nest elsewhere.
Wild geese cannot stand together.
You tell the truth and invite condemnation.
Your children cannot be of much help.
You are admitted through the golden door, even though you weren’t meant to be, because of the carelessness of those in charge.
