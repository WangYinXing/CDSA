Numeral 95    Gui Wu

Hexagram: Mountain on Mountain

Heavenly Dim Star

Hour Pillar Wu Zi – Earth Rat Wu Yin – Earth Tiger
Metaphor Road built level ground. Boating in stormy seas.
Explanation No danger throughout life. Overcome a lot of difficulties before you become successful.

Heavenly Power Star

Hour Pillar Wu Chen – Earth Dragon Wu Wu – Earth Horse
Metaphor Crane resting on tall pine tree. Fish in well swims to the sea.
Explanation When you are on a lofty position, you can show your great talents. After being trapped for a long time, there is much development.

Heavenly Noble Star

Hour Pillar Wu Shen – Earth Monkey Wu Xu – Earth Dog
Metaphor Hibiscus on an autumn day. Wandering bees making honeycombs.
Explanation No worries after middle age. Travel east and west to make good profits.

 

Destiny
This life is one where making a living starts early and retiring comes late. You will be rowing a sampan alone in the wind and waves. Encountering the wild fierce tiger in the mountain at night, in an embarrassing situation you can get away.  You know when to advance and when to retreat. You have the intention to complete things successfully, but you can also kill something with your words. There is no need to worry about minor hassles and major troubles will be removed.  What is a pity is that your relatives are like water, tasteless, and they become competitors. Do not complain about such unloving situations because it is your life.


Luck Verses

Foundation:
Work hard along the winding path for fame and fortune.
Boundless view of breeze and moon with fragrance of cinnamon,
When you go forward, you’ll see vibrant mulberry and beautiful chrysanthemum.
The scenery in the fields is very unusual, very uncommon.

Life Path:
In the olden days nothing was steady,
A lot of effort to bring up your family.
Goat and Dog years be understanding,
With your son, climb up success mountain.

Marriage:
Marriage is like testing the waters, is it deep or is it shallow?
Marriage is like the moon, sometimes new, sometimes full.
In the end, fame and fortune bring you luck, you’ll be lucky.
Friends come to celebrate, enjoy lots of good company.

Children:
Wonderful scene, peach is fragrant.
Fruits on the branch, they will ripen.
Support you give them is important,
All your children have great achievement.

Harvest:
Do not take the boat in Rabbit or Dragon years,
By the waterside there are worries and fears.
Water dragon in crystal palace you’d be visiting,
If instead of fishing from the shore, you went boating.


Style: Goose Flying in Fast and Gusty Winds
Goose flying in strong winds in high heavens.
Travel on horseback with whip in brave style.
Everyone says you will be lost in the dangerous place.
Who knows there is a road leading to success?
When you come home don’t bother to polish the mirror to look at yourself.
Rather go out to grind and polish your rusty sword.
If it were not for your brave determination, how can you be successful and jump up to the nine clouds from the plain level ground.
