Numeral 34    Ding Ding

Hexagram: Earth on Earth

Stars

Heavenly Well Star
Year or Hour Pillar Ding Chou – Fire Ox Ding Mao – Fire Rabbit
Metaphor Plough the land and get gold. Germinating plants are withered.
Explanation You work hard and you can become prosperous. You have to wander throughout your life. Enjoy the good days.

Heavenly Dust Star

Year or Hour Pillar Ding Si – Fire Snake Ding Wei – Fire Goat
Metaphor Moon shining over the pavilion. Nightingale singing in the valley.
Explanation You have your ancestors’ blessings, nothing to worry about. Danger in the beginning, safety in the end. Good progress climbing the career ladder.

 

Heavenly Tongue Star

Year or Hour Pillar Ding You – Fire Rooster Ding Hai – Fire Pig
Metaphor Crane standing on bare pine tree. Crow under the moon.
Explanation From a humble background, yet with great ambition very proud. The situation is modest and it is positive to have acceptance.

Poetic Life Reading

Destiny:

You are like a sheep dressed in tiger’s clothing.  In your straw house there is an animal head trophy. A straw house is pretty but it is not practical, it will be collapse when pushed. With the Retreating Spirit, bringing unluckiness, there is no fame and fortune.  A suspicious character, you are not a straightforward person and you are full of flattery. There is not much of a relationship between you and your siblings.  You are not particularly friendly with your family. You talk about people behind their back, but your heart is in a good place. Many a time you fail, because you do not communicate with people.  When everything looks right, it is time for you to cry. Even without meaning to, you can hurt your spouse and children. You break up your family, but your family will be re-united again. Amongst all the misfortune there will be fortune.  You are like a jade in the rock that will eventually show its brilliant side. It is like the image of a pearl hidden in deep waters. 

 

Luck Verses

You will get pearls that are precious.
At age thirty-six, a success so wondrous.
No need for plans to be set, or targets to be met.

Life Path: 
In Horse year meet someone who helps you prosper.
In troubled times, after Rat year problems will be over.
Whether it is rain and dew bringing sweetness,

It is all the same, the stars will shine over the river.

Marriage:
Husband and wife live in different places,
Fish carry messages to each other.
Eventually you will be together,
Do not grumble about being lonely.
In the end, good news to send,
Retirement is when your life is wealthy.

Children:
Two trees bear fruit means two sons,
Arriving before spring is over.
Because of your past good deeds,
Sons grow up to be somebody.
When you retire, have one son nearer.

Harvest:
Tiger, Monkey, Rabbit and Horse are years most important,
You can ride the cloud in the wind to reach your destination.
Under the moonlight at dawn, birds are singing.
When is it time for you to start waking?

Style: Plough the Land and Get Treasure
Don’t complain about the hard work plowing, because your efforts will bring you treasures.
The tree blossoms and bears fruit.
A couple of fish jump out of your pond.

With persistence, you will live an enjoyable life.

Commentary

Destiny

You are a mild person who puts on a cloak of bravado (“sheep dressed in tiger’s clothing”). A trophy hanging on the wall of a straw house is another metaphor for your life; things can look good without being practical. The “Retreating Spirit” means setbacks. Your nature is like hidden treasure buried deep underground that will eventually be unearthed, and shine in the daylight (“You are like jade in the rock that will eventually show its brilliant side”).

Luck Verses

Foundation

“When opening a pond or digging a well, you will get pearls that are precious,” suggests that when you are going about your own business, you find great rewards. To “wear a golden belt with robes of purple” means you have wealth and power.

Life Path

You will have success (“the stars will shine over the river”) even though you go through good times (“rain and dew bringing sweetness”) or challenging times (“wind and frost makes you shiver”).

Marriage

“Fish carry messages to each other” means messages are passed between husband and wife when you are apart.

Children (‘Children’ can also mean subordinates or followers)

Two sons are predicted. “Arriving before spring is over,” suggests that you have children when you are young. Because of good karma from your charitable acts, your sons will have achievement. For followers, you can attract successful followers early on.

Harvest

During Tiger, Monkey, Rabbit and Horse years you can get what you want (“ride the cloud in the wind to reach your destination”). “Under the moonlight at dawn, birds are singing” alludes to a very peaceful scene towards the end of your life.

Style

“The tree blossoms and bears fruit” suggests you see good rewards, whereas “A couple of fish jump out of your pond” implies that you might incur some losses.
