Numeral 59 Ji Ren

Hexagram: Earth on Water

Heavenly Fortune Star

Hour Pillar Ren Zi – Water Rat Ren Yin – Water Tiger
Metaphor Boating in the waves. Cicada singing sadly in the wind.
Explanation After all kinds of difficulties, you will enjoy success. When you live humbly beware of unexpected challenges.

Heavenly Witch Star

Hour Pillar Ren Chen – Water Dragon Ren Wu – Water Horse
Metaphor Banqueting by the flowers. Planting corn on a hot summer’s day.
Explanation Born with a silver spoon in your mouth. You have a very affluent lifestyle. It’s difficult in the present, but there is hope in the future.

Heavenly Book Star

Hour Pillar Ren Shen – Water Monkey Ren Xu – Water Dog
Metaphor Rowing a boat upstream. Trying to catch a fish in the forest.
Explanation Diligent throughout your life. Avoid wasting time and effort.

 

Destiny:
This is a person who thinks a lot and worries a lot. In this life it is easy to fail and easy to succeed.  Although it is difficult to have great accomplishment, there are lots of small successes. You know when to advance and when to retreat, and you know what is high and what is low. It can be difficult to make up your mind as you are a person who stands on firm ground. There is impatience over small matters, yet you can be relaxed over big things. Your sentiments are unpredictable. There will not be a fight over big money because instead of taking care of the dollars, you take care of the cents. It can feel like no matter how much you do for others, they will not be satisfied. When you rescue someone, they might not show their gratitude. There is misfortune hiding under fortune. Whatever is unfortunate will become fortunate in the end. Even though the Retreating Spirit affects your life, there is no danger of lawsuit and imprisonment.

 

Luck Verses

Foundation: 
When on top of the pole, you’re a performer,
Right in front of you there is danger.
There will be a girl to guide you to the leader.
At night, at the water concourse get help from a stranger.
Send many thanks to heaven after you prosper.

Life Path:
Gold and jade are not what is most precious.
Accumulation of good deeds is a true purpose,
Also, with good people, keep some closeness.
In this world, a lack of love is lamentable,
Friendships as thin as paper, not dependable.
When you look back repeatedly,
What appears illusory becomes reality.

Marriage:
In the pond mandarin ducks swim playfully,
Two or three couples keep the green lotus company.
Love and romance won’t come at the best of times, hardly.
At thirty-six, the moon will be full again then you marry.

Children:
By end of spring, no fruit on plum tree, as it were.
Look for the flower by the side of the water.
In later life, feel lonely on the river.
When hair has turned grey get successors.

Harvest:
Ox year is not the time to get on board with a new project or trip.
In Rat years don’t even think of riding a horse or boarding a ship.
When you see danger and there’s no danger, there’s fortune eventually.
You come back to sing a song amongst the flowers of prosperity.

 

Style: Mandarin Ducks Playing in the Water
Mandarin ducks play in lotus pond.
You will not be able to meet the one wearing red until after middle age.
If the money is there, you have to keep your hand in.
Even when you have grey hair you have to hold onto power.
Like wild geese flying in pairs to a distant land.
Mandarin ducks flirt by the pond-side.
If you still want to ask about your life, you will live a long life like the pine tree in your garden.
