Numeral 81    Ren Jia

Hexagram:  Water on Thunder

Heavenly Abundance Star

Hour Pillar Jia Zi – Wood Rat Jia Yin – Wood Tiger
Metaphor Watching the moon on the terrace. Goldfish in deserted well.
Explanation With other people’s help the result is excellent. Cherish your ability, even when there is a lack of opportunity and you are in humble circumstances.

Heavenly General Star

Hour Pillar Jia Chen – Wood Dragon Jia Wu – Wood Horse
Metaphor Willow on embankment in autumn. Withered flower meeting rain.
Explanation After middle age, situation is modest and solitary. Be cautious around your luck in later years.

Heavenly Noble Star 

Hour Pillar Jia Shen – Wood Monkey Jia Xu – Wood Dog
Metaphor Hibiscus and peony. Boat returns to peaceful waterfront.
Explanation Happy throughout life with fame and fortune. Can enjoy peace throughout life.

 

Destiny:
This life is blessed with the ability to think and plan, and, you are well versed in the application of such skills. You can plan for wealth without loss of fame because you are very patient with a determined mind.  With an upright character, there is no fear of attack from forces of evil, but you have to be careful with your own kin folks. Relatives are like strangers by the roadside. If you leave your hometown for a faraway land, you will feel full of energy even though you may be tired.

 

Luck Verses

Foundation:
Because it is lost, wild goose at the waterside is calling.
Doesn’t know if north or south the way to be heading. 
At sunset, lonely goose reflects lovely clouds whilst flying.
At the other end of the clouds, just hear the crane calling.

Life Path:
Even though there’s rain in garden, flowers will not bloom until later.
Spring has returned, swallow and nightingale come back for the flowers.
By the riverside with a tame deer for company,
Put a hook on the line and start fishing nicely.

Marriage:
Peach blossom casts a shadow on the water, bright and beautiful.
Mandarin ducks interfere with the serenity of nature, it’s not peaceful.
Who knows that after rain and wind, the moon is not full, flowers wither.
Mandarin ducks betray their vows, turn their backs, don’t stay together.

Children:
In the garden, the flower is beautiful.
Nightingale calls tenderly, on the willow.
Under your protection, help children avoid injury,
Don’t compete on horseback, or do sports that are risky.

Harvest:
During the Dog year, no need to be in a hurry.
A powerful person decides all for you already.
Who says the gentle slope is the long way to the next level?
Undeterred by wind and rain, firmly on this path you travel.

 

Style: Pine Trees Growing Taller than the Rest of the Forest
A tall pine tree grows up and out of the cold forest in winter.
By the river standing in the cold, but the cold doesn’t bother the pine tree.
On the water, a pair of mandarin ducks showing love and care for each other.
On distant skies the flying geese are casting shadows on the ground.
The branches with flowers are cut in the end.
Fame and fortune that you gain is not to your satisfaction, win some and lose some.
Just wait for Ox and Horse years for whatever you say there’ll be hundred people echoing your words, and ten thousand people admiring you.
