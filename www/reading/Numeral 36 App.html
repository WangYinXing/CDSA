Numeral 36    Ding Ji

Hexagram: Earth on Earth

Stars

Heavenly Storehouse Star

Hour Pillar Ji Chou – Earth Ox Ji Mao – Wood Rabbit
Metaphor Planting bamboo to become a bamboo forest. Fish swimming in the swamp are shocked by a disturbance.
Explanation The picture is one of descendants becoming prosperous. Even though you are facing adversity, you can be sure to get out of the situation.

Heavenly Balance Scale Star

Hour Pillar Ji Si – Earth Snake Ji Wei – Earth Goat
Metaphor Peach blossom on a spring day. Bright moon shining on plum blossom.
Explanation Good luck in early years, passable times in later life. You can enjoy a peaceful life throughout.

Heavenly Abundance Star

Hour Pillar Ji You – Earth Rooster Ji Hai – Earth Pig
Metaphor Spring breeze brings limited happy days. Putting on the clouds as clothing to see the moon.
Explanation Even though you get help, you need to manage your expectations of the benefits. Difficulty at present, hope is coming.

Poetic Life Reading

Destiny:

Your birth may be seen as an unlucky omen for your family because it coincides with something untoward happening to your relatives.  You are distant from your parents and children, so you should leave home and live a life far away. Hence, you will be leading a solitary life.  Even though you appear to be relaxed, you are in fact highly ambitious with some huge goals, it is like the sky is the limit.  Although you are working hard, sailing the waves for a thousand miles, you could find yourself performing on the street corner. Working life starts early, and you retire late. In this life you encounter a lot of trials and tribulations.  It is even frustrating when you think about things and you cannot make a decision. There is not much of a bond with your siblings, but you have a good rapport with friends. Even though climbing the mountain is a laborious task, in the end there is a time when you can glide along the path with ease.

Foundation:

One day you’ll grow wings leave water and fly skyward.
Riding a plane to reach the sky, eventually,
The Prosperity Horse brings you much glory.

Life Path:
Taking a bow and arrow you can shoot anytime,
Horse and Dragon years, up to the sky you climb.
Enjoy success by purple rose deep in the forest,
Listen to heavenly melodies from the finest.

Marriage:
After the wedding, you attract sympathy.
Foundation for relationship is unsteady.
To prevent collecting memories unhappy,
Avoid marrying for social status or money.

Children:
By the wall, plum wants to blossom.
Wind scatters flowers, a foursome,
Of green for sons and red for daughters.
In your back garden a strange fruit, that’s peculiar,
Like the heavenly peach, a stepchild who’s clever.

Harvest:
Bad luck in Geng and Jia years, face restrictions.
No success at these times, get out of situations.
Retire to the wilderness to find a boat on the river.
There the fishermen are singing and you can listen.

 

Style: Riding a Boat to Ford a River
In times of danger there is a savior.
You have to work hard.

Commentary

Destiny

“Even though climbing the mountain is a laborious task, in the end there is a time when you can glide along the path with ease,“ alludes to you overcoming challenges and working hard, then being able to enjoy life.

Luck Verses

Foundation

You have firm and high ambitions; bamboo and pine are sturdy and grow upwards. In Chinese legend there is a mythical creature, which starts off as a fish, known as a Kun, then transforms itself into a large bird, known as a Peng. You will be able to go far in life. The “Prosperity Horse” is symbolic of gaining wealth.

Life Path

“Taking a bow and arrow you can shoot anytime” implies you are ready to take action at anytime. You can make good progress during Dragon and Horse years. To “enjoy success by purple rose deep in the forest, listen to heavenly melodies from the finest” suggests you can enjoy a beautiful lifestyle.

Marriage

Choose a spouse with whom you are compatible to enjoy a happy marriage. It would be unwise to marry someone because of their wealth or position.

Children (‘Children’ can also mean subordinates or followers)

Four children are predicted. “In your back garden a strange fruit that’s peculiar, like the heavenly peach, a stepchild who’s clever” means you have a bright stepchild. For followers, you can attract many followers, including capable followers.

Harvest

Geng years are years ending with digit 0, such as 1990, 2000, 2010, 2020, etc. Jia years are those that end with digit 4, such as 1994, 2004, 2014, 2024. In Jia and Geng years it will be difficult to succeed because of bad luck. “Retire to the wilderness to find a boat on the river. There the fishermen are singing and you can listen” suggests you can retire in a rural environment near water.

Style

“Wealth is like snow in spring” means that money can melt away, it disappears. “Relationships are like dust on country roads” suggests that relationships break apart easily. “The wild goose is flying to leave home, like the mandarin duck losing its partner” alludes to you being single. In the end you will be successful (“like plum blossom after snow”).
