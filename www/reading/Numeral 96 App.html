Numeral 96    Gui Ji

Hexagram: Mountain on Earth

Heavenly Reporting Star

Hour Pillar Ji Chou – Earth Ox Ji Mao – Wood Rabbit
Metaphor Excavating a well without finding a stream. Watching the moon from the pavilion.
Explanation Avoid wasting time and effort working on something that cannot lead to any results. With somebody’s help, everything gets better.

Heavenly Farmland Star

Hour Pillar Ji Si – Earth Snake Ji Wei – Earth Goat
Metaphor Fine horse pulling cart of salt. Dry well welcomes the rain.
Explanation Cherish your great talents even when you have to be satisfied with holding a humble position. After all difficulties, you will encounter some good people.

Heavenly Crystal Star

Hour Pillar Ji You – Earth Rooster Ji Hai – Earth Pig
Metaphor Renovating a shabby house. Planting a pine tree in a pot.
Explanation Work hard throughout life and can revive family business. Although you expect stability, you still need to manage your expectations.

Destiny:
This is the life of a person who is born stubborn with high ambition. You have a kind heart, yet you are frank in speech. Suspicion and jealousy are invited into your life because you love to talk about other people. Whoever meets you will respect you because of your fearless nature. Whatever you may encounter, you are not afraid to go ahead. You have authority and can give orders properly. Time and again what begins with love ends in hate, all because of your enthusiasm.


Luck Verses

Foundation: 
If you’re promoted higher, it gets riskier.
Seeking money becomes tougher.
Ox and Dog years bring great achievement.
By the riverside, a deer you hunt down,
With all the rewards, return to hometown.

Life Path:
With emptiness in your gut, you’re seeking happiness.
Recollecting old days, shirt soaked in tears of distress.
Someone who can appreciate you is in the north-west.
No need to search everywhere, it would be needless.

Marriage:
Mandarin ducks, off to a good start, together flying.
Bad days of wind and rain are not long-lasting.
In later years, friends’ company you’ll be keeping.
All over willow forest cinnamon scent is seeping.

Children:
Blossoming flowers meet rain and drizzle.
Two fairy peaches, half green, half yellow.
For good fruits do many good deeds for charity.
With fragrant late plum blossom be happy.

Harvest:
For going home, a lack of opportunity,
A timetable for returning is unlikely.
Want to meet old friends all over the streams and mountains.
On a fisherman’s boat you hear monkeys and cranes singing.

 

Style: Pine Tree Standing Proudly in the Snow
Tall tree is used to the cold snow and frost.
The tree is full of integrity in its sturdy joints and with lofty ambition it stays evergreen.
Two geese fly high in the clouds.
Moon is shining brightly on a branch of flowers, casting a clear and sharp shadow.
Close relatives all like a dream.
Flesh and blood is like the water in the same well.
The boat is stable only after the winds.
You can sail all the way to the capital.
After stormy days there will be career success.
