Numeral 2    Jia Yi
 
Hexagram : Thunder on Wind

 

Stars

Purple Qi Star

Hour Pillar Yi Chou – Wood Ox Yi Mao – Wood Rabbit
Metaphor Willow blossoms turn into floating petals. The sick dragon walking in the rain.
Explanation The foundation is weak but there will be nice people coming to help. It will be a long period of bad luck but eventually, the good days will come.

Tall Dragon Star

Hour Pillar Yi Si – Wood Snake Yi Wei – Wood Goat 
Metaphor Fishing using a rod. Extracting gold from the sand.
Explanation Be wary of making ineffective efforts. Even though you put in a lot of effort, avoid being over-ambitious in your expectations.

Heavenly Revolutionary Star

Hour Pillar Yi You – Wood Rooster Yi Hai – Wood Pig
Metaphor Using a broken fan in autumn. Piling up stones beside gold.
Explanation There is a lack of endurance, beware of people who do not stand by you. Not all your endeavors turn out to be successes.  Be cautious in taking risks. Lucky enough not to lose everything.

Poetic Life Reading

Destiny:

You are bold and courageous, yet you are carefree. Your weakness is that you do not think ahead and plan carefully and you are not persistent. If someone laughs at you once, you will change your mind and alter your plans. Neither good luck nor bad luck will last. In your early years you are strong and mobile, yet you feel disappointed and discouraged in middle age. There are lots of unexpected difficulties in life. Even though you may have a family, you have to spend time away from your spouse and children. Friends are good company for entertainment only. They do not make suitable business partners. To live the life of a monk or a nun is a good solution.

 

Luck Verses

Foundation: 
The green willow tree surrounds the city.
The blossoms are so light, as light as a feather.
Yet, strong winds cannot disperse them all,
Despite hardship, the family is still together

Life Path:
During Tiger tail and Rabbit head, money will be made.
Ox and Goat will gain you a good name.
Though risky under the mulberry tree shade,
You will not lose the way, finding acclaim.

Marriage:
The mandarin ducks are tired, so weary,
Struggling in the rain, so dreary.
Though the time is the spring season,
There is a lack of a good companion.
Better to rest a while in lotus pond,
And watch the scared birds be gone.

Children:
The peach blossoms are ever so beautiful.
Two fruits are before the horse’s head.
Elegant clothes they’ll wear in the capital.

Harvest:
The evergreen pines live long,
Standing in the wind proud and strong.
The big fish seems to get to the sea.
Only to find a dream that is empty.

 

Style: A Pair of Loving Mandarin Ducks Floating in the Stream.
A pair of flying geese, each head off in their own direction.
When it rains, the love birds fly away deserting their companion.
Opportunity knocks but usually too late, never too soon.

Commentary

Destiny

Work or other travels could lead to you spending time away from your family. It is important not be distracted from your goals by what other people say. You could find a lot of contentment from self-development and spiritual growth, as implied from “live the life of a monk or a nun”. The spiritual side of life is rewarding.

Luck Verses

Foundation

Bad weather indicates difficult circumstances. Willow trees can bend, so you can adapt to changes. You might have started life facing hard times, yet there were positive scenarios too.

Life Path

At the end of Tiger years and at the start of the Rabbit years you can make money easily. During Ox and Goat years, you will gain a good reputation. In good times (“under the mulberry tree shade”) you will come across risky situations. However, you will not lose a way, but you will find the right action.

Marriage

Mandarin ducks are symbolic of a long-lasting couple. The verse hints at trials and tribulations when the tired mandarin ducks are struggling in the rain. Spring is symbolic of youth, so you might need to be patient before you meet your soulmate. Lotus pond means a pleasant place. To watch the scared birds be gone could mean that you are observing the mandarin ducks in the first line. When you notice other couples going through some strains with married life, you know you can be content with being single. On the other hand, this verse could also be interpreted to mean that you need to be aware that marriage requires effort to make it work. Enjoy being single when you are single.

Children (‘Children’ can also mean subordinates or followers)

Two fruits mean two children. To wear elegant clothes in the capital means they will be successful. If you do not have two beautiful and successful children this verse suggests you can lead capable people.

Harvest

Evergreen pines are symbolic of living a long life. The pine trees can withstand all sorts of weather conditions, suggesting that even when you are under pressure you are strong enough to cope. The big fish have ambitions, yet when they reach the sea they realise they do not have as much influence in a larger setting.

Style

A pair of geese symbolises a long-lasting couple. The first two lines of this section are suggesting beware of romance stumbling under challenging circumstances. The leaves and flowers may decay but the roots will stay means that if you have built a firm foundation then you can handle changes. You can endure many different conditions because your life is like an evergreen tree. A big fish swimming in shallow waters moves awkwardly, but it still gets there. Luck is such that you will reach your goals and you will climb to the very top of the ladder of success.
