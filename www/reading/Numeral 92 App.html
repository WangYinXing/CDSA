Numeral 92   Gui Yi

Hexagram: Mountain on Wind

Stars

Heavenly Penalty Star

Hour Pillar Yi Chou – Wood Ox Yi Mao – Wood Rabbit
Metaphor Tea leaves collected after spring. Using stones to mend a hole in the sky1.
Explanation It looks gorgeous on the surface, but inside your heart there are worries. Be cautious, for failures are probable and are difficult to correct.

Heavenly Exhausting Star

Hour Pillar Yi Si – Wood Snake Yi Wei – Wood Goat
Metaphor Pine and emerald cedar on top of the great wall. Head of fly and horns of snake.
Explanation Lofty ambitions are not easily achieved. Beware of misfortune.

Heavenly Prosperity Star 

Hour Pillar Yi You – Wood Rooster Yi Hai – Wood Pig
Metaphors Bright moon shining over plum blossom. Wearing beautiful clothes to return home.
Explanation Leisurely and happy life. Satisfaction throughout your life. Great rewards to bring home.

Poetic Life Reading

Destiny:

You are born with an intelligent character and a tall and noble appearance. You know your station in life. You are able to weigh up priorities and differentiate between what is pressing and what you can let go off. Even when you give the impression of being unconcerned, you have worries inside your heart. You are graceful and charitable towards others. Unfortunately, there are ungrateful people around you.

 

Luck Verses

Foundation:
Flowers blossom in gardens everywhere you go.
‘Why is my garden covered in frost and snow?’
They’re lucky, good days do not come to you early.
One day the sun’s warmth will return to spring valley.
Then you will be aroused and become young again.

Life Path:
All sorts of barriers you encounter.
Lofty ambitions to gain power.
At the right time no more you suffer.
Wealth and recognition from endeavors.

Marriage:
Mandarin ducks wrapped necks, it was like yesterday.
Turbulent ups and downs caused ducks to fly away.
Late in your life, flowers wither away and are gone.
New branches come out on old tree, can be relied on.

Children:
Three flowers means daughters, two fruits means sons.
Green and yellow beautify the branches, one by one.
In thick snow and heavy frost, damage can be done.
Unexposed good deeds give children protection.

Harvest:
Horse and Goat bring difficult days in your prime.
When drunk, see withered flowers, dreams not on time.
Recollect your entire life, everything is like a dream.
In the end crane and monkey keep you company in the mountain.


Style: Big Bird Peng Fighting Against the Wind
Big bird Peng leaves the northern territory.
Flying onto a high branch and staying there.
Fly into the clouds to leave for south.
Companion is playing in the pond.
Everywhere the flowers are blossoming, but yours are not.
When they bloom great gusts come.
All the flowers are gone in the morning, new buds are coming,
Spring scenery is found all over the nine mountains.

 

Footnote 1
From the legend of goddess Nu Wa who used stones to mend a hole in the sky.

Commentary

Destiny

Luck Verses

Foundation

“Flowers blossom in gardens everywhere you go. Why is my garden covered in frost and snow?” suggests that when you are young, you notice others have more luck than you do. When you are older, luck is with you, and you still feel young at heart.

Life Path

You need to overcome obstacles before you can fulfil your ambitions. Fame and fortune come when luck is with you.

Marriage

“Mandarin ducks wrapped necks, it was like yesterday” suggests a happy romance. Your relationship goes through difficult times. “Flowers wither away” implies that romance fades, yet you still have a reliable spouse.

Children (‘Children’ can also mean subordinates or followers)

Three daughters and two sons are predicted. By creating good karma with charitable acts your children can overcome difficulties (“In thick snow and heavy frost”). For followers, you can attract many of them.

Harvest

“Horse and Goat bring difficult days in your prime,” can refer to Horse years and Goat years being less fortunate, or it can mean you need to overcome difficulties in your prime years. Be patient, for it takes time to achieve your goals (“When drunk, see withered flowers, dreams not on time”). You enjoy a quiet life when you retire.

Style

Big bird Peng is a legendary bird that can fly long distances. When Peng will “fly into the clouds to leave for south,” it suggests you need to be prepared for a lengthy journey to success. Things might not work out for you in the beginning (“everywhere the flowers are blossoming, but yours are not”). After early disappointment, it works out well in the end (“new buds are coming, spring scenery is found all over the nine mountains”).
