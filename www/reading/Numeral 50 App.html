Numeral 50    Wu Gui

Hexagram: Mountain on Mountain

Stars

Heavenly Mechanical Star

Hour Pillar Gui Chou – Water Ox Gui Mao – Water Rabbit
Metaphor Autumn moon is round again. Yellow flowers in late life.
Explanation After middle age, many happy events. Determination is strong, late view is excellent.

Heavenly Pillar Star

Hour Pillar Gui Si – Water Snake Gui Wei – Water Goat
Metaphor Boating in stormy seas. Cold cicadas on the willow.
Explanation Experiencing all sorts of ups and downs. Vulnerable yet humble.

Heavenly Failure Star

Hour Pillar Gui You – Water Rooster Gui Hai – Water Pig
Metaphor Cold moon hanging in the sky. Renovating the broken home.
Explanation Artistic but solitary. Beware of taking on projects that are not easy to finish.

Poetic Life Reading

Destiny:

You are a born leader with planning and management capabilities.  A highly ethical law abiding citizen, people look up to you as a role model. Being discerning means you can easily distinguish the good from the bad, and you know what is what. Like a pillar of a house you like to be useful and supportive. Unfortunately, the Heavenly Failure star is in the central palace, this makes the Retreating Spirit too heavy.  It is tempting to retreat when life is not going your way, instead believe in yourself and remain steadfast to your goals. Misfortune will not be entirely harmful, but good things can be easily dispersed as well.  Competing during the lion dance, you make an attempt to collect the hanging reward from the hundred foot bamboo pole. You are not afraid to go for the top when it comes to competitions. You are brave enough to sail on stormy seas and can stay calm in the face of crisis. Why doesn’t danger bother you? Because of the Heavenly Mechanical star coming to your rescue whenever you need it.  You enjoy being helpful and can have a tendency to take on too much, then you wonder why there aren’t enough people to give you a hand! As well as making your mark as a worthy pillar of the community, remember to pay attention to your family.

 

Luck Verses

Foundation: 
Before you are rich, protect your name to look after your family.
The preparation to be prime minister is known already.
Waterways need to be cleared, before you can steer a boat,
To feast your eyes upon resplendent mulberry trees.

Life Path:
Rabbit, Rooster, Dragon and Tiger years you have success.
Make good use of a situation on the road to give orders.
In both Ox and Horse years, profits are the best.
Who gets the status to conquer the barbarians in the west?

Marriage:
At phoenix penthouse and dragon terrace spend time together,
To enjoy beautiful bright moon, the cool breeze is wonderful weather.
Mandarin ducks part, to the east, to the west, away each one flies.
Good days over, flowers won’t be glowing again before your eyes.

Children:
East wind blows over the peach on the golden branch.
Every flower, branch and leaf hangs firmly on the tree.
Come close to the beautiful and fragrant orchids again.
Descendants carrying on good family business, you’re happy!

Harvest:
You serve three emperors, as a duke in three reigns.
When in Rabbit and Ox years you experience change.
When you wait for the autumn moon in your jade parlor
You sleep in the west wind enjoying peace of mind.

 

Style: Sieving gold from sand
Don’t blame being busy throughout your life.
Filtering gold from sand doesn’t require much skill.
When the wild goose is flying over the village, it leaves only a shadow.
Mandarin ducks flying over the sand dunes does not leave any footprints behind.
You have gone over experiences half sweet and half bitter.
No matter how much you worry, it is going to be emptiness.
It happens that the fairy peach is blossoming for quite a while, leaving behind one flower to keep the spring wind company.

Commentary

Destiny

“The Heavenly Failure star is in the central palace, this makes the Retreating Spirit too heavy” means that you need to persist in when you encounter setbacks. If you don’t succeed at first stay determined and pick yourself up when you are down. The lucky stars will shine brightly and rescue you when you need it the most (“Heavenly Mechanical star coming to your rescue”).

Luck Verses

Foundation

It is important to protect your reputation. A high-flying career (“to be prime minister”) requires some careful planning. Once the way ahead is clear you will find success (“feast your eyes upon resplendent mulberry trees”).

Life Path

To “make good use of a situation on the road to give orders” means you have authority. You are brave when it comes to facing new challenges (“the status to conquer the barbarians in the west”).

Marriage

You visit dazzling places as a couple (“phoenix penthouse and dragon terrace spend time together”) and enjoy yourselves (“enjoy beautiful moon, the cool breeze”). Make the most of the good times you share.

Children (‘Children’ can also mean subordinates or followers)

You have good children who are able to take over a successful family business. For followers, your followers will be able to continue your legacy.

Harvest

You hold many positions of power, under three consecutive leaders (“serve three emperors”). There is a peaceful retirement waiting for you.

Style

Your life is meant to be busy and you need to keep going. A lot of what happens is fleeting and does not leave a lasting impression. Reflect on all the sweetness as well as the bitterness of experience.
