Numeral 68    Geng Xin

Hexagram: Lake on Heaven

Heavenly Hero Star

Hour Pillar Xin Chou – Metal Ox Xin Mao – Metal Rabbit
Metaphor Flying on the crane with loaded purse. Riding a horse on heavenly street.
Explanation Returning home with a big catch, you can become a great wealthy person. You are destined to hold a high position. Going up to the clouds.

Heavenly Riding Star

Hour Pillar Xin Si – Metal Snake Xin Wei – Metal Goat
Metaphor Riding a donkey in a lane. Walking with a stick, reciting a poem.
Explanation Modest career, yet you have great ambition. Suitable to study or do research work rather than going into business.

Heavenly Revolutionary Star

Hour Pillar Xin You – Metal Rooster Xin Hai – Metal Pig
Metaphor Throwing a ball under the moon. Broken mirror is dusty.
Explanation Although somebody is taking care of you, your determination is not firm. Risky situations are at hand, need to be careful.

 

Destiny:
When this star is shining on your life there will be a lot of gossip. Whatever you do there are a lot of crooked paths. You can gain real power that does not depend on you giving flattery to your superiors. It is easy for you to be happy and easy for you to get angry.  In this life, it is easy to get glory and easy to get shame.  You cannot hide even small secrets.  You believe in standing up and speaking your mind, sometimes even with hurtful language. There is room for improvement in your social and human relationship skills, which are not satisfactory.  Your relatives can be two-faced and it is like they hold a knife behind their laughing faces.  Parents are like a candle in the wind.

Luck Verses

Foundation: 
Everything looks great when moon is above centre of the sky.
In autumn, a gust of wind stirs up the waves as it blows by.
Those who know you gather together on the east river.
When time comes you will go to the nine heavens together,
Group of friends working get success thereafter.

Life Path:
To finish the job, you have to do things repeatedly.
Just remember you’ll make a name in this world, no doubt.
Hidden talent like jade buried in the field before it’s dug out.
A skillful sculptor makes it something gorgeous eventually.
Someone will come along who knows how to use your ability.

Marriage:
The plum in winter makes the riverside good scenery.
Pine trees grow to reach heaven, standing firmly.
Appreciate bright moon and cool breeze with good company.
Enjoy green mountains and deep rivers, lot of fun, so merry.

Children:
New branches are coming out on the pine.
A pair of fairy cranes on top of forest.
What a pity when bright moon is shadowed by wind and rain.
Do good deeds before your children can prosper and gain.

Harvest:
Green mountains and clear streams,
Behind closed door hear bells toll across fields.
Bright moon shines over deep green pine trees.
Baboon and crane in the wilderness you befriend.
Enjoy a good retirement life in the end.

 

Style: Turtle Hiding Under the Water
Moon in mid-sky develops a long shadow.
You’ll be holding the golden seal in Geng, Xin years.
You are rowing a sampan on misty river.
There’ll be huge turtle hiding under deep waters.
Wild geese call each other to fly in different formation.
Flowers in second moon become peach fruits.
Don’t care about many difficulties in your life.
When you are old, you will be facing golden gate and meeting noble friends.
