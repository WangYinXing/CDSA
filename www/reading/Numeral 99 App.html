Numeral 99 Gui Ren

Hexagram: Mountain on Water

Heavenly Lonesome Star

Hour Pillar Ren Zi – Water Rat Ren Yin – Water Tiger
Metaphor Fallen flower is meeting the rain. Wearing beautiful clothes on the back of an ox.
Explanation Beware of setbacks in life. Although you are rich you lack ambition.

Heavenly Imaginary Star

Hour Pillar Ren Chen – Water Dragon Ren Wu – Water Horse
Metaphor Fish in the pond escape from the net. Peony after autumn.
Explanation When you get rid of misfortune, you can expect great achievement. After middle age you will have great achievement.

Heavenly Exhausting Star

Hour Pillar Ren Shen – Water Monkey Ren Xu – Water Dog
Metaphor Moon shining over cold pond. Flowers blooming after autumn.
Explanation When you are down there will be noble people coming to give you a helping hand. After middle age you can enjoy life to your heart’s content.

 

Destiny:
This life is like bare branches meeting spring and it is like withered buds meeting the rain. After all the dangers, difficulties and barriers you will meet something to arouse your spirits. Before you turn forty you have worked very hard. Then you will be able to enjoy fortune. If not for the cold ice in the bottom of the water the plum tree cannot be blossoming in the garden.

 

Luck Verses

Foundation:
When the lifeboat is sailing on high waves, there’s no need for a paddle.
Throughout life success and failure are just like the waves, why struggle?
Bring your musical instrument to the tent of the commander.
And with your ability, follow the hero to become wealthy.

Life Path:
In middle age, good tidings to bring you to glorious lands, oh!
Old plum tree blossoms after going through frost and snow.
There’s hope by the waterside where green grass grows.
Bring your musical instrument to the wealthy and powerful.

Marriage:
In early days mandarin ducks get together.
In spring wind, they play in clear water.
Recollecting these days, it is like a dream, an ideal picture.
In later days, on another branch, another beautiful flower.

Children:
Three flowers blossom leaving two fruits hanging.
In late years there’s more, there’s another one coming.
All of them know how to bring glory to the family.
Be grateful and offer incense to heaven thankfully.

Harvest:
Remember all the gossip you had to handle.
Now you are lucky there’s no more scandal.
When you can wear gold belt with clothes beautiful,
It’s time to go fishing, retire after being successful.

 

Style: Tiger Crouching in the Plum Forest
In the evening tides row a sampan alone.
Failures and success throughout your life are like waves.
Sail your boat towards the north-west (power) when the wind is with you.
If you turn towards south-east (wealth), the waves will be high again.
Wild geese are flying over the water. 
Danger of mandarin ducks getting lost under the bridge.
Who knows when the tiger is roaring in the forest?
Then you know how bold and daring the hero is.
When you encounter danger then you will show bravery.
