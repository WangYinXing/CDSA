Numeral 64    Geng Ding

Hexagram: Lake on Earth

Stars

Flying Wheel Star

Hour Pillar Ding Chou – Fire Ox Ding Mao – Fire Rabbit
Metaphor Fish swimming in spring water. Wearing jute clothing in autumn.
Explanation An active character and everything is to your heart’s content. Your parents stay around until you are middle aged.

Heavenly Grass Star 

Hour Pillar Ding Si – Fire Snake Ding Wei – Fire Goat
Metaphor Watching moon on mountain. Clean as jade, clear as ice.
Explanation You enjoy good endings in everything throughout your life. Very honest and can enjoy a leisurely life.

Heavenly Storehouse Star 

Hour Pillar Ding You – Fire Rooster Ding Hai – Fire Pig
Metaphor Nightingale moves to another tree. Thin dew in the autumn.
Explanation Promotion all the time, getting better every time. After middle age life is tame.

Poetic Life Reading

Destiny:

This is the life of a person who thinks a lot and is very smart. You can get something unintentionally and create something from nothing.  Those who are close to you will be like clouds covering the bright moon. Wealth is like duckweed in the waves, bobbing up and down. Whatever turns out to be delightful, would have encountered a lot of trying difficulties along the way. It is easy to lose fame and fortune. Whatever misfortune you encounter can be cured.  There is no danger of facing lawsuits. It is suitable to leave your family business early and start your own career.


Luck Verses

Foundation:
Moon is like silver in the breezy cloudless sky.
Frosty red maple leaves moonlit at midnight.
Already in the palace, your future is bright.
Sound of drums and bells on lots of occasions,
Promotion after promotion, many celebrations.

Life Path: 
In late years, a lot of reorganization.
Great ambition and hard work gives you recognition.
Fame in this commercial world brings you fortune,
But you escape to enjoy life in a distant mountain.

Marriage:
Marriage pre-destined because of past life.
Wind blows away clouds reveals a moon bright.
Pine tree stands to proudly greet frost and snow.
Feel the chilly blanket and the cold pillow.

Children:
Tree is full of flowers in blossom.
One fruit means there is one son.
Mulberry and cinnamon means lots of grandchildren.
Recount the tales of your boat journey to them.

Harvest:
Horse or Ox year wind blows clouds away,
Time to go to the mountains they say.
In green mountain cranes and baboons keep you company.
Lie down and listen when you’re elderly,
To announcement of winter by the bell that’s frosty.

 

Style: Moon Shining Over Quiet Chamber
The moon is bright like an icy wheel.
Staying out of this world doesn’t carry a speck of dust.
All the worries do not disturb the satisfied person.
Living a leisurely life is good for this person.
When the full moon, also known as jade rabbit, rises above the sea,
The bright sun, also known as golden crow, shines through clouds.
Family is over the five lakes.
In spring flowers are gone and the fruits will ripen.

Commentary

Destiny

“Those who are close to you will be like clouds covering the bright moon,” implies that close ones can inadvertently hinder your efforts. “Wealth is like duckweed in the waves, bobbing up and down,” suggests that income can vary at different times. Once you overcome challenges you can succeed.

Luck Verses

Foundation

You find yourself in the right place and the future is looking promising. “Sound of drums and bells on lots of occasions,” implies there are lots of noisy celebrations.

Life Path

After achieving fame and fortune, you want to downsize your life (“escape to enjoy life on a distant mountain”).

Marriage

“Wind blows away clouds reveals a moon bright,” suggests that you can enjoy many good times in your relationship. “Pine tree stands to proudly greet frost and snow, feel the chilly blanket and cold pillow,” alludes to you being able to withstand difficulties, or it could mean that you live longer than your spouse.

Children (‘Children’ can also mean subordinates or followers)

Many daughters (flowers) and one son are the predictions for children. For followers, it indicates more female than male followers.

Harvest

In a Horse or Ox year obstacles are removed (wind blows clouds away) and you are likely to retire with pets (cranes and baboons). Take life easy when you are elderly for you are likely to enjoy a long life into your winter years.

Style

“The moon is bright like an icy wheel,” suggests that there is good fortune. “Staying out of this world doesn’t carry a speck of dust,” means you can be quite spiritual and leave behind your earthly concerns. You have an optimistic nature (“full moon rises above the sea, the bright sun shines through the clouds”). Your family is spread far and wide (“over the five lakes”). You will begin to see success when you are young (“In spring flowers are gone and the fruits will ripen”).
