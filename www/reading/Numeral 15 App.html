Numeral 15    Yi Wu

Hexagram: Wind on Mountain


Heavenly Repose Star

Hour Pillar Wu Zi – Earth Rat Wu Yin – Earth Tiger  
Metaphor The cold fish coming out of the water. Riding a horse on the street with flowers.
Explanation Whether advancing or retreating, manage your expectations. You have ability, value it for yourself. You have high ability, but you need to be very cautious in your actions.

Heavenly Thunder Star 

Hour Pillar Wu Chen – Earth Dragon Wu Wu – Earth Horse
Metaphor Rotten lotus receives dew. Firefly emerging from decayed grass.
Explanation Even if your son is not helpful, learn to enjoy your fortune. Put your intelligence to good use.

Heavenly Power Star

Hour Pillar Wu Shen – Earth Monkey Wu Xu – Earth Dog
Metaphor Palm tree planted on sand. Dew is floating on lotus leaf.
Explanation Whatever you are engaged in, do not be over-ambitious about wealth. You need to be more determined about accumulating wealth.

 

Destiny:
The fairies are plowing the fields. A beautiful and well to do woman fetches water from the river.  These images suggest that at your most leisurely, you will create hardship for yourself. You find it hard to enjoy life.  Although you are generous, intelligent and knowledgeable, you endeavor to establish several businesses without much success. Eventually, you will be able depend on one or two small businesses to keep you going. It seems as if you have to take care of a lot of things by yourself because your son is not helpful. When good fortune arrives you are not really able to take pleasure from it.  You are an unselfish and honest character, yet when you are kind to others, they will not reciprocate.


Luck Verses 

Foundation:
Green pine, green willow, green willow, green pine.
Like a crane that leaves its nest, you soar up in sky.
Yet you come back to the earth and get dusty, why?
As no support from pine and willow branches, upon, which you can rely.

Life Path:
In Pig year or Dog year you’re standing by riverside.
It’s time to climb the ladder, to reach the sky.
Gold, jade and mulberry trees can all be acquired.
A powerful person gives a helping hand at your side,

Marriage:
Herons leisurely swimming in the swamp.
When the moon is bright, what about a partner?
Meet a match after travelling to a distant land.
Find true love and a good companion.

Children:
The flowers are battered by night rain.
In later years you will have a son,
A letter of good tidings, he will be sending.
Of his great achievements, he will be telling.

Harvest:
In Dragon and Tiger years you’ll meet good friends.
The ship has sailed far, open sails to sail faster.
If you sail too fast in the river, the jewels you drop.
Alas, they cannot be found, splash, plop, plop.


Style: Bird Standing in Pine Forest
When the bird is hatched it flies away from the nest.
Landing on the branch of the green pine tree where it perches.
After your studies are over, you start work.
You want to revive family business, but it is not that easy.
Wild geese find it hard to fly in the clouds.
Mandarin ducks are battered by rain.
It is difficult for your children to be with you, but you bring it upon yourself.
When you want to launch a project, wait for right timing.
Wait until the moon shines over terrace.
In the beginning you may fail in a lot of your undertakings.
Eventually, you will have the trees and flowers in your garden.
