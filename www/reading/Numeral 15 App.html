Numeral 15    Yi Wu

Hexagram: Wind on Mountain

Stars

Heavenly Repose Star

Hour Pillar Wu Zi – Earth Rat Wu Yin – Earth Tiger  
Metaphor The cold fish coming out of the water. Riding a horse on the street with flowers.
Explanation Whether advancing or retreating, manage your expectations. You have ability, value it for yourself. You have high ability, but you need to be very cautious in your actions.

Heavenly Thunder Star 

Hour Pillar Wu Chen – Earth Dragon Wu Wu – Earth Horse
Metaphor Rotten lotus receives dew. Firefly emerging from decayed grass.
Explanation Even if your son is not helpful, learn to enjoy your fortune. Put your intelligence to good use.

Heavenly Power Star

Hour Pillar Wu Shen – Earth Monkey Wu Xu – Earth Dog
Metaphor Palm tree planted on sand. Dew is floating on lotus leaf.
Explanation Whatever you are engaged in, do not be over-ambitious about wealth. You need to be more determined about accumulating wealth.

Poetic Life Reading

Destiny:

The fairies are plowing the fields. A beautiful and well to do woman fetches water from the river.  These images suggest that at your most leisurely, you will create hardship for yourself. You find it hard to enjoy life.  Although you are generous, intelligent and knowledgeable, you endeavor to establish several businesses without much success. Eventually, you will be able depend on one or two small businesses to keep you going. It seems as if you have to take care of a lot of things by yourself because your son is not helpful. When good fortune arrives you are not really able to take pleasure from it.  You are an unselfish and honest character, yet when you are kind to others, they will not reciprocate.

Commentary

Destiny

It seems as if you are your own worst enemy as you make life hard for yourself. It can be your choice to enjoy life.

Luck Verses

Foundation

“Like a crane that leaves its nest, you soar up in the sky”, means you have high ambitions and you follow through on your plans. “Yet you come back to the earth and get dusty” means your progress is slowed because you need to attend to the practical details. You end up being responsible for sorting out the practicalities because there is nobody else to help out (“no support from pine and willow branches”).

Life Path

The time to go for your goals is during a Pig year or a Dog year. To “stand by the riverside” implies that you are ready to cross the river, to take a big step. Or, it can mean that there is a significant event when you are at the riverside. “Gold, jade and mulberry trees” refers to money and property.

Marriage

“Herons leisurely swimming in the swamp” implies you think that you are not in a hurry to settle down. A bright moon is symbolic of good fortune. You should look for a soul mate during fortunate times. When you travel to somewhere far away, you can find the one.

Children (‘Children’ can also mean subordinates or followers)

“The flowers are battered by night rain,” suggests that you might need to be patient when it comes to having children. It is predicted that a son you have in later years will eventually move away and become successful. For followers, capable followers will come later.

Harvest

You can meet good friends in Dragon years and Tiger years and go far doing business together, “ship has sailed far”. You need to be careful with expanding your business. “If you sail too fast in the river, the jewels you drop”, implies that if you attempt to grow too quickly you can lose your profits (“jewels”).

Style

“Wild geese find it hard to fly in the clouds” suggests that you can encounter some challenges in going for your goals. “Mandarin ducks are battered by rain” implies that you face troubles together with your spouse. To have “trees and flowers in your garden” means you will have a blossoming success.
