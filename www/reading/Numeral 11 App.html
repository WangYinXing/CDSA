Numeral 11    Yi Jia

Hexagram: Wind on Thunder

Stars

Abundant Reserves Star

Hour Pillar Jia Zi – Wood Rat Jia Yin – Wood Tiger 
Metaphor Sweet rain comes during the drought. Fierce tiger coming out of the forest.
Explanation After overcoming problems, there are opportunities. You have enough power to keep other people under control.

Heavenly Thief Star

Hour Pillar Jia Chen – Wood Dragon Jia Wu – Wood Horse
Metaphor Sail is on the mast. Pine trees experience frost.
Explanation Your diligence pays off. It is fortunate that there are people who help you. As long as you remain determined, even when you encounter mishaps, you will be unscathed.

Heavenly Axle Star

Hour Pillar Jia Shen – Wood Monkey Jia Xu – Wood Dog
Metaphor The musical instrument is in harmony with the melody. Planting a pine tree in the rain.
Explanation You do the right thing at the right time. Every move you make is advantageous. You have strong roots and you get blessings from your ancestors.

Poetic Life Reading

Destiny:

It is as if your destiny and luck cycles are reversed, it is challenging to establish your career.  When you are young, you may have a comfortable family background, yet when you set out on your working life, you need to make a lot of effort yourself.  There are many obstacles within the system that you have to overcome as you set about pursuing your career.  You often feel like giving up, yet you need to persevere and not let it bother you.  Relatives can easily desert you and accumulating wealth is not easy. Yet, in times of misfortune, the situation can turn around to good fortune.  Watch out for what you laugh about, for it can turn into something that is no laughing matter. Distant friends understand you well.  Those that are supposed to be close to you, your siblings and relatives become your enemies.  Leave your hometown to find a new life.  This is a not an easy life for a male, but for a female life is easier after you leave home.

Commentary

Destiny

Do not be disheartened when you face adversity because the situation can turn out to be lucky eventually. When it is the time to establish a career, you are better off leaving your hometown.

Luck Verses

Foundation

You have more luck when you leave your hometown, but you will miss home. “At sunset, fields with mulberry trees are yours” means that you will be successful in the end.

Life Path

Tiger and Goat years can turn out to be fortunate. Rabbit and Monkey years can be prosperous. You can become well known in Dog or Rooster years.

Marriage

Beauty is only skin deep. Get to know somebody and see them clearly before you commit. Choose a spouse based on their character, and not just based on their appearance.

Children (‘Children’ can also mean subordinates or followers)

“Three flowers will bear more fruit” means you will have three daughters. They will bring you grandchildren. If you have a son later in life, he will have a life of his own. Some of your followers will bring more followers. A follower who comes late might not stick around.

Harvest

Even when there are problems you can handle them. Dog and Snake years are good times. There is no need to be in a hurry for success, “riding a horse up the mountain” is done at a steady pace.

Style

Success will not come early. “One hundred flowers blossom” means there will be good fortune. The south-west might be a significant direction or location. As long as you are not overly-ambitious you will not make life hard for yourself.
