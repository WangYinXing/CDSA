Numeral 37    Ding Geng

Hexagram: Earth on Lake

Stars

Heavenly Rigid Star 

Hour Pillar Geng Zi – Metal Rat Geng Yin – Metal Tiger
Metaphor Hibiscus in front of the mirror. Fishing by the cold pond.
Explanation The great prosperity at present is not to be taken for granted. Changes can happen in the blink of an eye. Even though you work hard, you need to manage your expectations when it comes to doing what is unfamiliar.

Heavenly Noise Star

Hour Pillar Geng Chen – Metal Dragon Geng Wu - Metal Horse
Metaphor Swallow in autumn building up a nest. Shabby boat full of treasure.
Explanation Devoted to working hard for others throughout your life. Even though you have wealth luck, be careful of losing what you have.

Heavenly Calamity Star

Hour Pillar Geng Shen – Metal Monkey Geng Xu – Metal Dog
Metaphor Willow on the riverside in spring. Going up the pavilion at sunset.
Explanation Good luck in early years, but you have to be determined. Good luck during old age.

Poetic Life Reading

Destiny:

You can easily form good relationships in the beginning. However, you can easily fall out with friends and become foes in the end. By thinking of new techniques, you want to make what you do most proficient, but it turns out not to be the right way. You are frank with people and speak from the bottom of your heart, but eventually these people betray you. It is hard for you to establish good human relationships. The connection with siblings is not peaceful. Leave your hometown to start a new life outside. Live in solitude. When life looks promising, it turns out to be not so, your fortune is like clouds covering the bright autumn moon. Wealth is like petals being blown about in the spring wind, what is blooming can be easily scattered. Even though you have friends and relatives, it is as if you have none. You have to shoulder all the responsibilities yourself. Think about it carefully, who can work in partnership with you.

 

Luck Verses

Foundation:
Time decrees decay, time decrees glory.
With hard work comes strong opportunity.
In the end you’ll have power, victory,
With helping hands you rely on fully.

Life Path:
The path is very crooked and dangerous.~
Whip horse, speed up to reach destination.~
When you get help, success is right ahead.
Laugh proudly at your special achievement.

Marriage:
In lotus pond, two mandarin ducks play,
Other water fowl don’t disturb you anyway.
Arriving at north side of the mountain,
Here, it is very quiet for certain.
In old age wind and frost your companions.

Children:
Flowers bloom, flowers wither, don’t despair.
In the end a filial one will be there.
Five leaves are sons, three flowers are daughters,
One is outstanding, extremely clever.

Harvest:
When Tiger meets Goat it is time to go.
Whatever is going on, you already know.
Recalling all those beautiful days past,
Such great times, many good memories last.

 

Style: Spending Time Alone Upstairs
Every day at sunset on the beautifully decorated terrace, you look out to the horizon and the sea.
The lonely boat does not bring you any tidings from home.
You have travelled far over the waves.
Eventually you made it to your destination.
Trust your fate that there is opportunity for you to earn enough to raise your family.
If you ask the grey haired fisherman, he’ll tell you to follow the path on the waterways made by the bulrushes to reach paradise.

Commentary

Destiny

You can leave your hometown and establish yourself in another place. Choose your friends wisely.

Luck Verses

Foundation

You can succeed at the right time. You will succeed due to a strong work ethic and finding reliable assistance.

Life Path

“The path is very crooked and dangerous,” implies you encounter risky situations on the road to success. “Whip horse, speed up to reach destination” suggests that you need to persist with your goals and not to slow down.

Marriage

“In lotus pond, two mandarin ducks play,” means you enjoy married life. “Arriving at the north side of the mountain,…wind and frost your companions” suggest that you experience solitude.

Children (‘Children’ can also mean subordinates or followers)

Five sons and three daughters are predicted, yet children might come and go. A devoted child will stay by your side (“filial offspring will be there”). For followers, you can attract a lot of followers. Some of your followers will be loyal and some will be highly capable.

Harvest

“When Tiger meets Goat it is time to go” can have various meanings. If you are born in a Tiger year, it can mean when you encounter a Goat year or vice versa. Tiger meets Goat can also refer to other people you meet who might be born in Tiger or Goat years. When Tiger meets Goat it is the time to look back and realise you have had a good life.

Style

You have settled a long way from home and you do not hear from home very often. “If you ask the gray haired angler, he’ll tell you to follow the path on the waterways made by the bulrushes to reach paradise,” suggests to you to have faith that someone will point you in the right direction.
