Numeral 41    Wu Jia

Hexagram:  Mountain on Thunder

Heavenly Prosperity Star

Hour Pillar Jia Zi – Wood Rat Jia Yin – Wood Tiger
Metaphor Riding a horse at the edge of a cliff. Crossing the sea in a boat.
Explanation Be cautious about taking any risks. Smart in small matters, you need to manage your expectations of what can be achieved.

Heavenly Southern Star

Hour Pillar Jia Chen – Wood Dragon Jia Wu – Wood Horse
Metaphor Planting flowers during the grain rain1 period. Riding a horse on top of a defensive wall.
Explanation No hardships throughout life. Climbing up continuously. You experience one challenge after another. Be prepared for any stumbling blocks that might lie ahead.

Heavenly Plantation Star

Hour Pillar Jia Shen – Wood Monkey Jia Xu – Wood Dog
Metaphor Release a crane in front of the door. Playing a string instrument under the willow tree.
Explanation Although you have ability, you have a relaxed, easy-going attitude. Enjoy playing your song leisurely, even if other people might not appreciate your music.

 

Destiny:
You are righteous person, straight in your dealings, and are good at looking into the details. Your nature is both unbiased and unselfish.  Just like wind and thunder can be quite exciting, so is your character. Although you are kind at heart, you can still enjoy a bit of gossip.  In this life your destiny is such that you can benefit from serving a wealthy patron in many ways. This benefactor also likes to keep your company for wine and song in places of entertainment.  When it comes to doing business, your approach can be likened to ‘grafting a peach branch onto a plum tree’, which is to say that you transform an existing business into an apparently similar type of business, but it is actually different. For instance, you might turn an existing restaurant into a hotel, or a restaurant into a bar, or a boutique into a jeweler’s store. You are hurt time and again because of such business strategies. There is a need to change the leaves and roots and start again, which means you need to fundamentally change the business into something completely different. There is failure within success when you are changing your career all the time. However, you are destined to be continually transforming and reforming.
