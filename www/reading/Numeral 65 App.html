Numeral 65    Geng Wu

Hexagram: Lake on Mountain

Heavenly Ginseng Star

Hour Pillar Wu Zi – Earth Rat Wu Yin – Earth Tiger
Metaphor Valuable horse with a golden saddle. Thirsty horse drinking from spring.
Explanation Ability is well presented. Career will be great. When you are in despair there is someone coming to help.

Heavenly Lonesome Star

Hour Pillar Wu Chen – Earth Dragon Wu Wu – Earth Horse
Metaphor The snow meeting the wind. Accumulating the earth to become a mountain.
Explanation Although there is misfortune there is someone to help. Frugal throughout life, eventually becoming very wealthy.

Heavenly Phoenix Star

Hour Pillar Wu Shen – Earth Monkey Wu Xu – Earth Dog
Metaphor Flowers and nightingales on the bank with willows. Watching your old image in the mirror.
Explanation Success that is gained because of others might not be long-lasting. Leisurely and pleasant late years.

 

Destiny
This life is like a lonely cloud coming over the mountain, like a house in the wilderness riding the wind. Your heart is everywhere and your mind is not here. Establish yourself in a strange land doing business elsewhere. You plant a pine on east bank and appreciate its beauty from the west bank. Planting bamboo in south garden, then enjoy the shade in north garden. Wherever you get wealthy, you will return to your hometown to build a house. Your parents are like visiting guests and siblings are like fading stars. You don’t love the pleasures of your homeland. Wherever you can do well, you can make that place your home.


Luck Verses

Foundation:
No need to weigh the gold when there’s a bucketful.
Money is external and it can bring trouble.
In your hometown the fields you own may be several.
Better to dress up and move up to jade hall, leave as soon you’re able.

Life Path:
Work very hard, in middle age become successful.
Enjoy music and dancing in extravagant hall,
Borrow strength and power from another to become noble.
In the end you’ll lose their support, so be careful.

Marriage:
Moon shines over deep forest and moon sleeps there.
Mandarin ducks parted for some time, no longer a pair.
Can find another spouse, can find a love to share, can make a new bond,
For amongst all the water fowl, a beautiful red lotus grows in the green pond.

Children:
Two trees blossom, two fruits, that’s two sons.
Outside the wall, another fruit, another son.
In late years, orchids and cinnamon resplendent.  
Magnificent jade hall, full of your descendants.

Harvest:
At start of Ox or end of Horse year it’s time to go.
Not found green mountains and blue water though.
Life can be just like an empty dream, you know,
But people can constantly remember the hero.

 

Style: Phoenix Sleeping on Maple
Heart is undecided to move forward or to step back.
Worldly affairs are like playing chess with a fairy.
In the cold wind wild goose casts a shadow on the ground.
The evening rain on the maple tree makes a solitary sound.
A lot of trees cannot provide you with a shading canopy.
Unintentionally planted flowers become a forest.
Because of the woodcutter pointing you to the right way, you will get to the white land picking up gold.
