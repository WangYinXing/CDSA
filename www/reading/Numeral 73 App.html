Numeral 73    Xin Bing

Hexagram: Heaven on Fire

 

Heavenly Abundance Star

Hour Pillar Bing Zi – Fire Rat Bing Yin – Fire Tiger
Metaphor Petals follow the flow of the water. Catching the rabbit on the moon.
Explanation Diligent throughout your life, but to accumulate wealth is not so easy. You look happy. You can enjoy a good name, and fame exceeds material rewards.

Heavenly Broad-mindedness Star

Hour Pillar Bing Chen – Fire Dragon Bing Wu – Fire Horse
Metaphor Well polished bronze mirror is covered with dust again. Willow on the embankment in autumn.
Explanation You have the ability to bring your family back to prosperity. Yet, there are a lot of challenges. The later years appear to be not so prosperous.

Heavenly Bestowing Star

Hour Pillar Bing Shen– Fire Monkey Bing Xu – Fire Dog
Metaphor The garden in spring. Looking for the lotus on a summer day.
Explanation If you depend on your inheritance, it is difficult to maintain it for a long time. Develop independently. You get a son during your strong years. Whatever you wish for, you will get your heart’s desire.

 

Destiny
This life is like fording a river in strong gusts of wind. When you go across the sea and go through the mountains, there will be three rises and three falls in your life. You start your career quite early to bring wealth to your family.  Life will be very busy, so that you get children quite late. All this is pre-determined by your fate.


Luck Verses

Like a sick tiger that’s recovered, showing off paws and teeth that are powerful.

Life Path:

You can dress up to establish power again.

When the swallow flies away, it means separation.

Watch people outside, but home alone, solo.

Children:

Who knows when the fruit ripens, it bears a good name.
Son carries a great and good reputation,

Even the emperor has heard of your charity fame.

Harvest:
Snake and Dog years are lucky ones.
Birch forest is facing the setting sun.
On the high mountain, water is flowing,
But where is the person? The person?
Only a good name is left in heaven.

 

Style: Cloud Covering the Moon
