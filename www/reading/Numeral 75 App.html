Numeral 75    Xin Wu

Hexagram: Heaven on Mountain

Heavenly Seal Star

Hour Pillar Wu Zi – Earth Rat Wu Yin – Earth Tiger
Metaphor Fish in swamp is going into the sea. Wearing silk robes to travel by sampan.
Explanation Once you have good luck, you will have great achievement. You return home when you are successful and you never forget your origins.

Heavenly Fast Horse Star

Hour Pillar Wu Chen – Earth Dragon Wu Wu – Earth Horse
Metaphor Bare branches waiting for spring. Bells toll from a distant monastery.
Explanation When you are facing bleak days, stay put, and wait for the right timing to come. When you are successful, you should retire and your name will be heard in all directions.

Heavenly Polaris Star

Hour Pillar Wu Shen – Earth Monkey Wu Xu – Earth Dog
Metaphor Crane sitting on top of tall tree. Autumn rains showering on hibiscus.
Explanation You choose the right master and serve him then, your ability will flourish. When you are determined to be uncorrupt, to have clean dealings, there is no worry throughout your life.

 

Destiny
This life has firm power, a stiff power. You are a character with a lot of guts and great ambition.  Capable of a lot of good planning, you are alert to changes.  Competent people will admire you. Ignoble people will hate you.  Whatever you do, be careful of attack that hurts you, but don’t worry you will recover later.  It is unwise to be generous to people who will hate you or complain about you in the end. Fame and fortune comes with a lot of hard work. But when you are old, you can recollect the good old days.


Luck Verses

Foundation:
Like pine and cedar from the wild grass rising,
Re-shape yourself with great ambition, wild grass re-shaping.
Support from rainwater, into the clear river it is flowing.
Eventually, your good name will be left on the rocks therein.

Life Path:
South mountains are green, north mountains are blue.
Opportunities everywhere, friends talk business with you.
Nothing happens until find a great leader as a certainty.
Within your office he gives you the seal of authority.
One morning you cannot believe that it is for real,
The soldiers will follow the one who holds the seal.

Marriage:
Mandarin ducks in the river full of fish in the beginning.
When winds blow strong, even the pond has big waves attacking.
Scatter to different countries, where there’s water for swimming.
Find another love later and to the fisherman’s song you’re listening.

Children:
Peach and plum are in competition.
Also there is cinnamon most fragrant.
Dragon, Tiger, Goat, Monkey years most lucky,
Children and grandchildren are plenty.

Harvest:
When riding a horse to come home, unexpected, shocking events, truly.
By the riverside, what is it that’s there? It is the star of calamity.
If you foretell the future, it’s better to go into the mountains early.
You can still build the pavilion by the stream.
Always springtime, means many good times.

 

Style: Cloud and Fog over the Mountain Range
The clouds and the fog are deep in the mountain.
When you are holding the spear riding a horse alone, don’t think of going back.
When you can defeat a million, losing only three thousand on your side,
You can return home wearing purple and a golden belt.
When you dig into the rocks you can find jade.
When you plant flowers, they will become a forest.
In the battlefield there will be somebody pulling your hind legs.
When right time comes you will get someone who knows you well and fights with you.
