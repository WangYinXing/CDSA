Numeral 47    Wu Geng

Hexagram: Mountain on Lake

Stars

Heavenly Dispersing Star

Hour Pillar Geng Zi – Metal Rat Geng Yin – Metal Tiger
Metaphor Floating clouds hiding the moon. Yellow flowers everywhere in your garden.
Explanation Even though you are ambitious, you have to deal with setbacks and make a lot of effort to find success. Everything depends on hard work. Therefore, you need to try your best to make it successful.

Heavenly Worrying Star

Hour Pillar Geng Chen – Metal Dragon Geng Wu - Metal Horse
Metaphor Looking at the moon over the clouds. Withering buds.
Explanation Difficulties right now, there is hope in the future. A humble existence throughout life.

Heavenly Injury Star

Hour Pillar Geng Shen – Metal Monkey Geng Xu – Metal Dog
Metaphor Paper screen on the window attacked by rain. Winter plum coming into spring.
Explanation When there is a shallow foundation, you need to manage your expectations. When the good days are over, be prepared to manage your expectations.

Poetic Life Reading

Destiny:

Life is like dry germinating buds waiting for rain and bare branches waiting for spring. Whilst you are full of good intentions, things do not always go according to plan and what happens may be the opposite of what you intended. You help others, but what you get back is people blaming you and cursing you.  Fortune does not appear when it is supposed to.  Friends that you thought were intimate, turn out to be not really that intimate.  When you are in the city, your heart is at sea. There is a lot on your mind, so many reasons to worry. You try everything you can, but in the end you do not get good results. Therefore, avoid exhausting all your resources, and using up all your energy.  Let some things be. If you want to live with your spouse until you are old, do not be afraid to have a clattering good row, making a lot of noise to clear the air.


Luck Verses

Foundation:
The jade needs to be finely carved,
Even real gold still needs to be refined.
Other people help you to get to see the emperor.
Taking fairies to paradise, you can be the leader.

Life Path:
You must know that the arrow is in the hand of one who travels,
One day, the chance to ride a dragon to the jade capital.
Fortunately, you can be blessed by a person most noble.
There’s double glory to bring you success so wonderful.

Marriage:
Marriage, marriage, and then again marriage.
Mandarin ducks in places apart, sleeping.
Even though there’s sweet breeze and bright moon,
You are all alone, playing music and drinking.

Children:
Late spring, branches have good flowers blossoming.
In autumn there is one fruit hanging.
On the high terrace you want to receive good tidings.
Instead you hear the time teller at night, each hour announcing.

Harvest:
Year of Horse or Rabbit success is reached.
To go back to the mountains is your destiny.
Looking back in the old garden to see plum and peach,
Lots of guests in carriages make your home lively.

 

Style: Leading a Lone Army to the Outpost
The lone army is stationed at the outpost.
You are exhausted but the war is not ready.
Retreating spirit is governing your whole life, like the cloud overshadowing the moon.
All your relatives are as cold as ice.
You have experience of trying to be skillful, but it turns out to be clumsy.
You want peace, what you get is something shocking.
If you want your autumn heart to feel easy, the Rabbit has to follow the Rooster to see the bright moon again.

Commentary

Destiny

You have great expectations, yet it would be appropriate to take a relaxed approach. By accepting that there are some circumstances, which are often beyond your control you will save some of your energy.

Luck Verses

Foundation

You need to improve your skills (“jade needs to be finely carved, even real gold still needs to be refined”). To “get to see the emperor” means you will meet with the top person in an organization, and you get to manage a team (“taking fairies to paradise”).

Life Path

“The arrow is in the hand of the one who travels” implies the traveller holds the keys. To “ride a dragon to jade capital” means to travel luxuriously to the headquarters of an organization. You will be looked upon favourably and be highly successful.

Marriage

There are enjoyable times (“sweet breeze and bright moon”), yet relationships might not last (“mandarin ducks in places apart, sleeping”).

Children (‘Children’ can also mean subordinates or followers)

You start to have children when you are young (“late spring”) and the youngest son is born when you are older (“In autumn there is one fruit hanging”). Your children have their own lives and you look forward to hearing from them. For followers, you can gain good followers, yet some of them might be distant.

Harvest

You have achievement in a Horse year or Rabbit year. You will return to your hometown (“go back to the mountains”) and you welcome many visitors (“guests in carriages make your home lively”).

Style

You are waiting for action (“war is not ready”). Watch out for setbacks (“Retreating spirit”). Good times come in Rabbit years and Rooster years (“Rabbit has to follow the Rooster to see the bright moon again”).
