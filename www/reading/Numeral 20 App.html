Numeral 20    Yi Gui

Hexagram: Wind on Mountain

Heavenly Providence Star

Hour Pillar Gui Chou – Water Ox Gui Mao – Water Rabbit
Metaphor Wandering nightingale moving to a tree. Plum tree blossoming in the snow.
Explanation You can gain big profits by leaving home and climbing the corporate ladder step by step. Because you have solid roots, even in difficult circumstances you pull through unharmed.

Heavenly Snap Star

Hour Pillar Gui Si – Water Snake Gui Wei – Water Goat
Metaphor Rabbit on the moon. Filtering gold from sand.
Explanation Reputation gained exceeds the financial rewards. Avoid wasting your efforts. You make good efforts to manage your business, but you also need to manage your expectations.

Heavenly Sinking Star

Hour Pillar Gui You  – Water Rooster Gui Hai  – Water Pig
Metaphor Attempting to recover the dropped sword from the river by marking the spot on the boat. Pleasure boat travelling in the wind.
Explanation Avoid holding onto ideas as gospel. You need to let go of some thoughts to succeed. Be cautious about risks in your happiest moments.

 

Destiny:
This life is full of gossip and unpalatable rumors, which are hard to swallow. You are kind-hearted and your words are quite frank because you don’t hide anything. There is no way you are going to change your mind because of another’s opinions. You will not lose out to others. Life is not straightforward for you and you often wander about and go the long way round. Money comes from the east and goes to the west, readily passing through your hands. It is suitable for you to marry late and have children late. There will be sons followed by daughters. You like to learn a lot, yet your understanding is not profound. Despite making a lot of plans, not many will be followed through.

 

Luck Verses

Foundation: 
Controversy is with you, with you lifelong.
You’re like pine tree and bamboo, straight and strong.
Doing the right thing is what success depends upon.
Eventually, fame and fortune will come along. 

Life Path:
Trying to make the first move, like a hero.
On heavenly mountain, shoot three arrows,
For that is how your accomplishments grow.
You can enjoy the beauty of mountains and rivers,
All because you placed many bookmarks of plum flowers.

Marriage:
The wedding was a long time ago.
In the beginning get along well as a couple.
As time moves on, feel less content,
Maybe some regrets in the end.

Children:
Just after the rain, flowers bloom in fine weather.
Good fruits are expected, thereafter.
Petals blown away by night winds,
The prospect of many fruits rescinds.
Only one tree with leaves and flowers.

Harvest:
The time to go maybe Rooster or Monkey.
Bing and Ding years are full of gossip so nosy.
Leave the city to retire in the country,
Find a quiet place with lots of trees.
Bring a fishing rod to fish peacefully.

 

Style: Pine and Cedar Glorious at the Same Time
Take leave of your hometown.
Like the pine and bamboo you can withstand the extremes of hot and cold.
Wild goose flies away and leaves the flock early.
You may even part with your spouse early.
When the moon is at mid-sky clouds will disperse.
Over the pavilion you can enjoy the beautiful flowers,
Because the east wind blows several times, to bring the fairy from paradise to pick you up.
