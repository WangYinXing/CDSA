Numeral 100    Gui Gui

Hexagram: Mountain on Mountain

Stars

Cowboy Star

Year or Hour Pillar Gui Chou – Water Ox Gui Mao – Water Rabbit
Metaphor Watching the moon at dawn. Lighting a candle in the wind.
Explanation Avoid trying too hard when you are not encountering good days. The present fame and fortune might not be long lasting.

Heavenly Responsive Star

Year or Hour Pillar Gui Si – Water Snake Gui Wei – Water Goat
Metaphor Walking barefoot to hunt wild animals. Some fishes escape your net.
Explanation You stand up and show your ability and everybody will listen. When you can turn defeat into victory, everybody will adore you.

Heavenly Paradise Star

Year or Hour Pillar Gui You – Water Rooster Gui Hai – Water Pig
Metaphor Hibiscus under the autumn moon. The peach blossom floating in the waves.
Explanation After middle age there will be no worries. Although there are troubles there is no danger.

Poetic Life Reading

Destiny:

This life is like pine trees growing on the mountainside and like the bamboo growing tall. The root and the stem are precious, making your character very ethical, firm and determined. Born with a kind heart, your nature is to help under-privileged people and you enjoy helping people fight against injustice. Parents are like your honorable guests, they won’t bother to control your life. Siblings are like the moon behind the clouds, they are bright but they are not shining all the time.The people who are close to you are like the geese flying away. However, husband and wife are always together in the same nest. When you go home there is nothing to worry about. Children are not close to you, three keep a distance and two turn their backs. Don’t complain about the bickering in your life, after the storm you will sail in a peaceful sea.


Luck Verses

Foundation:
Born into a famous family, you can compete.
From a wealthy background, you’re in the elite.
Work hard and pass examinations.
Then the future is like sea and sky, immeasurable.
At age forty-five you’re prosperous and successful.

Life Path:
The first half of your life you have gone through a lot,
Happily encountering fame and fortune was the upshot.
Gold is refined and jade is polished, well known and affluent.
In late years, you should be most grateful and give thanks to heaven.

Marriage:
Whilst the sun is bright, thick clouds bring unexpected shower.
After a thousand miles, bright moon you will encounter.
In a distant land, again you come together.
Nobody disturbs you when closing the door to your chamber.
Protected from disturbance outside the window, there’s no bother.

Children:
When flowers bloom wind and rain come.
Two branches are good in the back garden.
Orchids and mulberry are very lovely.
Enjoy a wonderful life under the sky.

Harvest:
In those days you had great ambition.
Kun fish to big Peng bird transformation,
Over nine states big Peng is flying.
Rooster and Dog years you’re laughing,
To east mountain where the fairies staying,
There a great time you’ll be enjoying.

 

Style: Pine Trees Deep in the Mountains
Whilst you are young, you are hard-working.
Then you know fame and fortune come together.
As long as the roots are unbroken, the grass at the front of your house will grow.
But you need to plant the plum tree on the mountain with seeds.
When the flowers wither away, don’t complain because a fruit will come early.
When the plum is blossoming, don’t complain about the moon coming late.
When is it a good time in life?
Just wait for the dragon and tiger to roar.

Commentary

Destiny

You believe in doing the right thing, so follow your heart. There is no need to look to your relatives for approval. Life will be calm in the end.

Luck Verses

Foundation

There are a lot of opportunities (“future is like sea and sky, immeasurable”), if you gain qualifications.

Life Path

You can enjoy success later in life, having dealt with challenges earlier.

Marriage

There is disruption to a good relationship (“sun is bright, thick clouds bring unexpected shower”). After being apart, you get back together (“after a thousand miles, bright moon you will encounter”). You enjoy an intimate relationship as a couple.

Children (‘Children’ can also mean subordinates or followers)

There might be some difficulties (“wind and rain”) when it comes to having children, yet it turns out well and they arrive. “Orchid and mulberry are very lovely,” suggests that your children are attractive and pleasant.

Harvest

When the mythological creature, Kun as a fish, turns into big bird Peng, it symbolizes someone who is able to adapt and go far as a result. “Over the nine states big Peng is flying,” means you cover the whole country. “To east mountain where the fairies are staying,” suggests that you go to a wonderful place where you enjoy yourself.

Style

It is easy for you to flourish close to home (“as long as the roots are unbroken, the grass at the front of your house will grow”). If you have bigger ambitions you need to start from scratch, without any prior advantage (“But you need to plant the plum tree on the mountain with seeds”). “When the flowers wither away, don’t complain because a fruit will come early. When the plum is blossoming, don’t complain about the moon coming late,” means that even if it is not what you expect, events will turn out well. “Just wait for the dragon and tiger to roar” implies that you will have a good time throughout your life, because they roar regularly.
