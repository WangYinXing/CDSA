Numeral 61    Geng Jia

Hexagram:  Lake on Thunder

Large Tying Rope Star

Hour Pillar Jia Zi – Wood Rat Jia Yin – Wood Tiger
Metaphor Autumn swallow returning to nest. Draw a mark on your boat to identify the spot where there is a lot of fish, so you know where to come back to fish again.
Explanation Diligent throughout your life. Need to manage your expectations. Avoid wasting your energy, with ideas that don’t work.

Heavenly Slowing Star

Hour Pillar Jia Chen – Wood Dragon Jia Wu – Wood Horse
Metaphor Orchid growing in the garden. Pull down the blind to fish from the inside cabin.
Explanation Destiny says you have an honorable son who can bring prosperity to your family and establish his own career. You do not like to expose whatever you are doing.

Heavenly West Star

Hour Pillar Jia Shen – Wood Monkey Jia Xu – Wood Dog
Metaphor Fish with beautiful scales in a deserted well. Bright sun shining on the curtain.
Explanation Your great talent has yet to meet opportunity. However, you can apply yourself to the safekeeping of the business at home. Happiness is right in front of you.

 

Destiny:
This is a life that is full of great plans and high ambitions. Your character is natural like an undecorated tree, free of artificial ornaments.  In whatever you do you have a mature approach. Like shallow waters in autumn, your relationship with your relatives does not run deep. Like cold charcoal in winter, your relationship with your brothers and sisters lacks warmth. Even though you are well organized and plan ahead there are still lots of ups and downs. You can waste time in getting approval. Whenever you do people a favor there is a lack of kindness in return.  Like setting the house on fire when you burn incense, your good deeds bring you misfortune. It can happen that there are delays to good things coming your way and opportunities can be missed and lost. If you want to have your heart’s content ride the Horse and Dragon, for in these years your wishes will be fulfilled.


Luck Verses

Foundation:
Sampan on dry land cannot row anywhere,
So might as well use fishing rod right here.
Like jade buried in green field for many a year,
You’re appreciated when those from afar appear.
People nearby, those around locally,
Don’t discover your precious ability.

Life Path:
By chance hit the target with your arrow,
Hit the valuable, golden animal.
All of a sudden, in a strange land, a noble person you will encounter.
You’re expected to return to heaven after your earthly adventure1
In fairyland heaven, the trees are always in spring,
It is all magnificent, every single thing.  

Marriage:
Orchid on rocks, shallow roots grow not deeply.
Past good deeds keeps orchid anchored firmly.
Water fowls living in great company.
Moon shining bright makes awesome autumn lights.

Children:
One twig of fabulous flowers outside the wall,
Unexpectedly, meets wind and rainfall.
Mandarin ducks play at water junction of Snake and Dragon.
Flourishing peaches and plums means many children.

Harvest:
In Horse or Ox year you can be a military general.
In Dragon or Tiger year go to the mountains to repose.
In the southern chamber lay on the magical pillow
To dream amazing dreams, wake up feeling hollow.
Sound of flying geese is clear and rings out across the universe.

 

Style: Fishing in Clear Waters
The ancient deserted house cannot attract swallows to nest.
Whenever you meet the bridge there is a road leading to the heaven pavilion.
In Ren Wu year there is fame and fortune.
You have to wait with firmness on the fishing palace.
Let the tale of chicken ribs be a reminder for you not to be too clever.
To win a competition is not really a good thing because over-exposing your talent can bring misfortune.
In the courtyard, if you ask about when the east wind, which brings good tidings, arrives at your house.
The answer is you have to plant flowers in a dangerous field, for then it will blossom on the entire tree.

 

Footnote 1
A Chinese belief is that fairies who commit misdeeds in heaven receive a penalty of being sent to earth to live a human life. When they complete their time on earth they can return to heaven.  Life on earth is much harder than living in heaven, therefore the implication is that the person works hard.
