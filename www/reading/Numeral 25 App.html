Numeral 25    Bing Wu

Hexagram: Fire on Mountain

 

Heavenly Abundance Star

Hour Pillar Wu Zi – Earth Rat Wu Yin – Earth Tiger
Metaphor Open the bottle and drink alone. Watching the moon in the snow.
Explanation You cope well and are naturally happy. Although you come from a modest family background, you enjoy the fortune of an easy life.

 

Heavenly Wealth Star

Hour Pillar Wu Chen – Earth Dragon Wu Wu – Earth Horse
Metaphor Thirsty horse drinking from the spring. Planting corn in the hot summer.
Explanation When you are exhausted you arrive at a beautiful place. If you work hard now, you will reap the rewards later.

 

Heavenly Brightness Star

Hour Pillar Wu Shen – Earth Monkey Wu Xu – Earth Dog
Metaphor Frost in summer. Yellow chrysanthemums standing in the cold in late autumn.
Explanation During those times when you are trying hard, be prepared for the unexpected. Since you are virtuous and you stand on your principles, you enjoy a good retirement.

Destiny:
Your life is peaceful, it is like the spring wind blowing on your face. Everyone is your friend. You are very ambitious.  You know the long and short of it, and you know what is serious and what is light-hearted. Able to negotiate well, you are a good mediator. In times of difficulty, you know how to solve problems.  You can run a business in hard times. Although you are impatient, you are kind-hearted and there is never anything evil in your heart.  Nobody can say you lack good taste, even if you live in a shabby house, you appreciate music, art, playing chess and reading books. Your sense of refinement is such that it is important to you to wear good jewelry of gold and jade, even if you do not possess the finest robes. You hope everything is in order, but unfortunately, there are setbacks in your life. However, when facing adversity you only need to be firm and true to yourself, then spring will come and the flowers will blossom.

Luck Verses

Foundation:
Like pine tree and bamboo you stand upright,
Withstand wind and cold, day and night.
You have ambition, endure bitter winter cold.
Eventually, your house is filled with gold.
Enjoy watching the beauty and serenity unfold,
From on top of the world, when you are old.

Life Path:
Ups and downs when you are young, don’t worry.
In middle-age, success comes out of failure surely.
Fame and fortune comes in your late years.
Enjoy life in twilight years, no doubts, no fears.

Marriage:
Beneath the bamboo and parasol tree, a happy marriage.
Outside your fence, the wild flowers are blossoming.
Getting close to seduce you, luring and enticing.
The couple escapes to the mountains in a blue carriage.

Children:
In the heavy rain, one fruit means one son.
Even if not so many children,
Still have joy of grandchildren.
Goat and Dog years bring descendants.

Harvest:
In Horse year you don’t believe the danger ahead is there.
In the second half of the Pig year, take lots of extra care.
You catch a deer, but down the hillside it disappears.
The orchids outside your window can withstand the rain, no fear.

Style: Wild Goose Chasing Phoenix
The distant mountain is coming out from behind the clouds.
Like the wild goose flies high to chase the phoenix, you have high ambitions to get something good.
Don’t complain that the gold is buried beneath the earth.
You will meet noble people who will give you a hand.
You want to be like a pine tree, standing up straight in the bitter cold winter.
If you want to ask when will the flower bear fruit?
You need only one branch to bring the spring inside your fence.
