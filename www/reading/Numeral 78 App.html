Numeral 78    Xin Xin

Hexagram: Heaven on Heaven

Heavenly Protection Star

Year or Hour Pillar Xin Chou – Metal Ox Xin Mao – Metal Rabbit
Metaphor Drawing a picture of a biscuit when you are hungry. Skillful technician lamenting on the lack of materials to finish the job.
Explanation Career becomes empty at the blink of an eye. Cherish you ability, even when there is a lack of opportunity.

Heavenly Trapped Star

Year or Hour Pillar Xin Si – Metal Snake Xin Wei – Metal Goat
Metaphor Watching the moon late at night. Meeting the rain over cold pond.
Explanation The situation is modest in late life. Situation is humble and there is a lot on your mind.

Heavenly Secret Star

Year or Hour Pillar Xin You – Metal Rooster Xin Hai – Metal Pig
Metaphor Plum blossom in a vase. Spring flowers and autumn moon.
Explanation Life is challenging. Beware of bringing misfortune to your spouse. From youth to old age, everything is as you wish.

 

Destiny:
You establish a career, which is not based on your inheritance. How you make a living is independent of your relatives. The means for your lifestyle comes like the grass in spring, which does not need to be planted and sprouts out by itself. Don’t cry over spilt milk. You have to trust that accumulating bit by bit, you gain a lot. No need to work too hard to find what you want because it won’t come to you when it is not the right time. Even if you walk so far as to wear out a pair of iron shoes, if it is not the time you won’t find what you are looking for.  When the right time comes, things come to you automatically.

Luck Verses

Foundation: 
Water hibiscus opens up in the pond.
Bamboo and pine grow in the snow.
When the time comes, the wood will become a pillar and beams.
Waiting for a fish in the woods, not much misfortune it seems.
No need to put yourself in danger,
What you want won’t come sooner.

Life Path:
When luck is with you, get a huge fortune, get richer.
Goat and Rooster years come out of the dust, go into water.
Friends who understand you will shoot an arrow to the sky.
Sit comfortably on the fairy pond and enjoy a peaceful life.

Marriage:
In spring two peaches bloom means two romances, two bonds.
Mandarin ducks under the moon won’t stay together for long.
Separation, another relationship comes along after it ends.
In later years, enjoy an active life amongst friends.

Children:
In famous garden, flowers will bring fruit that’s outstanding.
Unripe green and ripe yellow fruits are on the branches, hanging.
Good situation, nightingales and swallows are singing.
When swallows are gone with spring, with autumn geese are coming.

Harvest:
Dragon is flying amidst the thunder in the heavens.
Dragon’s head and Goat’s horns are times for separation.
Like the old days, from green mountain to distant lands water flowing through.
After your dreams, don’t remember the sound of the cuckoo.

 

Style: Medicinal Mushrooms Growing on Rocks
Gold ornaments must be refined.
Medicinal mushrooms upon rocks have strange roots.
Goose flying alone will not come back.
You should cross the path in the sky.
What about your career in the old days, where is it now?
The east wind simply blasts it away.
Just look at your back garden to examine the fruit trees there.
In late years, there will be a cinnamon tree that gives out good fragrance.
