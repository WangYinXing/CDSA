Numeral 77    Xin Geng

Hexagram:  Heaven on Lake

Stars

Heavenly Penalty Star

Hour Pillar Geng Zi – Metal Rat Geng Yin – Metal Tiger
Metaphor Butterfly fluttering amongst the flowers. Fishing on a forest of rocks.
Explanation Appear busy, but what is achieved is another story. A lot of barriers to overcome, success is not easy.

Heavenly Lonesome Star

Hour Pillar Geng Chen – Metal Dragon Geng Wu - Metal Horse
Metaphor Green pine in the snow. Sun in early morning, shining through the forest.
Explanation Lose your ambition, enjoy a wonderful retirement life. Working very hard in early days.

Heavenly Phoenix Star

Hour Pillar Geng Shen – Metal Monkey Geng Xu – Metal Dog
Metaphor Playing the musical instrument inside the tent. Playing the musical instrument in the snow.
Explanation Very clever and full of wise plans, therefore can have great achievement. Getting joy, no misfortune, no harm.

Poetic Life Reading

Destiny:

In this life you have to make an effort in planning and executing the plans.  There are a lot of challenges to face and you have to work hard. You do everything by yourself and set your mind to the tasks at hand. Although you have a lot of acquaintances, you don‘t have a lot of bosom friends.  Since you don’t appreciate the beauty of flowers, you don’t get up early in spring, but you love the moon, so you go to bed late at night. This means you can enjoy success late in life. Do you have the money to ensure a good supply of the seven essential ingredients for a household (firewood, rice, oil, salt, soy sauce, vinegar, tea)? Apart from yourself, who else will know? You have a lot of opportunities to work hard outside. It is rare for you to enjoy life quietly at home.

Luck Verses

Foundation:
Don’t mind too much if there’s often uncertainty,
Since life can be but a dream that’s empty.
All you need is to raise your fist on firm ground.
Someone superior will make a decision that’s sound.

Life Path:
Ten year luck comes with Snake and Dog decades.
When the ice melts, fire is burning there will be light.
Pass examinations, as news spreads, your name is known.
Where foreigners invade borders, the important war zone,
Shoot three arrows, kill enemy leader.
All is quiet, peace reigns, war is over.

Marriage:
A past life destines the mandarin ducks a couple thereafter.
Even though you may separate, eventually you’ll be together.
Congratulations for your wedding, many friends gather.
Moon shines upon pond, where beauty of lilies glitters.

Children:
By the wall, flowers blossoming, three fruits ripen.
When you are fifty, children go and children come.
Standing upright on three legs is the caldron,
Where mother is cooking, for her little ones.

Harvest:
Ox and Horse years, good times you’ll be having.
Alas, the door is closed in the evening.
On solitary night, star and moon fading.
Don’t be sad when spring is over,
Autumn has arrived, this is nature.


Style: Fish and Dragon Playing in the Waves.
When relatives come face to face, you don’t recognize each other.
Half the reason is because you have been apart for a long time.
Half of the reason is because the relationship lacks real sentiment.
Wild geese fly over wilderness in autumn.
Fish and dragon are mixed up in the waves.
To shoot monkey instead of rabbit is wasting effort.
Trying to fish under the same tree is a waste of time.
Eventually, when you come to five lakes under bright moon, you can fly straight to the palace on the moon.
You have to be at the right place at right time to be successful.

Commentary

Destiny

In your later years you can reap the rewards of your hard work.

Luck Verses

Foundation

The time will come when someone in a leadership position will point you in the right direction. Meanwhile, remain determined (“raise your fist on firm ground”).

Life Path

“Ten-year luck comes with Snake and Dog decades,” means Snake years or Dog years mark the start of ten years of good luck. “When ice melts, fire is burning there will be light” suggests that you will be noticed and gain recognition. “Where foreigners invade borders, the important war zone, shoot three arrows, kill enemy leader,” implies that you are a good fighter and can defeat the enemy. This is a metaphor saying that you can compete and win.

Marriage

A happy marriage is pre-destined due to past life connections. “Moon shines upon pond, where beauty of lilies glitters,” suggests many good times.

Children (‘Children’ can also mean subordinates or followers)

Three children are predicted. In your later years your children still come back home and enjoy spending time with the family. For followers, some may come and go, yet there will be some loyal ones.

Harvest

“Alas, the door is closed in the evening, on solitary night, star and moon fading,” implies that after you have enjoyed good luck, luck can decrease. Time is forever moving, in a cycle (“spring is over, autumn has arrived”) and it would be wise to accept that life changes.

Style

You will be traveling into new territory (“Wild geese fly over wilderness in autumn”) and you need to know who is who (“Fish and dragon are mixed up in the waves”). There is no point in trying to pursue something, which is not right for you (“To shoot monkey instead of rabbit is wasting effort” and “Trying to fish under the same tree is a waste of time”).
