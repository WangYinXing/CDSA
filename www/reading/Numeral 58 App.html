Numeral 58    Ji Xin

Hexagram: Earth on Heaven

Stars

Heavenly Bank Star

Hour Pillar Xin Chou – Metal Ox Xin Mao – Metal Rabbit
Metaphor Dry well receiving rain. Plant pine trees in deserted garden.
Explanation When you are miserable there are good people coming your way. You make an effort developing your business, in the end it is rewarding.

Heavenly Injury Star

Hour Pillar Xin Si – Metal Snake Xin Wei – Metal Goat
Metaphor Watching the moon from the pavilion. Lotus blooming in the urn.
Explanation You can expect good results. Manage your expectations of prosperity.

Heavenly Witch Star 

Hour Pillar Xin You – Metal Rooster Xin Hai – Metal Pig
Metaphor Rat in the barn. Crane standing on pine tree.
Explanation You can expect an affluent lifestyle. You have a disciplined character, so suitable to set foot on higher land.

Poetic Life Reading

Destiny:

When this life is examined in detail, it looks like someone performing on top of a pole, there is no stable footing. It is also like building a house by the sea, even with a pillar it will not be safe. Whether you are going forward or backward there will be a lot of uncertainty.  Relatives cannot give you a helping hand. Siblings will become your enemies, so it is suitable to start all over again outside. Never depend on your family business.  Everybody from afar respects you.  Those who are close to you, treat you as if you are of no importance. Even when you are successful do not show off too much, otherwise people be laughing at you. If you leave your hometown, to your surprise there will be honorable people to help you.


Luck Verses

Foundation: 
Gold and treasure grow automatically.
By middle age a lot of things waiting for you.
Ambition to establish something? Follow through.
Then, mulberry trees grow fully, clouds make way for the moon.

Life Path:
A lot of changes to your doors because you have to travel,
You have to go to the water concourse to meet people.
Someone who appreciates your song, with musical instrument plays along.
About drinking alone, never worry, plenty of company.

Marriage:
In the sand dunes mandarin ducks and water fowls gather.
Before flying to the five lakes, they group together. 
Row your boat through weeds, to middle of the lake steer.
Drop the net to catch fish under the bright moon in autumn.
To find the right one requires a lot of determination.

Children:
After the flowers have withered,
One fruit on the branch means one son.
Because of many good deeds you’ve done,
Your only son gives you grandchildren.

Harvest:
Do not travel along the risky pathway, risky roads.
On horseback or in a boat, shocking events are close.
If you come across danger, there’s fortune that follows.
Because of the fortunate star being present,
Come back to life in a deadly situation.


Style: Planting a Pine Tree on the Rocks
The pine tree branches reach up in the sky because of the firm character.
You can stand the cold weather naturally.
Your great talent will be appreciated eventually.
Floating in the waves you have to let others go first.
There are four or five flowers blossoming in autumn.
There are two or three rows of flying geese at sunset.
If you can foretell success and failure in your life then build your boat and travel to a beautiful land. 

Commentary

Destiny

Since your family background is unlikely to be beneficial to you, it would be a good idea to leave your hometown. You can find success and enjoy life more when you go elsewhere.

Luck Verses

Foundation

You can go for your goals and succeed eventually. “Mulberry trees grow fully, clouds make way for the moon” implies that you will prosper and problems will disappear.

Life Path

“A lot of changes to your doors because you have to travel,” means you change residences many times due to travelling around. To “go to the water concourse to meet people” can be taken literally, or it could imply that you go to a busy place. You can work together with someone who appreciates you (“appreciates your song, with musical instrument plays along”).

Marriage

Even though there are eligible singles around, you need make some effort when it comes to love and marriage (“row your boat through weeds”). “Drop the net to catch fish under bright moon in autumn” alludes to you finding the right partner in your middle-aged (autumn) years.

Children (‘Children’ can also mean subordinates or followers)

One son is predicted, and there will be grandchildren because you generated good karma with charitable acts.

Harvest

Be careful when it comes to taking risks. Your luck is such that you can recover from dangerous situations.

Style

You are determined and ambitious (like the pine tree), with the ability to withstand adversity (cold weather). “Floating in the waves you have to let others go first” suggests that you need to be patient and wait until the time is right. You have success in your later years (“flowers blossoming in autumn”) and life becomes more organised (like geese flying in formation). If you know what is good for you, be prepared to travel.
