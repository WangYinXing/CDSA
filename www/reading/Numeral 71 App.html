Numeral 71    Xin Jia

Hexagram:  Heaven on Thunder

Stars

Heavenly Party of Officers Star

Hour Pillar Jia Zi – Wood Rat Jia Yin – Wood Tiger
Metaphor Woodcutter singing in the snow. Fish from the well is getting out of the swamp.
Explanation A humble life, but you amuse yourself very well. In the midst of difficulties for a while. There is hope for becoming prosperous.

Heavenly Door Star

Hour Pillar Jia Chen – Wood Dragon Jia Wu – Wood Horse
Metaphor Fishing over cold pond. Watching the moon as dawn approaches.
Explanation Even though you work hard, you need to manage your expectations when it comes to doing what is unfamiliar. Avoid trying too hard when you are not encountering good days.

Heavenly Red Jade Star 

Hour Pillar Jia Shen – Wood Monkey Jia Xu – Wood Dog
Metaphor Withered willow encountering frost. Moon shines over pine in spring.
Explanation Be careful during challenging times. You are mature when you are young. Someone appreciates your ability.

Poetic Life Reading

Destiny:

This life is like the blossom in the spring forest and like the moon in autumn; the timing looks favorable. Although the colors are beautiful in the scenery, it turns out to be fruitless.  Living in modest conditions, the means of making a living is dried up. You have to work very hard during daylight hours and spend a lot of time thinking in the evening.  When you are advancing you are thinking of retreating. Because you are frank and can offend people with your words, you need to watch what you say in certain situations. Thereby, you can avoid your efforts being in vain, and your diligent nature will not be wasted.  Nevertheless, ignoble people will be dissatisfied, they think you are not doing enough. Relationship with relatives is like melting snow in late spring, cold when it should be warm.  Relationship with siblings is like the reflection of flowers in the mirror, lacking in real sentiment.    

Luck Verses

Foundation:
Watch the risky tower from afar, watch it wisely.
Don’t approach the perilous place, don’t be greedy.
Don’t go in for the riches inside, don’t do anything risky.
When once again spring comes around again,
Good tidings from dragon gate, suddenly then.

Life Path:
Many sources of wealth, to ask there is no need.
Success and failure comes naturally, no worry.
Right ahead, the road you see is lined with mulberry trees.
On jade pavilion enjoy bright moon and cool breeze.

Marriage:
Travel a long way, return home after long absence.
Marriage intact, being away no interference.
Matchmaker brings you good tidings, good communication.
Mandarin ducks play under flowers without any suspicion.

Children:
Outside the wall, one flower, one fruit, hanging.
Trouble with rain and wind when blossoming.
In later years, watch son having great achievement.
Orchid and jade blossoming within the fence; grandchildren.

Harvest:
You are determined to live a long life.
Watch out for the Rooster by the water.
A picturesque scene of blue water, green mountain,
Under the moon, to crane and baboon listen.

 

Style: Clouds over the Terraced City
With thick clouds over the end of the horizon,
When you are looking west at the capital city, you cannot see your home.
When spring is still chilly and flowers are blossoming, cold rain at night is unwelcome.
In late autumn the lonely flying goose is landing on the cold sand.
When it comes to other people who do not have children, they can make changes more freely.
Yet you cannot take such risks, you have to work hard and wear out your shoes.
What is good about this life? Where can you enjoy life?
In the little temple under bamboo and pine shadow,
Children have own life and cannot take care of you.

Commentary

Destiny

Not expecting too much from others and being careful in what you say will help you get the most out of life.

Luck Verses

Foundation

It would be wise to not rush into a risky proposition. Stand back and observe the situation. You will hear of another opportunity at the right place (“dragon gate”).

Life Path

There are various ways of making money. Despite ups and downs, you find that you are on the road to success (“mulberry trees”). Eventually, you will enjoy good fortune in a luxurious place (“jade pavilion”).

Marriage

You might spend a lot of time apart from your spouse due to work. “Matchmaker brings you good tidings” means you only hear good things from other people whilst you are away. You maintain a happy marriage (“mandarin ducks play under flowers without suspicion”).

Children (‘Children’ can also mean subordinates or followers)

One daughter (flower) and one son (fruit) are predicted. There might be difficulties at first with having children (“trouble with rain and wind when blossoming”). You will enjoy the company of your grandchildren (“orchid and jade blossoming within the fence”). For followers, they will turn out to be highly capable (“having great achievement”).

Harvest

“Watch out for the Rooster by the water,” could have various meanings. It could mean to be careful near water during a Rooster year, or it could mean to be cautious in a Water Rooster year. You enjoy a retirement in a pleasant place.

Style

You end up a long way from your hometown (“you cannot see your home”). Be cautious in your youth (“spring is still chilly”). You go a long way on your own (“In late autumn the lonely flying goose is landing on the cold sand”). To “wear out your shoes” implies you work very hard going back and forth. To enjoy life in “the little temple under bamboo and pin shadow,” means retiring to a quiet place.
