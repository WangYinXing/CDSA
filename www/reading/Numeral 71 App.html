Numeral 71    Xin Jia

Hexagram:  Heaven on Thunder

Heavenly Party of Officers Star

Hour Pillar Jia Zi – Wood Rat Jia Yin – Wood Tiger
Metaphor Woodcutter singing in the snow. Fish from the well is getting out of the swamp.
Explanation A humble life, but you amuse yourself very well. In the midst of difficulties for a while. There is hope for becoming prosperous.

Heavenly Door Star

Hour Pillar Jia Chen – Wood Dragon Jia Wu – Wood Horse
Metaphor Fishing over cold pond. Watching the moon as dawn approaches.
Explanation Even though you work hard, you need to manage your expectations when it comes to doing what is unfamiliar. Avoid trying too hard when you are not encountering good days.

Heavenly Red Jade Star 

Hour Pillar Jia Shen – Wood Monkey Jia Xu – Wood Dog
Metaphor Withered willow encountering frost. Moon shines over pine in spring.
Explanation Be careful during challenging times. You are mature when you are young. Someone appreciates your ability.

 

Destiny:
This life is like the blossom in the spring forest and like the moon in autumn; the timing looks favorable. Although the colors are beautiful in the scenery, it turns out to be fruitless.  Living in modest conditions, the means of making a living is dried up. You have to work very hard during daylight hours and spend a lot of time thinking in the evening.  When you are advancing you are thinking of retreating. Because you are frank and can offend people with your words, you need to watch what you say in certain situations. Thereby, you can avoid your efforts being in vain, and your diligent nature will not be wasted.  Nevertheless, ignoble people will be dissatisfied, they think you are not doing enough. Relationship with relatives is like melting snow in late spring, cold when it should be warm.  Relationship with siblings is like the reflection of flowers in the mirror, lacking in real sentiment.

Harvest:

Style: Clouds over the Terraced City
With thick clouds over the end of the horizon,
When you are looking west at the capital city, you cannot see your home.
When spring is still chilly and flowers are blossoming, cold rain at night is unwelcome.
In late autumn the lonely flying goose is landing on the cold sand.
When it comes to other people who do not have children, they can make changes more freely.
Yet you cannot take such risks, you have to work hard and wear out your shoes.
What is good about this life? Where can you enjoy life?
In the little temple under bamboo and pine shadow,
Children have own life and cannot take care of you.
