Numeral 45    Wu Wu

Hexagram: Mountain on Mountain

Heavenly Noble Star

Year or Hour Pillar Wu Zi – Earth Rat Wu Yin – Earth Tiger
Metaphor Cool breeze under the moon. Decayed chrysanthemums meeting the frost.
Explanation A comfortable lifestyle. No worries throughout life. Beware of adversity in late years.

Heavenly Confusion Star

Year or Hour Pillar Wu Chen – Earth Dragon Wu Wu – Earth Horse
Metaphor Roll down the blind and drink alone. Going hunting and meeting the tiger.
Explanation Enjoy leisure in the woods, but it is unsuitable to go to faraway places. When you work far away, you will get great profits.

Heavenly Literary Star

Year or Hour Pillar Wu Shen – Earth Monkey Wu Xu – Earth Dog
Metaphor A lot of rivers flowing into the sea. Fishing on a sampan.
Explanation You are broad-minded and hold a high position. Work hard throughout life and manage without much help from others.

 

Destiny
This life is like a plant that germinates but doesn’t flower, or if the flowers do blossom there are no fruits. Sometimes there are a lot of leaves and a lot of flowers. Sometimes there are a lot of dark clouds without rain. On the outside you are as beautiful as flowers and leaves. On the inside you have tiger’s fur with a lambs-wool lining, tough and tender at the same time. Alas, you only make a paper boxing champion. You can make thirty cents, but you lose two dollars. In a war you may kill a million, but you lose three thousand yourself. You are merciful in rescuing others, but unfortunately what you plan is mostly unsuccessful. Nevertheless, whatever misfortune you encounter becomes fortunate in the end. What is fortunate in the beginning will become empty in the end. Don’t complain about walking an extra mile because you will leave misfortune behind.

 

Luck Verses

Foundation:
In the autumn, wild geese fly in a flock,
Three of them will leave, they head for the north.
Lone cloud tries to block broad heavenly path.
One bright moon shines in all its glory.

Life Path:
Early success or early failure, don’t grumble.
In the end all failures become successful.
Sometimes you should stand alone on autumn river.
You can pick the right path, you have control.

Marriage:
A past life bond means spouse is pre-destined.
Fall for one who has faults, but don’t complain.
Marrying not for love is ill-omened,
What follows could be tragic with much pain.

Children:
Firstly, flowers on the branch will blossom.
In autumn, fruit bears you a very good son.
The color of the fruit is deep yellow.
Whatever difficulties, your son can handle.

Harvest:
In Tiger year or Dragon year take care.
Don’t catch cold wind and rain, beware.
Blessings from fortunate gods are needed.
Recall the dream in autumn whilst you’re inebriated.


Style: Standing at the Red Door Alone.
The wind and the rain are cold and sad.
Lonely wife standing against the red railings with the door to the balcony half open.
Half-hearted, she wonders about when the husband will return on horseback.
Life is like an urn with three legs.
Family is like flowers on separate stems.
You have to separate because of your karma, for the time being.
Don’t even ask when you will be together, heaven will not leak its secret to you.
