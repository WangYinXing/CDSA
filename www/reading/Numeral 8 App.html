Numeral 8    Jia Xin

Hexagram: Thunder on Heaven

Stars

Golden Phoenix Star

Hour Pillar Xin Chou – Metal Ox Xin Mao – Metal Rabbit
Metaphor Fish swimming in spring. Dried up sapling, rotting away.
Explanation When you are successful, stay alert and expect the unexpected. Watch out for obstacles.

Jade Hall Star

Hour Pillar Xin Si – Metal Snake Xin Wei – Metal Goat
Metaphor Chrysanthemums by the roadside. Planting corn in the hot summer.
Explanation You can enjoy a good old age. Difficulties in the beginning, but it will turn out good in the end.

Golden Pavilion Star

Hour Pillar Xin You  – Metal Rooster Xin Hai – Metal Pig
Metaphor Fox borrowing the tiger’s coat of terror. Digging out treasure.
Explanation You have the support of your bosses and can scare people in your boss’s name. You can gain unexpected wealth.

Poetic Life Reading

Destiny:

Out of an ordinary bird’s nest get a phoenix.  Like getting a pearl out of a clam, you come from the grassroots to have great achievement.  It is suitable to leave your hometown for you cannot follow father’s profession.  However, you can carve your own career.  You heart is most kind, yet your well meaning words can come across as blunt in speech.  You tend to be relaxed about big things, yet you stress about small things.   There is always something on your mind.  You experience difficulty in the beginning and prosperity in the end.

 

Luck Verses

Foundation: 
A piece of metal can be hammered into shape.
There are plants that grow on rocks.
A wealthy family with a majestic home,
Will help you establish a career of your own.

Life Path:
Rat year and Ox year are lucky times.
You can reach the phoenix pond,
Where political power can be gained,
And prosperity obtained.

Marriage:
Musical instruments are dusty, due to lack of play.
How sad that your young spouse passed away.
Other opportunities cannot compare,
To those happy, early days of tender loving care.

Children:
The tree blows in the wind,
One fruit falls to the ground.
What do Goat or Dog years bring?
Birth of your only offspring. 

Harvest:
Goat years and Dog years are lucky, most promising.
You get good views of the vast territory with Ren and Ding.
Steer your boat with the wind to get to where you are going.


Style: Moon Hiding Behind Clouds
The moon is bright, the clouds are dark.
The wind blows into the big river, making waves, unsettling the water.
You have to prepare for becoming successful out of many failures.
Be careful, when you catch a pair of carps, for you only get one.
Like lightning, the shimmering candle cannot regain light.
The body is stable, heart is unsettled.
When you ask what does the setting sun look like?
It is like the petals falling onto the path in the evening wind.
When you need to work hard, don’t complain.
When you have some success don’t boast.
At age seventy-two you can count the flowers under the moon in spring,
For then you will find out just how much success you have.

Commentary

Destiny

You can overcome challenges to become successful. You would be better off not to be involved in a family business or to follow the same career as your parents.

Luck Verses

Foundation

Just as “a piece of metal can be hammered into shape”, you can create a new self-image. You can thrive under challenging circumstances, like “plants that grow on rocks”. There will be mentors who can help you establish your career.

Life Path

To reach “phoenix pond” means you can reach the top of your field. You can be influential as well as being affluent.

Marriage

When a couple play musical instruments together it is symbolic of a harmonious marriage. “Musical instruments are dusty” alludes to a couple not being together. In the olden days this was usually due to the early death of the spouse. In modern times, it can be due to a relationship break-up. You will have other romantic opportunities, yet you will tend to hanker for your first love.

Children (‘Children’ can also mean subordinates or followers)

Fruits refer to children. You are most likely to have a child in a Goat or Dog year. For followers, you can meet a loyal follower in a Goat or Dog year.

Harvest

It is a good idea to make plans during Ren years or Ding years. Ren years are those ending with digit 2 (e.g. 1992, 2002, 2012, 2022). Ding years are those ending with digit 7 (e.g. 1997, 2007, 2017, 2027). “Steer your boat with the wind” can mean you need to keep up the pace with your plans, or it can mean to go with the flow

Style

Be mindful of problems, “dark clouds” coming during good times, “bright moon”. There could be some ups and downs. You have to prepare for becoming successful out of many failures. Be aware that there will be times when you don’t get everything you expect. You will be able to look back on a successful life.
