Numeral 88    Ren Xin

Hexagram: Water on Heaven

Stars

Greedy Wolf Star

Hour Pillar Xin Chou – Metal Ox Xin Mao – Metal Rabbit
Metaphor Shoot through the clouds with one arrow. Barn is full from a good harvest.
Explanation Forget about academic studies, you can learn military skills that take you sky high. Born with a silver spoon, there is no worry whatsoever.

Heavenly Jade Plate Star

Hour Pillar Xin Si – Metal Snake Xin Wei – Metal Goat
Metaphor Ox sleeping on spring grass. Planting leaves on a rock.
Explanation You live well. Happiness throughout your life. Work hard throughout life, but be cautious about the expected reward.

Heavenly Celebratory Star

Hour Pillar Xin You – Metal Rooster Xin Hai – Metal Pig
Metaphor The breeze is clear and the moon is bright. Path overshadowed by bamboo.
Explanation When you have made up your mind to be clean and upright, then happiness comes in multiples. Spend a leisurely life under the trees.

Poetic Life Reading

Destiny:

This life is like the autumn moon at night.  It is as cold as the frosty sky in the morning. There are not enough friends. You work doubly as hard. When you are enthusiastic you will encounter gossip. When there should be nothing, there is a storm. When there should be a good ending, there is something missing. When people should enjoy beauty there are regrets. Every day you have to run around. Everywhere you are looking for trouble.

Luck Verses

Foundation: 
Lonely pine tree and dying cedar know it is windy and rainy.
Change leaves and re-locate roots, then there’s prosperity.
When time goes like the flowing waters carrying the petals, spring is quiet.
Go over two layers of mountain before earning a good name to your credit.

Life Path:
The road is long, but to the waterfront, it doesn’t bring you.
To become successful get rid of the old and follow the new.
No matter how many rivers and mountains you go through,
Eventually must go home, in old age enjoy glory you pursued.

Marriage:
Pair of mandarin ducks are standing in pond, stand in two.
Under the bright moon lament over your solitude,
Apple blossoms and water plants quiet and subdued,
Fisherman spends time by clear water when fishing concludes.

Children:
Two flowers with petals fallen.
A branch with fruit in autumn.
Don’t sigh over green and yellow colors.
Maybe worries among the mulberry trees.

Harvest:
Luck is gone with Rooster and Dog, years that were lucky.
In old age there are some things to cause you to worry.
Upon the broken, ceramic roof tiles, the ice is melting.
On wide and endless sea, on a boat as a guest, you’re sailing.

 

Style: Flying Geese Parading Over the Sky
When the sky is high and the land is wide,
It is quite difficult to depend on your wings.
Two cranes flying high do not return in time.
When the water is flowing in the opposite direction to the clouds,
Flowers are carried away by the waves.
When time is not with you, all your efforts will be in vain.
When luck is with you, you do not need to sweat.
If you put out everything and just go along with people,
Then, rice in jade bowl and soup in golden dish will bring good tidings.

Commentary

Destiny

The “autumn moon at night” is most bright, which suggests you have good luck. However, there could be a lack of emotional support (“as cold as the frosty sky in the morning”). Managing your expectations is the key to making the most of life.

Luck Verses

Foundation

“Lonely pine tree and dying cedar know it is windy and rainy,” implies that you remain determined through difficult times. To prosper you need to make some major changes (“change leaves and re-locate roots”). Be patient when you are young (“spring is quiet”), because success will come after overcoming many challenges (“go over two layers of mountain”).

Life Path

Success is a long journey, which involves discarding old ideas and implementing new concepts. It is predicted that you will return to your hometown in your later years to enjoy the rewards from your efforts.

Marriage

A pleasant and tranquil married life is predicted (“apple blossoms and water plants quiet and subdued, fisherman spends time by clear water when fishing concludes”), yet you also spend time in your own company (“lament over your solitude”).

Children (‘Children’ can also mean subordinates or followers)

It is predicted that children will come later in life (“fruit in autumn”). There is no need to worry over what might have been because you have good luck (“mulberry trees”). For followers, good ones will join you later.

Harvest

You enjoy good luck during Rooster and Dog years. “Upon the broken, ceramic roof tiles, the ice is melting,” suggests that in your later years, there are matters at home that require your attention. Nevertheless, you can still enjoy yourself (“On wide and endless sea, on a boat as a guest, you’re sailing”).

Style

You want to go far and you have a long way to go (“sky is high and the land is wide, it is quite difficult to depend on your wings”). It might not all go according to plan (“Two cranes flying high do not return in time”). If you go with the flow, you can expect things to work out, eventually.
