Numeral 7    Jia Geng

Hexagram: Thunder on Lake

Cloud Cover Star

Hour Pillar Geng Zi – Metal Rat Geng Yin – Metal Tiger 
Metaphor Watching the moon above the clouds. Flowers blooming in the rain.
Explanation Enjoy the good times, bad times are left behind. Make the most of the good times.

Heavenly Pride Star

Hour Pillar Geng Chen – Metal Dragon Geng Wu - Metal Horse
Metaphor Building an earth mound. Stream flows into a pond then flows into another pond1. 
Explanation You can expect to be fairly well off. Avoid being narrow-minded when seeking success.

Heavenly Dynamic Star

Hour Pillar Geng Shen – Metal Monkey Geng Xu – Metal Dog
Metaphor Fisherman singing in snowy bay. Riding a horse on a narrow mountain path.
Explanation Even in circumstances of poverty, you can be very happy. In risky situations, be careful and cautious.

 

Destiny:

You can be rigid or flexible.  When it comes to weighing up the pros and cons, you do it most carefully. With much hard work in the early years, prosperity comes late. Problems are solved? Not quite. Have you reached your destination? Not quite.  Many times you are almost there.  Enthusiasm does not last long enough for you to run the whole course.  If some people do not acknowledge you, they are superficial and have judged you on your appearance. Relationships with your relatives are not that supportive or caring. It is suitable to have sons later in life.  Decision-making is not straightforward and you need to overcome blocks before you can decide. There seems to be a hundred things to think about and to worry about.   If you do business on your own, your good reputation exceeds the financial rewards. 

Luck Verses

Foundation: 
Whether you are female or male,
Romance will come without fail.
One hundred years of happiness is your fate,
A good reputation will come, it could be late.

Life Path:
During the year of the Dog, you are like a bird with new wings.
Year of Rooster and Ox, you will have new weapons, new things.
You can meet helpful people at the waterside,
This leads to success, working side by side.

Marriage:
You could well remain single,
Or if you marry you become a widow.
You have fame, so the opposite sex, you can attract.
Yet, relationships don’t seem to last.

Children:
Fruits don’t like it when it is rainy.
For little ones to fall ill it is so easy.
Those born under Ox or Goat, will be lucky,
Because they will grow up healthy.

Harvest:
Geng and Xin bring worries, these years seem the darkest.
Thereafter, in Ren and Gui years reap a good harvest.
The bright moon sets in autumn.
Enjoy a quiet life on retirement.

 

Style: Flower Meeting Late Spring
After getting married, you will be widowed.
Your character is clever and open.
Wild geese fly in formation.
When you are drunk, you dream of fame and fortune.
To have daughters is better than having sons.
Relationships outside marriage, like butterflies on flowers won’t last.
If a good friend comes from afar, open a bottle of wine and have a laugh.

 

Footnote 1
A stream flowing into one pond, then flowing into another, forms a figure of eight shape. This shape is referred to as ‘calabash water’, since it is the same shape as the calabash gourd.
