Numeral 21    Bing Jia

Hexagram:  Fire on Thunder

Heavenly Grace Star 

Hour Pillar Jia Zi – Wood Rat Jia Yin – Wood Tiger
Metaphor Fish swimming out of the net. Fireflies emitting light.
Explanation Someone comes to your rescue when you need help to escape from a risky situation. Even with a shallow foundation you can establish a home and career.

Heavenly Forest Star

Hour Pillar Jia Chen – Wood Dragon Jia Wu – Wood Horse
Metaphor Fallen petals washed away by water. Fish is very happy in deep waters.
Explanation Wandering about without settling down, searching for happiness everywhere. A relaxed life, fortune that comes later has no bounds.

Heavenly Pivot Star

Hour Pillar Jia Shen – Wood Monkey Jia Xu – Wood Dog
Metaphor Noisy cicadas in the willow tree. Ox sleeping on spring grass.
Explanation Hardworking throughout life, you need to manage your expectations. In your early years you live a leisurely life, the years ahead may prove to be more challenging.
   

Destiny:
You are born under a star that brings you big opportunities during times of change.  It is like the wind and the clouds coming together auspiciously, and you can have great accomplishment when the world around you is shifting.  The stars that rule your destiny also bring you harmonious relationships with your superiors.  You abhor unethical people and do not tolerate their appalling behavior.  When misfortune strikes you know how to handle the situation.  You go forwards rather than hide and you can stay in control.  Like a bird carefully building a bird’s nest, twig by twig, you care about your relatives throughout life.  Imperfect planning and a lack of motivation can hamper your efforts.  Constantly on the move, you can travel around a lot.  In this life, it is easy come, easy go for you, and there are a lot of changes. Your spouse tends to be vulnerable to the ups and downs of life.  It is most suitable for you to leave your hometown to establish a life outside for yourself.


Luck Verses

Foundation: 
You can enjoy good fortune, everything comes naturally.
Going to and fro, establishing yourself wherever you go,
Form good relationships with people who hold authority.
Even when in trouble, no need to stress, no need to worry.

Life Path:
Shoes on, ready to go, but you don’t know where to stand.
If you have the ambition to climb the ladder, it is at hand.
Travel afar and afterwards you will be highly respected.
Powerful people to give you a hand can be expected.

Marriage:
Mandarin ducks in the pond having fun at play.
After not much happiness, separate, go own way.
Lots of herons in the pond mean opportunities.
In your later years you will not be lonely.

Children:
The branches bear fruit, you’ll have children,
Grandchildren too, since your heart is kind.
New births to celebrate, red and purple to decorate,
On many occasions, thank heaven, many descendants.

Harvest:
In Ox and Horse years, on the path to success,
Like-minded friends with profitable plans come together.
Fishing boat is sturdy, about the winds, don’t worry.
Go out to fish in any direction, whatever the weather.

 

Style: Two Lotus Leaves Covering the Water
When you start out, you are barefoot, yet you know you can look to the future.
Like a lotus coming out of muddy pond, with no mud adhered to it, you are not smeared by mud.
You can adapt to changes in this world to make a good living.
Whether you use your physical or mental abilities, you have to work hard and career success comes late.
Wild geese flying in formation breaks up the clouds, they come together and disperse.
Mandarin ducks argue and separate.
If you want to know what is happening in your life,
Ask the cloud to lend you a ladder to reach the moon.
No need to ask, get on with your life.
