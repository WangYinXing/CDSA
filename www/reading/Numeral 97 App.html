Numeral 97    Gui Geng

Hexagram:  Mountain on Lake

Stars

Heavenly Fortune Star

Hour Pillar Geng Zi – Metal Rat Geng Yin – Metal Tiger
Metaphor When the moon is bright the stars become dimmer. The garden in late spring.
Explanation You have a good understanding. Therefore, there is no worry and no hesitation. Whatever you encounter is a pleasant environment. Career is prosperous.

Heavenly Glory Star

Hour Pillar Geng Chen – Metal Dragon Geng Wu - Metal Horse
Metaphor Watching the moon from the pavilion. Digging a well on top of a mountain.
Explanation There is someone to give you a helping hand and the result is excellent. Try your best to do business. Not afraid of pushing yourself.

Heavenly Pole Star

Hour Pillar Geng Shen – Metal Monkey Geng Xu – Metal Dog
Metaphor Boating against the current. Autumn willow dancing in the wind.
Explanation Diligent throughout your life. Because you lack determination, be careful of risky situations.

Poetic Life Reading

Destiny:

n this life you believe in being fair. Born with a stable character, you will not say black is white. You can distinguish between the white and the green. You have control over the ignoble people and you can get close to the honorable people. You admire and respect the noble people. You love to learn and want to be-friend the nice people. Because you are not that determined, people who are failures are jealous of you. With an ability to keep secrets inside your bosom, other people cannot guess what is on your mind.

When you have great and bright ambition,
Success depends on your determination.
Fortunately, there is a road leading to glory,
Borrow a friend’s status to start this journey.

Life Path:
Horse year will bring you good tidings.
In Goat and Dog years, ease in everything.
Fly over to a distant land, without knowing,
A boss from a past life you will be meeting1.

Marriage:

Children:
In both gardens, cinnamon blossoms.

Harvest:
Stay away from drinking and romance, stay well away.

Style: Pine Tree on the Rock
The pine tree is reaching the sky when it is old.
Stiff and strong, it is not afraid of the attack by snow and frost.
You shoulder the tons of heavy loads all by yourself.
Then you will be peaceful and can enjoy your heart’s content.
You will not tell lies or make yourself mysterious.
You can see far beyond but you do not boast about your ability.
Whether you will be poor or rich is all down to destiny.
Don’t admire the foolish people who are blessed with the full house of gold. 

 

Footnote 1
A boss from a past life will seek you out in the present life because you did a good job before.  The boss will be able to find you. You do not need to do any searching yourself.

Commentary

Destiny

White refers to what is right and black signifies what is wrong. In between black and white there are shades of gray, when things are not clearly right or wrong. To “not say black is white” means you will not say something is right, when you know it is wrong. “You can distinguish between the white and the green,” is like saying you can tell the difference between white and shades of gray.

Luck Verses

Foundation

An influential friend can help you in your career. You need to persist in pursuing your goals.

Life Path

The idea of “a boss from a past life” is tied to reincarnation. In a previous life, a boss was pleased with your work. In this lifetime, his reincarnated self looks for your reincarnated self to work together again.

Marriage

“Pair of mandarin ducks back to back in the rain, they’re standing,” suggests that you encounter problems in your relationship. It would be wise to be loyal to your spouse.

Children (‘Children’ can also mean subordinates or followers)

Children are predicted (“cinnamon blossoms”), and a son (“fruit”) is likely to arrive late in your life (“autumn”). You create good karma through charitable acts, therefore your children will keep you company when you are old.

Harvest

“Withered branch of tree blossoms again with greater beauty,” suggests that you are still fortunate in your later years.

Style

You have the stamina and ability to handle difficulties throughout life.
