Numeral 97    Gui Geng

Hexagram:  Mountain on Lake

Heavenly Fortune Star

Hour Pillar Geng Zi – Metal Rat Geng Yin – Metal Tiger
Metaphor When the moon is bright the stars become dimmer. The garden in late spring.
Explanation You have a good understanding. Therefore, there is no worry and no hesitation. Whatever you encounter is a pleasant environment. Career is prosperous.

Heavenly Glory Star

Hour Pillar Geng Chen – Metal Dragon Geng Wu - Metal Horse
Metaphor Watching the moon from the pavilion. Digging a well on top of a mountain.
Explanation There is someone to give you a helping hand and the result is excellent. Try your best to do business. Not afraid of pushing yourself.

Heavenly Pole Star

Hour Pillar Geng Shen – Metal Monkey Geng Xu – Metal Dog
Metaphor Boating against the current. Autumn willow dancing in the wind.
Explanation Diligent throughout your life. Because you lack determination, be careful of risky situations.

 

Destiny:
In this life you believe in being fair. Born with a stable character, you will not say black is white. You can distinguish between the white and the green. You have control over the ignoble people and you can get close to the honorable people. You admire and respect the noble people. You love to learn and want to be-friend the nice people. Because you are not that determined, people who are failures are jealous of you. With an ability to keep secrets inside your bosom, other people cannot guess what is on your mind. 

 

Luck Verses

Foundation:
Never mind the wins, losses and difficulties.
When you have great and bright ambition,
Success depends on your determination.
Fortunately, there is a road leading to glory,
Borrow a friend’s status to start this journey.

Life Path:
Horse year will bring you good tidings.
In Goat and Dog years, ease in everything.
Fly over to a distant land, without knowing,
A boss from a past life you will be meeting1.

Marriage:
Pair of mandarin ducks back to back in the rain, they’re standing.
In late years, to play with you in the sand dunes, friends are coming.
With a companion to hold your hand in front of the screen of your door,
Don’t try to pretend to be a great lover going all over the place, therefore.

Children:
In both gardens, cinnamon blossoms.
There will be a fruit, wait till autumn.
Having done many good deeds for charity,
In late years, you can avoid being lonely.

Harvest:
Stay away from drinking and romance, stay well away.
All because these things will lead to tears later one day.
Then look into the past, no particular danger, nothing risky.
Withered branch of tree blossoms again with greater beauty.

 

Style: Pine Tree on the Rock
The pine tree is reaching the sky when it is old.
Stiff and strong, it is not afraid of the attack by snow and frost.
You shoulder the tons of heavy loads all by yourself.
Then you will be peaceful and can enjoy your heart’s content.
You will not tell lies or make yourself mysterious.
You can see far beyond but you do not boast about your ability.
Whether you will be poor or rich is all down to destiny.
Don’t admire the foolish people who are blessed with the full house of gold. 

 

Footnote 1
A boss from a past life will seek you out in the present life because you did a good job before.  The boss will be able to find you. You do not need to do any searching yourself.
